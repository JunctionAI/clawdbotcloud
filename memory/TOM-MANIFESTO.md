# TOM HALL-TAYLOR — PERSONAL MANIFESTO

*Created: February 17, 2026*
*Context: Written after a period of intense self-reflection following burnout from ALTERNATE film project*

---

## CORE IDENTITY

**I am a high-intensity creative operator, not a scale-focused agency builder.**

I thrive at the intersection of:
- **Technology × Creativity × Innovation**
- C-suite strategic roles (not managing dozens of clients)
- Projects that feel like "world firsts"

I am NOT built for:
- Managing 10+ clients simultaneously
- Repetitive operational work
- Pure execution without creative ownership

---

## WHAT MAKES ME LOSE TRACK OF TIME

1. **Operational projects I'm genuinely into** — agent systems, AI automation, tools I find fascinating
2. **Creative projects at the forefront** — innovative, first-of-its-kind work (like AI filmmaking)
3. **Being part of something unique** — world firsts, things nobody else has done

**The Pattern:** When technology meets creativity meets innovation, I'm all in.

---

## WHAT DRAINS ME

- Too many clients (even 10 was too many)
- Repetition without novelty
- Pure service delivery without strategic ownership
- Operating without a collaborator/thought partner (Zach, etc.)

---

## THE IDEAL STRUCTURE

If money wasn't a factor, I would have:

| Category | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **One Operational Project** | High-paid, C-suite level involvement (DBH, PG) |
| **One Creative Project** | Innovative, passion-driven, reputation-building |
| **Consulting** | High-ticket, expertise-based, selective |
| **Travel** | Built into the lifestyle, not sacrificed for work |

**Maximum concurrent commitments:** 3 projects + consulting
**Anything beyond this = breakdown risk**

**Current anchor:** Deep Blue Health (~$52k/year base)

The base provides:
- Freedom to pursue passion projects without survival pressure
- Permission to say no to wrong-fit clients

## KNOWN VULNERABILITIES

### When I go too deep:
- Health deteriorates (sleep, nutrition, exercise disappear)
- Relationships suffer (family, friends go uncontacted)
- Substances creep in as coping mechanisms
- Self-talk turns negative and harsh
- I become emotionally volatile

### Warning signs to watch for:
- Skipping meals or eating poorly for multiple days
- Not exercising for a week+
- Not contacting family for extended periods
- Sleep under 6 hours consistently
- Using substances to cope with intensity
- Feeling like "everything depends on this one thing"

### Recovery protocol:
1. Eat something real
2. Move body (walk minimum, gym ideal)
3. One message to family (doesn't have to be deep)
4. Sleep prioritization
5. Talk to someone (Zach, flatmates, anyone)

---

## DECISION FILTER

When evaluating any new opportunity, ask:

1. **Does this align with my wiring?** (Creative × Tech × Innovation)
2. **Does this fit within 3 projects max?**
3. **Does this protect or threaten my base?**
4. **Will this drain me through repetition/scale?**
5. **Is there a collaborator/thought partner involved?**
6. **Can I maintain health while doing this?**

**If 4+ answers are negative → DECLINE or REDESIGN**
