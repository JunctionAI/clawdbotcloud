## Risk Mitigation

### Risk 1: "We look defensive"
**Mitigation:** Frame comparison as helpful, not combative  
**Example:** "SimpleClaw is great for X. We're built for Y. Here's how to choose."

### Risk 2: "We give SimpleClaw free publicity"
**Mitigation:** Only mention them in helpful context  
**Strategy:** Focus on buyer education, not competitor takedown

### Risk 3: "Pricing seems high compared to them"
**Mitigation:** Always tie price to ROI, not features  
**Message:** "We're not competing on price. We're competing on value delivered."

### Risk 4: "We can't prove ROI for all customers"
**Mitigation:** Offer 7-day free trial  
**Guarantee:** "If you don't save 10 hours in week 1, full refund—no questions."
