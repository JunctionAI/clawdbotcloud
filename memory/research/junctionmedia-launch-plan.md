## Executive Summary

JunctionMedia.ai is positioned to become New Zealand's first AI-native marketing agency, filling a critical market gap between expensive enterprise solutions and DIY tools. This plan provides a concrete roadmap from launch to first clients to sustainable growth.

**The Opportunity:**
- No true AI-first agencies in NZ market
- Gap at $3k-$10k/month price point for SMEs
- 70-85% gross margins possible (vs 20-40% traditional agencies)
- First-mover advantage in ANZ region

**The Goal:**
- **Day 30:** Website live, first client signed
- **Day 60:** 2-3 clients, $15k-$25k MRR
- **Day 90:** 3-5 clients, $30k-$50k MRR, sustainable operations

## Part 2: Website Build Checklist & Timeline

### Website Platform Decision

**Recommended:** Framer or Webflow
- Modern, fast, professional templates
- Built-in hosting and SSL
- Easy to update without developer
- Cost: ~$20-50/month

**Alternative:** Carrd (simplest, $19/year) for ultra-MVP

### Page Hierarchy

```
junctionmedia.ai/
├── Home (hero, value prop, CTA)
├── Services (packages, pricing hints)
├── How It Works (the process)
├── Case Studies (proof, when available)
├── About (Tom's story, why this)
├── Resources (lead magnet download)
├── Contact (form + Calendly embed)
└── Blog (for SEO, content marketing)
```

### Homepage Elements (Must-Haves)

| Section | Purpose | Copy Focus |
|---------|---------|------------|
| **Hero** | Immediate clarity | "Expert Strategy. AI Execution." + one-liner value prop |
| **Problem** | Resonate with pain | "Your marketing team is stretched thin. Content can't keep up with demand." |
| **Solution** | Your approach | "AI agents managed by marketing experts, not the other way around." |
| **How It Works** | Process clarity | 3-step visual: Discover → Deploy → Deliver |
| **Packages** | Anchor pricing | Three tiers with "starting at" pricing |
| **Social Proof** | Trust | Logos, testimonials, case study preview |
| **About** | Human connection | Brief founder story, photo |
| **CTA** | Action | "Book a Discovery Call" - Calendly embed |

## Part 5: Pricing Finalization Recommendations

### Recommended Pricing Structure

Based on research, competitive analysis, and margin targets:

#### Tier 1: STARTER — $3,000/month
**"AI Content Foundation"**

**Included:**
- 1 AI content agent (blog + social)
- 8 blog posts/month
- 30 social posts/month
- 10 hours strategic oversight
- Monthly performance report
- Bi-weekly check-in call

**Target Client:** Companies testing AI, limited budget, specific content needs
**Gross Margin Target:** 75%+

---

#### Tier 2: GROWTH — $5,000-$8,000/month
**"AI Marketing Engine"**

**Included:**
- 3 AI agents (Content + Social + Email/Ads)
- 12 blog posts/month
- 50 social posts/month
- 2-4 email campaigns/month
- Basic paid media management (up to $20k spend)
- 20 hours strategic oversight
- Weekly performance reports
- Weekly strategy call

**Target Client:** Growing companies needing comprehensive support
**Gross Margin Target:** 70%

---

#### Tier 3: SCALE — $10,000-$15,000/month
**"AI Marketing Department"**

**Included:**
- Full AI agent suite (5+ agents)
- Unlimited content within reason
- Paid media management (up to $50k spend)
- Email marketing automation
- Analytics and reporting
- 40 hours strategic oversight
- Dedicated account strategist
- Bi-weekly strategy sessions

**Target Client:** Established companies replacing agency or building out marketing
**Gross Margin Target:** 65%

---

### Entry Point: AI Readiness Assessment

**One-Time Project: $2,500-$5,000**
- 2-3 hour discovery session
- Current state analysis
- AI opportunity identification
- 90-day implementation roadmap
- Delivered in 1 week

**Purpose:** Low-risk entry point, converts to retainer 60%+ of time

---

### Pricing Philosophy

1. **Value-based, not hourly** — Price outcomes, not time
2. **Annual minimums** — 12-month contracts (negotiable to 6 for first clients)
3. **Prepay discounts** — 10% off for quarterly prepay, 15% for annual
4. **Deposit policy** — 50% upfront on projects, first month upfront on retainers
5. **Scope protection** — Clear deliverables, change order process for additions

### Pricing Decisions Tom Needs to Make

| Decision | Options | Recommendation |
|----------|---------|----------------|
| **Entry price point** | $2k vs $3k vs $5k | Start at $3k (can always go up) |
| **Show prices on website?** | Yes vs "Contact us" | Show "starting at" ranges |
| **Minimum contract term** | 3mo vs 6mo vs 12mo | 6 months initially, move to 12 |
| **Payment terms** | Monthly vs quarterly | Quarterly with discount |
| **Retainer vs project split** | 70/30 vs 80/20 vs 100% retainer | Target 80% retainer |

## Part 9: Risk Mitigation

### Top Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| **Slow sales** | Medium | High | Multiple channels, warm network first, lower entry tier |
| **Client churn** | Medium | Medium | Excellent delivery, QBRs, 6-mo minimums |
| **AI quality issues** | Low | High | Human QA on everything, clear SLAs |
| **Burnout (solo)** | High | High | Systemize early, contractor at $30k MRR |
| **Competition** | Low (now) | Medium (later) | First-mover, build relationships, niche down |
| **Cash flow gaps** | Medium | Medium | Deposits, prepay discounts, invoice financing |

### Contingency Plans

**If sales slower than expected:**
- Lower entry tier to $1,500/month
- Offer 3-month trials
- Increase outreach volume
- Consider strategic partnership/white-label

**If delivery overwhelmed:**
- Pause new sales temporarily
- Emergency contractor engagement
- Adjust package scope
- Communicate transparently with clients
