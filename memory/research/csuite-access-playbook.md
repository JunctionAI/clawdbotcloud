## 2. Executive Networking Organizations

### YPO (Young Presidents' Organization)

**Overview:** Premier global network for chief executives. 36,000+ members in 142 countries.

**NZ Chapters:**
- YPO Auckland
- YPO Wellington
- YPO New Zealand (national)

**Membership Requirements:**
- CEO/President/Chairman of company
- Under 45 at time of membership
- Revenue: $15M+ (sales/service/manufacturing) or $12M+ (agency)
- 50+ full-time employees OR 15+ with $2.5M+ annual compensation
- ~$10,000+ annual membership fees

**Why It Matters:**
- Direct peer access to CEOs
- Monthly forum meetings (confidential peer groups)
- Annual events like YPO EDGE
- Members include household names globally

**Strategy for Tom:**
- May not qualify directly unless JunctionMedia meets thresholds
- Alternative: Build relationships with YPO members through advisory work
- Speak at YPO chapter events
- YPO members often sit on boards of larger companies

### EO (Entrepreneurs' Organization)

**Overview:** Global network for entrepreneurs. 18,000+ members in 60+ countries.

**NZ Chapters:**
- EO Auckland
- EO Wellington
- Chapters operate locally with regional governance

**Membership Requirements:**
- Founder/Owner of business
- $1M+ annual revenue
- Controlling ownership stake

**Why It Matters:**
- Lower barrier than YPO
- Many members scale to larger companies
- Strong NZ presence
- Members become C-suite of larger orgs

**Strategy for Tom:**
- Highly accessible if JunctionMedia hits $1M revenue
- Join local chapter
- Build peer relationships that grow into enterprise clients
- EO members often recommend service providers to each other

### Institute of Directors (IoD) New Zealand

**Website:** iod.org.nz

**Overview:** NZ's leading governance organization. Directors, board members, and aspiring directors.

**Key Programs:**
- **Governance Essentials** - Entry-level board training
- **Company Directors' Course (CDC)** - Flagship program
- **Advanced Directors' Course** - For experienced directors

**Why It Matters:**
- Board members influence company decisions
- Directors sit across multiple companies
- One director relationship = access to multiple C-suites
- Networking events and branch activities

**Strategy for Tom:**
- Consider CDC certification for credibility
- Attend IoD events in Auckland/Wellington
- Target directors who sit on multiple boards
- Position as thought leader on AI/media to boards

## 5. LinkedIn Strategies for Executive Access

### Profile Optimization

**Headline Formula:**
`[Role] | Helping [Target Audience] with [Outcome] | [Credibility Marker]`

Example: "Founder @ JunctionMedia.ai | Helping Enterprise CMOs 10x Content Output | Worked with Perpetual Guardian, [Other Names]"

**About Section:**
- Lead with outcomes, not features
- Include social proof
- Clear call to action
- Keywords: AI, Enterprise, CMO, CEO, Marketing Transformation

### Connection Strategy

**Direct Outreach (Cold):**
- Connect with personalized note (under 300 chars)
- Reference specific content they've shared
- Don't pitch—build relationship first
- Follow-up cadence: Connect → Engage content → DM value → Meeting

**Warm Introduction Path:**
1. Identify mutual connections
2. Ask for introduction via message (not InMail)
3. Provide "forwardable" blurb

**Content Engagement:**
- Comment meaningfully on target executives' posts
- Share their content with your perspective
- Consistent visibility > sporadic outreach

### Content Strategy for Executive Attention

**What C-Suite Engages With:**
- Industry trends and data
- Peer case studies
- Contrarian viewpoints
- Personal leadership stories
- AI/technology implications

**Posting Cadence:**
- 3-5x per week
- Mix: 60% value content, 30% engagement, 10% personal

**Hashtag Strategy:**
`#CMO #CEO #AIinBusiness #MarketingLeadership #NZBusiness`

### LinkedIn Sales Navigator (Premium)

**Worth Investment For:**
- Advanced search filters (company size, seniority)
- InMail credits
- Lead recommendations
- CRM integration

**Search Filters to Use:**
- Seniority: CXO, VP, Director
- Function: Marketing, Sales, Operations
- Company Size: 1000+ employees
- Geography: New Zealand, Australia
- Industry: Target sectors
