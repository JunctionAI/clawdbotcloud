# NZ Startup Funding & Bootstrapping Options for AI Agency

*Research compiled: February 2026*

---

## Executive Summary

For a NZ-based AI/marketing agency with limited cash but infrastructure needs, the best path is likely:
1. **Immediate:** Bootstrap with client prepayments + equipment financing
2. **Short-term (0-6 months):** Revenue-based financing once you have consistent income
3. **Medium-term (6-18 months):** Angel investment if scaling aggressively
4. **Ongoing:** R&D Tax Incentive for any qualifying AI development work

---

## 1. Government Grants & Incentives

### ⚠️ Callaghan Innovation Update (IMPORTANT)
**Callaghan Innovation is being disestablished** (announced January 2025). The organization is winding down through 2026 with services transferring to Public Research Organisations (PROs) and MBIE.

- Previous grants (R&D grants, Getting Started grants) are **no longer accepting new applications**
- Some specialized services still operate (Biotechnologies, HealthTech Activator, Measurement Standards)
- Check [Disestablishment Updates](https://www.callaghaninnovation.govt.nz/about-us/disestablishment-updates/) for latest status

### R&D Tax Incentive (IRD) ✅ STILL ACTIVE
**This is now the primary government support for R&D in NZ**

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Tax Credit** | 15% of eligible R&D expenditure |
| **Minimum Spend** | $50,000/year in eligible R&D |
| **Maximum** | $120 million/year |
| **What Qualifies** | Activities seeking to resolve scientific or technological uncertainty |

**For an AI Agency - Potentially Qualifies:**
- Developing novel AI/ML models
- Creating new automation frameworks
- Building proprietary tools with R&D elements
- NOT: Applying existing AI tools to client problems (that's implementation, not R&D)

**Application Process:**
1. Check eligibility at [IRD R&D Tax Incentive](https://www.ird.govt.nz/research-and-development/tax-incentive)
2. Register for the incentive
3. Submit general approval application
4. Submit R&D supplementary return with tax filing

**Reality Check:** The $50k minimum means you need significant R&D spend. Better for year 2+ when you have revenue.

### NZTE (New Zealand Trade & Enterprise)
Focuses on **export-ready businesses**. They help 5,000+ NZ exporters annually.

- Primarily for businesses already exporting or export-ready
- Provides: export advice, in-market insights, global networks, growth funding solutions
- **Not ideal for early-stage service businesses** - better suited for product companies or established exporters
- Contact: [nzte.govt.nz](https://www.nzte.govt.nz)

### Regional Business Partner Network
- Provides access to business mentoring and capability building
- Can help connect to local funding sources
- Check your regional economic development agency for specific programs
- Often have small grants ($5k-$20k) for training, capability building

### Regional Development Agencies
Each region has economic development support - check your local council:
- **Auckland:** Tātaki Auckland Unlimited (aucklandunlimited.com)
- **Wellington:** Wellington Regional Economic Development Agency
- **Canterbury:** ChristchurchNZ
- **Others:** Check local council websites

## 2. Bootstrap Strategies (Recommended Starting Point)

### Client-Funded Growth Model
**The most realistic path for a service agency with limited capital**

#### Strategy A: Prepaid Retainers
- Offer 10-20% discount for 3-6 month upfront payment
- Example: $5,000/month retainer = $27,000 upfront (at 10% discount)
- Use this capital to fund infrastructure

**How to pitch it:**
> "We're offering early adopter pricing: pay quarterly upfront and save 15%. This helps us invest in the AI infrastructure that directly benefits your projects."

#### Strategy B: Project Deposits
- Require 50% upfront on all projects
- Standard practice in creative/tech industries
- Non-negotiable for new clients

#### Strategy C: Milestone-Based Billing
- Bill at each milestone, not just project completion
- Week 1: 30% (kickoff)
- Week 3: 30% (mid-project)
- Week 5: 40% (delivery)

### Productized Service Launch
Instead of custom work, create packaged offerings:

| Package | Price | What's Included |
|---------|-------|-----------------|
| AI Audit | $2,500 | Analysis of AI opportunities in their business |
| AI Marketing Setup | $5,000 | Content automation, chatbot, analytics |
| Monthly AI Ops | $3,000/mo | Ongoing AI management + optimization |

**Benefit:** Predictable revenue, easier to sell, can be partially automated.

### Lean Infrastructure Approach
Before seeking funding, minimize what you need:

| Need | Expensive Option | Bootstrap Option |
|------|-----------------|------------------|
| AI Compute | Dedicated servers | Pay-as-you-go cloud (AWS/Azure credits) |
| Software | Enterprise licenses | Startup programs (free tiers) |
| Office | Commercial lease | Home office / coworking |
| Staff | Employees | Contractors / revenue share |

**Startup Programs to Apply For:**
- AWS Activate: Up to $100k in credits
- Microsoft for Startups: Up to $150k Azure credits
- Google for Startups: Cloud credits + mentorship
- OpenAI Startup Program
- Anthropic partnerships

## 3. Equipment & Asset Financing

### Kiwibank Asset Finance ✅ RECOMMENDED
- Loans specifically for business assets/equipment
- Secured against the asset purchased
- Match lending term to asset life
- Fast online application via Xero/MYOB integration
- Decision in minutes for some applications
- Contact: [kiwibank.co.nz/business-banking/borrowing/asset-finance](https://www.kiwibank.co.nz/business-banking/borrowing/asset-finance/)

**Good for:** Computers, servers, equipment

### Prospa NZ (Non-Bank Lender)
- Business loans from $5k to $500k
- Same-day funding possible
- Online application in 10 minutes
- No property security required for smaller amounts
- Contact: [prospa.co.nz](https://www.prospa.co.nz)

**Products:**
| Product | Amount | Features |
|---------|--------|----------|
| Small Business Loan | $5k-$150k | Same day access |
| Business Loan Plus | $150k-$500k | Larger, longer term |
| Line of Credit | $2k-$500k | Draw as needed |

**Reality:** Higher interest rates than banks, but faster/easier approval.

### ScotPac (Invoice & Trade Finance)
NZ's largest non-bank SME lender with 30+ years experience.

| Product | Use Case |
|---------|----------|
| **Invoice Finance** | Unlock cash from unpaid invoices (up to $150M facilities) |
| **Boost Business Loan** | Up to $100k unsecured in 24 hours |
| **Trade Finance** | Manage import/export cash flow |

- No property security needed for invoice finance
- Contact: [scotpac.co.nz](https://www.scotpac.co.nz) | 09 309 0701

**Best for:** Once you have invoices out to clients, use invoice factoring to get immediate cash.

## 4. Angel Investors & VC in NZ

### Angel Association New Zealand (AANZ)
The peak body connecting startups with angel investors.

- Nationwide network of high net worth individuals, VCs, and startup investors
- Regular networking events and pitch nights
- Educational sessions for founders
- Searchable database of angel groups
- Contact: [angelassociation.co.nz](https://www.angelassociation.co.nz) | info@angelassociation.co.nz

**How to engage:**
1. Attend pitch nights (find via their events page)
2. Apply to present to angel groups
3. Network at their events

**Reality Check for AI Agency:**
- Angels typically want **equity** and **scalable businesses**
- Service agencies are harder to pitch than product companies
- Better if you're building proprietary AI tools/products alongside services

| Network | Focus | Typical Investment |

## 5. Revenue-Based Financing

Revenue-based financing (RBF) is ideal for agencies because:
- No equity dilution
- Payments scale with your revenue
- Faster than traditional loans
- Works for service businesses

### NZ Options

#### Prospa Line of Credit
- $2k-$500k available
- Draw down as needed
- Only pay interest on what you use
- [prospa.co.nz](https://www.prospa.co.nz)

#### ScotPac Invoice Finance
- Turn unpaid invoices into immediate cash
- Facility grows with your business
- No property security
- [scotpac.co.nz](https://www.scotpac.co.nz)

#### Kiwibank Revolving Credit
- Flexible business loan, no fixed repayment period
- Draw and repay as needed
- Fast online application
- [kiwibank.co.nz](https://www.kiwibank.co.nz/business-banking/borrowing/)

### International RBF Platforms (May Serve NZ)
- **Clearco** - E-commerce focus, may not suit agencies
- **Pipe** - For recurring revenue businesses
- **Capchase** - SaaS focused

## 7. Realistic Self-Funding Timeline

### Assumptions
- Starting with minimal capital
- AI/Marketing agency model
- Solo or small team initially
- Mix of project and retainer work

### Month-by-Month Projection

#### Months 1-3: Survival Mode
| Month | Revenue Target | Focus |
|-------|---------------|-------|
| 1 | $3,000-5,000 | Land 1-2 small projects |
| 2 | $5,000-8,000 | Convert to retainer(s) |
| 3 | $8,000-12,000 | Build recurring base |

**Cash Strategy:**
- 50% deposits on all projects
- Offer prepaid retainer discounts
- Use free cloud credits
- Work from home

#### Months 4-6: Foundation Building
| Month | Revenue Target | Focus |
|-------|---------------|-------|
| 4 | $12,000-15,000 | 2-3 retainer clients |
| 5 | $15,000-18,000 | Systematize delivery |
| 6 | $18,000-22,000 | First infrastructure investment |

**Cash Strategy:**
- Invoice factoring if needed
- Apply for equipment financing
- Consider first contractor

#### Months 7-12: Scale Phase
| Month | Revenue Target | Focus |
|-------|---------------|-------|
| 7-9 | $22,000-30,000/mo | Build team, systems |
| 10-12 | $30,000-50,000/mo | Product development, larger clients |

**At this point:**
- Self-funding from operations
- Line of credit for smoothing
- Consider R&D tax incentive
- Optionally approach angels for growth capital

### Key Milestones for Self-Sufficiency

| Milestone | Target | Why It Matters |
|-----------|--------|----------------|
| **$10k MRR** | Month 3-4 | Covers basic expenses |
| **$20k MRR** | Month 6-8 | Can hire first person |

## 10. Reality Check: What Investors Actually Want

For an AI agency seeking external funding, understand what investors look for:

### What Makes an Agency "Investable"
1. **Productized offering** - Not just custom services
2. **Recurring revenue** - High % on retainers
3. **Gross margins 60%+** - Showing efficiency
4. **Growth rate** - 20%+ month-over-month
5. **Defensibility** - Proprietary tools, unique methodology
6. **Team** - Track record, relevant experience

### Red Flags for Investors
- 100% project-based (no recurring)
- Founder-dependent (can't scale without you)
- No differentiation from other agencies
- Low margins
- No proprietary technology

### The Hybrid Model (Most Fundable)
Build a **productized service + SaaS** hybrid:
1. Agency services generate cash flow
2. Build tools internally for efficiency
3. Spin out tools as SaaS products
4. The SaaS component is what raises funding
