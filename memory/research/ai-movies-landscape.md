# AI-Generated Movies & Short Films Landscape
*Research compiled: February 2026*

## Executive Summary

**Key Finding: Tom and Zac are among the pioneers if they've created a 10+ minute fully AI-generated movie.** The landscape shows very few true "AI movies" exist—most content is short films (1-4 minutes) or feature films using AI as an enhancement tool rather than full generation.

## 1. Full AI Movies (10+ Minutes)

### Currently Released
Almost none exist as truly full-length AI-generated films. The field is nascent.

### Announced/Upcoming (2026)
| Film | Country | Status | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| **Kaur vs Kore** | India | Set for 2026 release | First Indian feature film using generative AI; features AI dual-role for Sunny Leone |
| **Chiranjeevi Hanuman – The Eternal** | India | Set for 2026 theatrical | Created by Vijay Subramaniam; heavily criticized by Bollywood for being "entirely AI" |

### First AI Series
- **Mahabharat: Ek Dharmayudh** (India) - Released October 2025 on JioHotstar
  - 100 episodes, first series with entirely AI-generated visuals and character animations
  - Mythological content

### Reality Check
Most "AI films" claiming firsts are:
- Under 10 minutes
- Use AI for assistance/enhancement, not full generation
- Short films from competitions (1-4 minutes typical)

**This means a 10+ minute fully AI-generated movie from Tom and Zac would be genuinely pioneering.**

## 2. Higgsfield.ai

### About the Platform
Higgsfield is an **AI video tool aggregator/platform** rather than a single model. Based on their current website:

**Key Products:**
- **Vibe Motion** - Turn prompts into motion-designed videos
- **Grok Imagine** - Generate cinematic videos with synchronized audio
- **Kling 2.6** - Cinematic videos with audio (up to 30 seconds with motion control)
- **AI Influencer Studio** - Create viral AI characters
- **Nano Banana Pro** - "Best 4K image model ever"
- Transitions, Face Swap, Recast, Click-to-Ad tools

**Integrations:**
- Sora 2 Community
- Wan 2.5 Community  
- MiniMax Hailuo 02
- Seedance Pro

### Launch Timeline
Unable to find exact launch date through web research. The platform appears to be a relatively recent aggregator that bundles multiple AI video generation tools together, including integrations with Sora 2 and other major models.

### Content Created with Higgsfield
The platform focuses on:
- Social media content (TikTok-style)
- UGC (User-Generated Content) style videos
- Commercial/brand videos
- AI influencer creation
- Rather than narrative filmmaking

## 5. "First AI Movie" Claims & Legitimacy

### Timeline of Key Releases

| Date | Model/Product | Company | Significance |
|------|--------------|---------|--------------|
| 2022 | CogVideo | Chinese researchers | First text-to-video model (9.4B parameters) |
| 2022 | Make-A-Video | Meta | Early text-to-video, partial |
| Feb 2023 | Gen-1 | Runway | First commercial video-to-video |
| Mar 2023 | Gen-2 | Runway | First commercial text-to-video publicly accessible |
| Jun 2024 | Dream Machine | Luma Labs | Consumer text-to-video |
| Jun 2024 | Kling AI | Kuaishou | Extended to international users |
| Dec 2024 | Sora | OpenAI | High-profile launch, ChatGPT Plus users |
| Sep 2025 | Sora 2 | OpenAI | Major upgrade with social features |
| May 2025 | Veo 3 | Google | Notable for audio generation |

### Why Most "First AI Movie" Claims Are Dubious

1. **Length Issues**: Most AI "films" are 1-10 minutes
2. **Hybrid Production**: Many use AI for enhancement, not full generation
3. **Definition Problems**: What counts as "AI-generated"?
4. **Short Film Dominance**: Festival winners are typically 1-4 minutes

### Current Legitimate "First" Claims
- **First AI-assisted Oscar Winner**: Everything Everywhere All at Once (used Runway for effects)
- **First AI Series**: Mahabharat (October 2025, India)
- **First Feature Films**: Indian productions claiming 2026 release

### The Gap in the Market
**No one has definitively claimed "first full-length AI-generated movie" (10+ minutes, entirely AI visuals) that has been publicly released and verified.**
