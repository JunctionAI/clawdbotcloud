## Objection Handling

### 🗣️ "SimpleClaw is faster to set up"

**Response:**  
"Absolutely! SimpleClaw optimizes for deployment speed—you're up in under a minute.

But here's what we optimize for: **business value delivered per week.**

SimpleClaw saves you 30 minutes on day one.  
Clawdbot saves you **10+ hours every single week** after that.

What matters more to your business—30 minutes today, or 500+ hours this year?"

---

### 🗣️ "SimpleClaw is cheaper"

**Response:**  
"Let me reframe the question: What's the ROI?

If SimpleClaw is $50/month and saves you 1 hour per week = ~$15/hour value  
If Clawdbot is $500/month and saves you 10 hours per week = $50/hour value

We're not more expensive—we're **more efficient.**

Plus, we include skills, security, monitoring, and support. SimpleClaw charges for hosting. We charge for **business outcomes.**"

---

### 🗣️ "Can't I just use OpenClaw for free?"

**Response:**  
"You absolutely can! OpenClaw is excellent open-source software.

Here's what you'd need to build yourself:
- ✅ Memory system (50+ hours dev time)
- ✅ Skills library (100+ hours curating)
- ✅ Mission Control (200+ hours architecture)
- ✅ Heartbeat monitoring (50+ hours)
- ✅ Security protocols (100+ hours + ongoing)
- ✅ Maintenance and updates (ongoing)

**Total: 500+ hours of dev work.**

At $100/hour (conservative), that's **$50,000 in labor.**

Our $500/month = $6,000/year. You break even in 6 weeks.

After that, you're saving 494 weeks of development time."

---

### 🗣️ "What if I start with SimpleClaw and upgrade later?"

**Response:**  
"That's a valid path! Here's what to consider:

**SimpleClaw is great for:**
- Testing if AI assistants work for you
- Personal/hobby projects
- Learning OpenClaw

**Switch to Clawdbot when:**
- You need business-critical reliability
- You want skills beyond basic chat
- You need multiple agents coordinating
- Security becomes important
- You value your time >$50/hour

Most of our customers started DIY, hit limits, then came to us. We can migrate you—but it's easier to start with the right foundation."

## When to Recommend SimpleClaw (Yes, Really)

Be honest—it builds trust.

**Recommend SimpleClaw when prospect:**
- Has <$1,000/month budget (not our customer yet)
- Wants to "test the waters" before committing
- Is technically savvy and likes tinkering
- Needs extremely simple use case (just chat)
- Doesn't need security/reliability

**Follow-up message:**  
"SimpleClaw is a great starting point! When you're ready for business-grade features (memory, multi-agent, security), we'd love to talk. Here's our contact: [link]"

**Why this works:**
1. Shows we're not desperate
2. Builds trust (honest advice)
3. They remember us when they outgrow SimpleClaw
4. We're not wasting time on unqualified leads
