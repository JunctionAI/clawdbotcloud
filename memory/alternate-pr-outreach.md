## 5. "MAKING OF" CONTENT ANGLES

### Core Narrative Angles

#### A. "Solo Creator, Full Feature" Story
- **Hook:** One person + AI tools = feature film
- **Content:** Day-in-the-life, workspace setup, iterative process
- **Platform:** YouTube documentary (15-30 min), Twitter thread

#### B. "Tool Stack" Technical Breakdown
- **Hook:** Exact tools, prompts, and workflows
- **Content:**
  - Video generation: (Runway, Pika, Sora if used)
  - Image generation: (Midjourney, DALL-E, Stable Diffusion)
  - Audio: (ElevenLabs, Suno, etc.)
  - Editing: (Premiere, DaVinci)
  - Upscaling: (Topaz, etc.)
- **Platform:** No Film School article, YouTube tutorial

#### C. "The Failures" Compilation
- **Hook:** AI doesn't always work - here's what went wrong
- **Content:** Blooper reel of AI failures, weird generations, uncanny valley moments
- **Platform:** TikTok/Reels (viral potential), YouTube short

#### D. "Prompt to Screen" Scene Breakdown
- **Hook:** From text prompt to final scene
- **Content:** Pick 3-5 key scenes, show exact prompts → raw output → editing → final
- **Platform:** Twitter thread, YouTube video

### Secondary Content Angles

#### E. "Cost Breakdown"
- **Hook:** Making a feature film for $X (subscription costs only)
- **Content:** Itemized costs, time investment, comparison to traditional budgets
- **Platform:** Blog post, Reddit AMA (r/filmmakers, r/Filmmaking)

#### F. "Acting Without Actors"
- **Hook:** Creating characters and performances with AI
- **Content:** Character design process, consistency challenges, emotional beats
- **Platform:** YouTube, film school lectures

#### G. "Soundtrack Creation"
- **Hook:** AI-generated score and sound design
- **Content:** Suno/other music AI workflow, Foley generation, mixing
- **Platform:** Music tech blogs, YouTube

#### H. "Worldbuilding in Prompts"
- **Hook:** Creating consistent sci-fi aesthetics through text
- **Content:** Art direction through prompts, maintaining visual coherence
- **Platform:** Concept art communities, ArtStation, Behance

### Content Calendar Suggestion

| Timing | Content | Platform |
|--------|---------|----------|
| T-6 weeks | Teaser + "Tool Stack" reveal | Twitter/YouTube |
| T-4 weeks | "Prompt to Screen" first scene | Twitter thread |
| T-3 weeks | "Failures" blooper reel | TikTok/Reels |
| T-2 weeks | Full "Making Of" documentary | YouTube |
| T-1 week | Cost breakdown + AMA | Reddit, blog |
| Release week | Press interviews, podcast appearances | Everywhere |
| T+1 week | Extended scene breakdowns | YouTube |
