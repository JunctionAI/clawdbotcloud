## DESIGN NOTES

**Overall Feel:**
- Minimal, confident, unhurried
- Lots of whitespace
- Premium but not pretentious
- Dark mode or light mode option
- Subtle AI/tech aesthetic without being corny

**Typography:**
- Clean sans-serif for body
- Bold/distinctive headline font
- Generous line height

**Color:**
- Monochrome base with one accent color
- Not too corporate, not too creative-agency
- Something that feels "tech-forward boutique"

**Photography/Imagery:**
- Mix of authentic photos (Tom working) and AI-generated visuals
- ALTERNATE section should be most visually rich
- Rest of site can be more minimal
