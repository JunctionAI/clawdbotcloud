## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**Key Finding:** Wealth management firms in NZ are underutilizing digital channels and content marketing, creating a massive opportunity for PG to dominate will-writing acquisition with a modern, trust-first digital strategy.

**Market Gap:** Most competitors rely on traditional awareness (billboards, radio) + referrals, with weak digital funnels and no systematic content engine. PG can win by building a digital-first acquisition system targeting 35-55 year olds (estate planning sweet spot).

## PART 2: MARKETING CHANNELS BREAKDOWN

### A. DIGITAL CHANNELS (High ROI, Scalable)

#### 1. **Search Engine Optimization (SEO) / Content Marketing**

**How it works:**
- Create educational content targeting high-intent search queries
- Examples: "how to write a will in NZ", "estate planning checklist", "do I need a trust?"
- Google ranks content, drives organic traffic
- Convert visitors via trust-building + clear CTA

**Wealth Management Examples:**
- Forsyth Barr: Investment research reports, market insights
- Vanguard (AU): Educational articles on retirement planning, estate strategies
- Fidelity (US): Life events planning hub (guides, tools, calculators)

**PG Opportunity:**
- **Target keywords:** "will lawyer Auckland", "estate planning NZ", "family trust setup"
- **Content types:** Blog articles, guides, checklists, FAQs, videos
- **Funnel:** Awareness (educational) → Consideration (case studies) → Conversion (book consultation)

**Expected Results:** 
- 6-12 months to rank
- 500-2,000 organic visitors/month (year 1)
- 2-5% conversion = 10-100 consultations/month
- Cost: $2-5k/month (content + SEO)

---

#### 2. **Paid Search (Google Ads)**

**How it works:**
- Bid on high-intent keywords ("will writing Auckland", "estate lawyer near me")
- Show ads to people actively searching for will/trust services
- Pay-per-click model ($3-15/click in NZ for legal services)

**Best Practices:**
- Target location-specific terms (Auckland, Wellington, Christchurch)
- Use ad extensions (phone, location, reviews)
- Landing pages optimized for conversion (testimonials, clear pricing, booking)

**Expected Results:**
- Immediate traffic (day 1)
- Cost per acquisition: $200-500/client (depending on LTV)
- Scalable (increase budget = increase leads)

**PG Opportunity:** Currently minimal Google Ads presence for "will" keywords. Low competition = cheaper CPCs.

---

#### 3. **Social Media Advertising (Facebook/Instagram)**

**How it works:**
- Target demographics (age 35-65, homeowners, parents, small business owners)
- Use interest/behavior targeting (e.g., "recently engaged", "new parents", "small business owner")
- Retarget website visitors who didn't convert

**Content Angles:**
- "Protect your family's future in 30 minutes" (will writing)
- "What happens if you die without a will?" (fear appeal, then solution)
- "We helped 10,000+ Kiwis secure their estates" (social proof)

**Expected Results:**
- Lower intent than Google (not actively searching)
- Higher volume, lower cost per click ($1-3)
- Good for awareness + retargeting

**Best for:** Building brand awareness with younger demographics (35-50) who aren't yet actively searching

---

#### 4. **YouTube / Video Marketing**

**How it works:**
- Create educational videos answering common estate planning questions
- Rank on YouTube search (2nd largest search engine globally)
- Build trust through face-to-camera expertise

**Video Ideas:**
- "5 things you MUST include in your NZ will"
- "Trust vs. Will: Which do you need?"
- "Estate planning for business owners"
- "What happens to your KiwiSaver when you die?"

**Benefit:** Video builds trust faster than text (see expert, hear voice, assess credibility)

**Expected Results:**
- Long-term asset (videos rank for years)
- 100-1,000 views/video (year 1)
- High trust conversion (video viewers convert 2-3x higher)

**PG Opportunity:** ZERO competitors doing this well in NZ. First-mover advantage.

---

#### 5. **Email Marketing / Lead Nurture**

**How it works:**
- Capture emails via lead magnets ("Free Estate Planning Checklist")
- Nurture sequence (5-10 emails over 4-6 weeks)
- Convert cold leads into consultations over time

**Email Sequence Example:**
1. Immediate: Deliver checklist
2. Day 2: "Here's why most wills fail" (educational)
3. Day 5: "Client story: How Jane protected her family" (social proof)
4. Day 10: "Ready to get started? Book your consultation" (CTA)
5. Day 20: "Still thinking about it?" (re-engagement)

**Expected Results:**
- 15-30% open rates
- 2-5% click-through to booking
- Converts leads who aren't ready immediately

---

### B. TRADITIONAL CHANNELS (Still Relevant for 50+ Demo)

#### 1. **Radio Advertising**

**Current Usage:** Public Trust uses radio heavily (e.g., NewstalkZB, RNZ)

**Effectiveness:**
- Reaches 50+ demographic (estate planning prime age)

- Call-to-action: "Call now for free consultation"

**Cost:** $500-2,000/week for local market campaigns

**PG Consideration:** Works for older demo, but expensive. Better for brand awareness than direct response.

**Current Usage:** Declining but still used by older wealth firms

**Effectiveness:**
- Reaches 60+ demographic
- Trusted medium for older generations
- Long lead times, hard to track ROI

**Cost:** $1,000-5,000/ad (depending on publication)

**PG Consideration:** Low priority unless targeting 65+ exclusively

---

#### 3. **Billboards / OOH (Out-of-Home)**

**Current Usage:** Some wealth firms use billboards near affluent suburbs

**Effectiveness:**
- Brand awareness (not direct response)
- High cost, low measurability
- Works for top-of-mind recall

**Cost:** $3,000-10,000/month per billboard

**PG Consideration:** Better for brand than acquisition. Digital is more cost-effective.

---

#### 4. **Seminars / Webinars**

**Current Usage:** Forsyth Barr runs regular webinars for clients

**Effectiveness:**
- High trust-building (face-to-face or live video)
- Educational positioning (thought leadership)
- Converts attendees at 10-30% (high intent)

**Format:**
- In-person: "Estate Planning 101" at community centers
- Webinar: "How to protect your assets in 2026" (Zoom)

**PG Opportunity:** Host monthly educational seminars (digital + physical) to generate leads

**Expected Results:**
- 20-50 attendees per event
- 10-30% book consultation after
- Low cost, high trust

**Tactic:** Position trusts as wealth protection vehicles, target small business owners and property investors
