## WHAT IS ALTERNATE?

ALTERNATE started as an AI film project. As of February 2026, it has pivoted to become a **full brand and business** operating across three pillars:

1. **Video Production & Films**
2. **Events & Community**
3. **Products & Merchandise**

The business entity is an eCom store that encompasses all three pillars.
