## Tone Issues (Should Fix)

### Overpromising Language
- "synthesizing strategy into domination" → "delivering measurable results"
- "absolutely dominating our category" → "significantly outperforming previous results"
- "Results Guaranteed" → "Performance-focused partnership" (less legally risky)
- "100% Satisfaction Guarantee" → Remove or define what this actually means

### Unsubstantiated Claims
- "500% Average ROI" - needs real data or remove
- "10x Productivity" - based on what measurement?
- "400+ hours of work per month" - how is this calculated?

**For immediate launch:**
