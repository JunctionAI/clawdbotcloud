## 2. YouTube/Vimeo Premiere Strategy

### Pre-Premiere Phase (4-6 weeks before)

#### YouTube Optimization
- **Title Format:** `[FULL FILM] "Title" - Made Entirely with AI | First AI-Generated Short Film`
- **Thumbnail:** High-contrast, cinematic frame with subtle "AI Generated" badge
- **Description:** Include:
  - "First/among the first fully AI-generated short films"
  - Tools used (Sora, Runway, Midjourney, ElevenLabs, etc.)
  - Behind-the-scenes links
  - Press/media contact info

#### Premiere Features
- **Schedule premiere** 1-2 weeks out to build anticipation
- **Enable premiere chat** for live discussion during first viewing
- **Prepare 2-minute countdown video** with behind-the-scenes teaser
- **Pin comment** with links to making-of content

#### SEO Keywords
```
AI generated film, AI movie, artificial intelligence film, Sora film, 
Runway Gen-3 movie, AI short film, generative AI cinema, first AI movie,
AI filmmaking, AI animation, text to video film
```

### Vimeo Strategy
- **Staff Pick submission** - contact Vimeo curation team directly
- **Vimeo On Demand** for potential monetization
- **Higher quality encoding** for industry/press viewing

### Multi-Platform Launch
| Platform | Purpose | Timing |

| Vimeo | Industry/press, quality showcase | Day 1 |

## 9. Key Messaging & Angles

### Primary Narrative
> "One of the first fully AI-generated short films, proving that AI isn't 
> replacing filmmakers—it's empowering individual creators to realize 
> cinematic visions that were previously impossible."

### Supporting Angles
1. **Democratization**: "A single creator made a film that would have required a full production team"
2. **Creative tool**: "AI as collaborator, not replacement"
3. **Future of indie film**: "The barriers to filmmaking are dissolving"
4. **Technical achievement**: "Pushing the boundaries of what AI tools can create"

### Talking Points
- AI generated X% of the visuals, but the creative vision was 100% human
- This took [X weeks/months] vs. traditional production which would require [Y]
- The tools included [list], demonstrating how multiple AI systems can work together
- This is just the beginning—imagine what's possible in 2-3 years
