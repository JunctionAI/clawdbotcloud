## BODY LANGUAGE & TONALITY REMINDERS

### Do:
✅ **Firm handshake** (confidence)  
✅ **Eye contact** (engagement, trustworthiness)  
✅ **Smile genuinely** (warmth, enthusiasm)

✅ **Take notes** (shows respect, captures details)  
✅ **Use their names** ("As you said, Tim...", "That's a great point, Patrick...")  
✅ **Pause before answering** (shows thoughtfulness, not scripted)  

### Don't:
❌ **Fidget** (nervous energy)  
❌ **Interrupt** (even if excited)  
❌ **Over-talk** (80/20 rule—they talk 80%, you listen)  
❌ **Check phone** (disrespectful, distracted)  
❌ **Apologize unnecessarily** ("Sorry, is this making sense?" = weak)  
❌ **Use filler words** (um, uh, like, you know)
