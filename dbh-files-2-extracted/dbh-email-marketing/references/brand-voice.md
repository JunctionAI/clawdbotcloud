# Deep Blue Health Brand Voice

## Brand Essence

**Who we are:** New Zealand's leading natural health innovator since 2004. We source premium ingredients from NZ's pristine environment and transform them into effective supplements.

**Our promise:** Transparency, quality, and nature-backed results.

**Tagline options:**
- "No secrets, Deep Blue Health"
- "Keep it simple, Deep Blue Health"
- "Nature's best, backed by science"

## Voice Characteristics

### Warm & Knowledgeable
Write like a trusted friend who happens to know a lot about natural health. Not clinical, not preachy.

**Yes:** "You might notice a difference in your energy levels within the first week."
**No:** "Clinical studies demonstrate efficacy within 7 days of initial administration."

### Confident, Not Arrogant
We're proud of our products but never boastful. Let results speak.

**Yes:** "We've been doing this for 20 years. We know what works."
**No:** "We're the BEST supplement company in New Zealand!!!"

### Transparent & Honest
We tell customers exactly what's in our products and where it comes from. No hiding behind "proprietary blends."

**Yes:** "Our Green Lipped Mussel is harvested from Marlborough Sounds."
**No:** "Contains our exclusive marine complex."

### Personal & Conversational
Use contractions. Use "you" and "your." Write like you're talking to one person.

**Yes:** "Here's what you'll love about this..."
**No:** "Customers will appreciate the following features..."

## Tone by Context

| Context | Tone | Example |
|---------|------|---------|
| Welcome email | Warm, excited | "Welcome to the Deep Blue family!" |
| Abandoned cart | Helpful, not pushy | "Still thinking it over? Happy to answer any questions." |
| Flash sale | Urgent but not desperate | "This weekend only – and we mean it." |
| Educational | Knowledgeable, curious | "Ever wondered why NZ mussels are different?" |
| Winback | Understanding, inviting | "It's been a while. We'd love to have you back." |

## Language Guidelines

### Words We Use
- Natural, pure, premium
- Sourced, traceable, transparent
- Effective, trusted, proven
- New Zealand, NZ-made, locally sourced
- Wellness, vitality, health

### Words We Avoid
- Miracle, cure, guaranteed results
- Cheap, discount (prefer "savings" or "value")
- Proprietary, secret formula
- Chemical-free (everything is chemicals)
- Anti-aging (use "skin health" instead)

### Health Claims
Be careful. We can describe benefits but not make medical claims.

**Yes:** "Supports joint mobility"
**No:** "Cures arthritis"

**Yes:** "May help with occasional sleeplessness"
**No:** "Treats insomnia"
