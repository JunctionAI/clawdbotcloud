## Common Leaks (What to Look For)

1. **Lifestyle creep**
   - Did spending increase as income increased?
   - Going out more? Better food? Nicer things?

2. **Hidden subscriptions**
   - Streaming services
   - Software you forgot about
   - Gym memberships not used

3. **Social spending**
   - Dates, drinks, events
   - Adds up FAST

4. **"Soft" fraud**
   - Buying rounds for friends
   - Lending money not returned
   - Impulse purchases

5. **No budget/tracking**
   - If you don't track, you don't control
   - Money just... disappears
