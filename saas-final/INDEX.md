## 📊 Decision Framework: SaaS vs Services

### You're Reading This Because...
The PRODUCT-DECISION document chose **Services-First (Fast Path)**, but this package provides the **complete SaaS system** as a reference or fallback.

### When to Use This SaaS System

**Use SaaS if:**
- ✅ You want to scale to 1,000+ customers without hiring
- ✅ You're okay with 60-90 day revenue ramp (slower than services)
- ✅ You have 4 weeks to build infrastructure
- ✅ You want fully hands-off operations

**Use Services-First if:**
- ✅ You want revenue in 7-21 days (faster)
- ✅ You want to validate pricing/features with real customers first
- ✅ You're okay with high-touch delivery (1-2 hours per customer)
- ✅ You'll transition to SaaS later (Month 3-6)

### Hybrid Approach (Recommended)
**Month 1-2:** Services (validate with 10-20 customers)  
**Month 3-4:** Build SaaS (using this package)  
**Month 5+:** Transition customers to self-service

**Best of both worlds:** Fast revenue + validated SaaS product.

## 🎓 Key Learnings (From Research)

### Pricing (research/pricing-strategy.md)
- **Value-based:** Price on time saved, not cost incurred
- **Tiers:** $199/$499/$999 (Starter/Pro/Enterprise)
- **Anchoring:** List high-to-low (makes $499 feel reasonable)
- **ROI:** 10-20x return for customers (defensible pricing)

### Zero-Touch (research/zero-touch-model.md)
- **Automated provisioning:** Stripe → Convex → Railway → Email (2 min)
- **OAuth integrations:** 2-click setup, no manual config
- **Intelligent defaults:** Works immediately, customize later
- **Self-service docs:** 80%+ questions answered without support

### Development (research/dev-roadmap.md)
- **Timeline:** 4 weeks (140 hours solo)
- **Stack:** React + Convex + Railway + Stripe
- **Testing:** End-to-end before launch
- **Deployment:** Vercel (frontend) + Railway (backend)
