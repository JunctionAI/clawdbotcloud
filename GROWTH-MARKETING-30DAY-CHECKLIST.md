## EMERGENCY PROTOCOLS

### If You're Behind on Customer Target
**Week 1 (Target: 5, Actual: 2-3):**
- Lower pricing to $500-750 for quick wins
- Offer "First month 50% off"
- Double outreach volume (10 per day → 20)
- Shift to higher-converting channels (warm network)

**Week 2 (Target: 15, Actual: 8-10):**
- Increase ad spend ($500 → $1,000)
- Launch referral program early (Week 2 vs Week 3)
- Reddit/HN seeding (value-first posts)
- Hire SDR to handle call volume

**Week 3 (Target: 40, Actual: 20-25):**
- Triple ad spend ($1,650 → $5,000)
- Hire 2 more SDRs
- Launch limited-time offer ("5 spots left this week")
- Accelerate ProductHunt/HN launches

**Week 4 (Target: 100, Actual: 50-60):**
- Adjust target to 50 customers ($50k MRR - still success)
- Focus on quality over quantity
- Plan Month 2 scaling strategy
- Celebrate progress, don't stress the miss

---

### If Ads Don't Convert
**Week 2 Test:**
- Pause ads with <1% CTR after 48 hours
- Test 5 ad variations (headlines, images, CTAs)
- Focus budget on best performer

**Week 3 Decision:**
- If ROAS <3:1, shift budget to organic
- Double down on Reddit/HN/content
- Use ads only for retargeting (warm audience)

**Week 4 Pivot:**
- Organic-only strategy (outreach + content + community)
- Can still hit 50+ customers without ads
- Save $5k ad budget for Month 2 optimization

---

### If Churn is High (>10%)
**Immediate Actions:**
- Call churning customers (exit interview)
- Identify common reasons (price, quality, fit)
- Fix root cause (over-deliver, better onboarding)

**Week 2 Fix:**
- Weekly check-ins with all clients (proactive support)
- ROI tracking (show time saved, calculate value)
- Referral incentives (keep engaged by referring)

**Week 3 Prevention:**
- Improve onboarding (clearer expectations, faster results)
- Build self-service resources (docs, tutorials, FAQs)
- Hire customer success manager (dedicated support)
