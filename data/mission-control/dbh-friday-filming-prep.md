## Product Talking Points (Vietnamese-Friendly Framing)

### 🩸 DEER BLOOD (Bổ Máu)
**Key Concept:** "Bổ máu" = blood nourishment — resonates deeply with Vietnamese health philosophy

**Simple Script:**
> "This is deer blood from New Zealand — natural blood nourishment, or 'bổ máu'. Unlike regular iron tablets that cause constipation, this is the same type of iron already in your blood. Your body recognizes it as food, not a chemical. Many customers see pink cheeks and more energy within 3-4 weeks."

**Key Points:**
- 2-3x better absorption than regular iron
- NO constipation (biggest complaint with iron supplements)
- From clean, green New Zealand deer farms
- Perfect for: menopause, heavy periods, fatigue, post-surgery

**Vietnamese Hook:** "Bổ máu tự nhiên — không táo bón" (Natural blood nourishment — no constipation)

---

### 🦴 LIQUID CALCIUM (Canxi Lỏng)
**Key Concept:** Prevention of osteoporosis — Vietnamese women very concerned about bone health

**Simple Script:**
> "After menopause, women lose bone very fast. Your body takes calcium FROM your bones if you don't have enough in your blood. This liquid calcium absorbs faster than tablets — very easy to take, very gentle. Combined with Vitamin D so your body can actually use it."

**Key Points:**
- Liquid = faster absorption, no tablet dissolving
- Contains Vitamin D (essential partner)
- Prevents osteoporosis — strong bones for active life
- Great for lactose intolerant (most Vietnamese are)

**Vietnamese Hook:** "Xương chắc khỏe, phòng ngừa loãng xương" (Strong bones, prevent osteoporosis)

---

### 🧠 GINKGO BILOBA (Bạch Quả)
**Key Concept:** Mental clarity and memory — staying sharp for family

**Simple Script:**
> "Ginkgo biloba comes from the oldest tree species on Earth — 270 million years old. It increases blood flow to your brain. More blood means more oxygen, clearer thinking, better memory. Takes about 4 weeks to feel the full effect, so be patient."

**Key Points:**
- Improves blood flow to brain
- Enhances short and long-term memory
- Helps with mental clarity and focus
- 50:1 concentration = very potent extract
- Takes 4 weeks for full effect

**Vietnamese Hook:** "Tăng cường trí nhớ, minh mẫn hơn" (Enhance memory, clearer mind)

## Suggested Video Formats

### Format 1: Personal Experience (3 videos, 60-90 sec each)
**Script structure for each product:**
1. **Hook (5 sec):** "Mệt mỏi mãi không hết?" (Always tired?)
2. **Problem (15 sec):** Common struggle (fatigue/weak bones/forgetfulness)
3. **Discovery (15 sec):** "I found this from New Zealand..."
4. **Solution (30 sec):** How product helped, simple explanation
5. **Proof (15 sec):** Show product, mention NZ origin, certifications
6. **CTA (10 sec):** Where to buy

### Format 2: Product Showcase (1 video, 2-3 min)
Show all 3 products as a "family health bundle" — perfect for Vietnamese gifting culture.

> "Today I want to show you 3 products I use for my family's health — all from New Zealand, all natural..."

### Format 3: Q&A Style
Interviewer asks questions, influencer answers naturally:
- "What did you notice first when using this?"
- "Would you recommend this to your mother/grandmother?"
- "How does this compare to products you've tried before?"

## Cultural Do's and Don'ts

### DO ✅
- Mention New Zealand origin prominently
- Frame as "family care" — gifting to parents
- Show certifications (laboratory tested, HACCP)
- Use respectful, educational tone
- Include Vietnamese subtitles in final video

### DON'T ❌
- Don't be overly salesy or aggressive
- Don't make medical claims (can't say "cures")
- Don't rush — Vietnamese audiences appreciate detail
- Don't skip testimonials — word of mouth is everything
