# Life Vision & Manifestation Skill

A deep self-authoring framework for clarifying your ideal life, visualizing it, and translating it into action.

## Purpose

Help users:
1. **Clarify** what they actually want (not what they think they should want)
2. **Visualize** their ideal future in concrete, vivid detail
3. **Identify gaps** between current trajectory and desired destination
4. **Translate** vision into actionable plans

## The Questioning Framework

### Phase 1: The Unfiltered Vision (Dream State)
*No constraints. Pure desire. What does the soul actually want?*

1. **Time Travel:** It's 5 years from now. Everything went right. Describe your average Tuesday in extreme detail — from waking up to going to sleep.

2. **The Feeling:** What emotions do you experience most days in this future? What feelings are notably absent?

3. **The Work:** What are you spending your time building/creating/doing? Not the job title — the actual activities that fill your hours.

4. **The People:** Who's in your life? Describe your relationships — romantic, friendships, professional, family.

5. **The Environment:** Where do you live? What does your space look like? What city/country? Urban/rural/coastal?

6. **The Body:** What does your physical self look like and feel like? Health, energy, appearance?

7. **The Money:** What's your financial reality? Not just income — how does money flow in your life? What's your relationship with it?

8. **The Legacy:** When you look back at what you've built/contributed, what matters most?

### Phase 2: The Anti-Vision (Fear State)
*What you're running FROM is as important as what you're running TO.*

1. **The Nightmare:** It's 5 years from now. Everything went wrong. You made all the default choices. What does that life look like?

2. **The Regret:** What would you regret most about that path?

3. **The Warning Signs:** What behaviors/patterns/decisions would lead you there?

### Phase 3: The Gap Analysis (Current Reality)
*Where are you actually standing right now?*

1. **Current State:** Describe your life today with the same detail as the vision.

2. **The Delta:** What's the biggest gap between now and your vision?

3. **The Drift:** If nothing changes, where are you headed? Is it closer to the vision or the nightmare?

4. **The Blockers:** What's actually stopping you? Be brutally honest.

### Phase 4: The Bridge (Strategy)
*Connecting vision to action.*

1. **The Levers:** What are the 3-5 things that, if changed, would most dramatically shift your trajectory toward the vision?

2. **The First Step:** What's the smallest action you could take THIS WEEK that moves you toward the vision?

3. **The Identity Shift:** Who do you need to BECOME to live that life? What beliefs/habits/patterns need to change?

4. **The Commitment:** What are you willing to sacrifice to get there?

## Visualization Prompts (for Higgsfield/Video Gen)

After completing the questions, generate prompts for visualizing key scenes:

**Scene Templates:**
- "A [age] year old [description] waking up in [location], [morning routine details], [emotional state]"
- "POV of [user] working on [specific work], in [environment], [energy/vibe]"
- "[User description] with [people] in [location], [activity], [atmosphere]"
- "Cinematic shot of [user] at [milestone moment], [achievement details]"

## Output

After questioning, produce:
1. **Vision Statement:** 1-paragraph crystallized vision
2. **Anti-Vision Statement:** 1-paragraph nightmare to avoid
3. **Gap Analysis:** Key deltas between now and vision
4. **Action Plan:** Concrete next steps
5. **Visualization Prompts:** Ready-to-use prompts for video generation
