## Smart Fake Marketing Strategy

### The Concept
Make AI-generated promotional content look like organic viral moments:
- Fake billboards in real locations
- Fake news articles/headlines
- Fake fan reactions
- Fake "spotted in the wild" moments
- Fake celebrity endorsements (careful with likeness)

### Execution Framework

**Layer 1: Fake World-Building**
- Generate "ALTERNATE billboard in Times Square"
- Create "movie poster spotted on subway"
- Make "premiere red carpet" with AI characters

**Layer 2: Fake Social Proof**
- AI influencer "reaction" videos to trailer
- Fake fan accounts posting about movie
- Generated "behind the scenes" that looks candid

**Layer 3: Fake Press**
- Generate headline images: "AI Film Breaks Records"
- Create magazine cover mockups
- Fake interview stills

### Making It Believable
1. **Imperfect = Real** — Add grain, slight blur, "phone quality"
2. **Context clues** — Show hands holding phone, partial crops
3. **Native captions** — Write like actual fans, not marketing
4. **Mixed media** — Combine AI with real footage/photos
