# ALTERNATE Visual Style Guide

## Camera Settings (Higgsfield Cinema Studio)

**Standard Setup:**
- Camera: Studio Digital S35
- Lens: Premium Modern Prime
- Focal Length: 35mm
- Aperture: f/4
- Aspect Ratio: 16:9
- Resolution: 2K

**Always include:** Film grain, cinematic quality, anamorphic lens flares where appropriate

---

## Visual Worlds

### 1. APARTMENT (Present Reality)
**The cozy anchor point**

**Color Palette:**
- Warm golden tones
- Amber and honey highlights
- Soft orange from string lights
- Deep browns and earth tones

**Lighting:**
- Practical string lights (fairy lights)
- Warm tungsten glow
- Soft shadows
- Golden hour feeling even at night

**Set Dressing:**
- Tapestries on walls
- Bookshelves
- Comfortable couches/beanbags
- Wooden floors
- Plants

**Mood:** Intimate, safe, grounded, friendship

**Prompt Keywords:** cozy apartment, string lights, warm golden tones, tapestry, bokeh lights, intimate atmosphere, tungsten warmth

---

### 2. CYBERPUNK TOKYO 2226
**The neon future**

**Color Palette:**
- Neon pink (primary)
- Cyan/teal (primary)
- Electric blue
- Deep purple shadows
- Hot magenta accents

**Lighting:**
- Neon signs everywhere
- Rain reflections on wet streets
- Holographic displays
- Police lights (red/blue)
- HUD/visor glow (orange for Tom)

**Environment:**
- Rain-slicked streets
- Towering buildings with holographic ads
- Flying vehicles/hovercrafts
- Dense urban canyons
- Japanese signage

**Mood:** Exhilarating, dangerous, freedom, escape

**Prompt Keywords:** neon-soaked Tokyo, cyberpunk 2226, rain-slicked streets, pink cyan neon, Blade Runner aesthetic, Akira inspired, holographic displays, flying vehicles, motion blur

---

### 3. MEDIEVAL BATTLEFIELD
**The epic past**

**Color Palette:**
- Desaturated/muted tones
- Cold grays and steels
- Deep reds (cloaks, banners)
- Muddy browns
- Overcast sky whites

**Lighting:**
- Overcast diffused light
- Smoke and dust particles
- Fire/torch light for night
- Cold natural daylight

**Environment:**
- Open battlefields
- Stone castles
- Military encampments
- Flags and banners (red prominent)
- Armies in formation

**Mood:** Epic, weighty, consequential, lonely despite crowds

**Prompt Keywords:** medieval battlefield, Ridley Scott inspired, Kingdom of Heaven quality, epic scale, armies clashing, cold desaturated palette, steel armor, red banners, overcast sky

---

### 4. SACRED GEOMETRY / PORTAL EFFECTS
**The transition**

**Color Palette:**
- Glowing orange (primary)
- Neon green (secondary)
- Golden light emanating
- Psychedelic rainbow hints

**Visual Elements:**
- Mandala patterns
- Concentric circles
- Sacred geometry shapes
- Radiating light
- Particle effects

**Usage:**
- Phone screen glow
- Reality transitions
- Character reveals
- Poster/promotional art

**Prompt Keywords:** sacred geometry mandala, glowing orange and green, psychedelic portal, concentric circles, radiating light, mystical glow

## Shot Types

### Establishing Shots
- Wide 16:9 compositions
- Show full environment
- Characters small in frame
- Set the world

### Character Portraits
- Medium close-up
- Signature elements visible (visor, red hair, etc.)
- Emotional lighting matching world
- Shallow depth of field

### Action Shots
- Motion blur for speed
- Dynamic angles
- Multiple elements in frame
- High energy composition

### Intimate Moments
- Tight framing
- Warm tones
- Soft focus backgrounds
- Character connection

### POV/HUD Shots
- First-person perspective
- Holographic overlays
- Speed indicators
- Visor frame visible
