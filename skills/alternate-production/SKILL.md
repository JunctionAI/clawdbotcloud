## Content Types

### Social Media Posts
- Match film's mysterious/philosophical tone
- Use taglines: "Reality is just a glitch" / "But the questions are real"
- Reference the choice: "GENERATE AGAIN? YES/NO"

### Scene Recreation
- Match exact visual DNA from film
- Use same camera settings (Studio Digital S35, 35mm, f/4)
- Maintain character consistency via handles

### New Promotional Shots
- Stay within established visual worlds
- Keep character appearances consistent
- Match cinematic quality (16:9, film grain, anamorphic flares)

## Key Story Beats

1. **Inciting Incident** — Three friends discover glowing phone with sacred geometry
2. **The Journey** — Experience alternate lives (cyberpunk, medieval, paradise)
3. **The Choice** — "CONTINUE STORY? YES/NO" — choose reality or keep generating
4. **Resolution** — Dawn in apartment, phone on floor, friends together
