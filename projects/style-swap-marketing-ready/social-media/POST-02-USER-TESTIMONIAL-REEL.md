## Production Specifications

### Video Settings
- **Resolution:** 1080x1920px (full HD vertical)
- **Frame Rate:** 30fps (or 60fps for smoother motion)
- **Format:** MP4 (H.264 codec)
- **File Size:** Under 100MB for Instagram, under 287MB for TikTok
- **Audio:** 128kbps AAC stereo

### Filming Requirements
- **Camera:** iPhone 13+ or equivalent (minimum 4K capability)
- **Lighting:** Ring light or natural window light (avoid harsh shadows)
- **Microphone:** Lavalier mic for clear audio (wireless preferred)
- **Background:** Clean, minimal, on-brand colors
- **Framing:** Vertical composition, subject centered, headroom 10-15%

### Editing Checklist
- ✅ Add captions/subtitles (85% of users watch without sound)
- ✅ Color grade for consistency
- ✅ Add smooth transitions (quick cuts, no long fades)
- ✅ Include B-roll for visual interest
- ✅ Keep text overlays on screen for 2+ seconds (readable)
- ✅ Export with high quality settings

## Casting: User Testimonial

### Ideal Candidate Profile
- **Age:** 25-35
- **Demographics:** Style-conscious, frequent online shopper
- **Personality:** Authentic, relatable, enthusiastic
- **Experience:** Real Style Swap user with measurable results
- **On-Camera:** Comfortable speaking, natural delivery

### Script (Talking Points - Allow Natural Delivery)
1. Open with attention-grabbing savings number
2. Describe problem (returns, time waste, money loss)
3. How they discovered Style Swap
4. Specific way they use it (before every purchase)
5. Measurable results (return rate, money saved, time saved)
6. Enthusiastic recommendation

### Compensation
- **Micro-influencer (< 10K followers):** $150-300 + free premium access
- **Mid-tier (10-50K followers):** $500-1000 + partnership deal
- **Real user (non-influencer):** $100-200 + 6 months premium free

## Audio Strategy

### Option 1: Trending Sound (Higher Viral Potential)
- Research current trending sounds on TikTok
- Must fit testimonial/transformation theme
- Replace voiceover with text + trending audio
- Add subtle background music

### Option 2: Original Voiceover (Higher Authenticity)
- User's natural voice (authentic feel)
- Professional audio cleanup
- Add subtle background music (royalty-free)
- Better for trust-building, lower viral ceiling

### Recommendation:
Create BOTH versions:
- Version A: Trending sound for TikTok
- Version B: Voiceover for Instagram Reels

## Posting Strategy

### Instagram Reels
- **Best Time:** Tuesday-Thursday, 7-9 PM
- **Share to:** Feed + Reels Tab + Stories
- **Pin:** First comment with FAQ or link
- **Hashtags:** Mix of high-volume (#fashion) + niche (#virtualtryon)

### TikTok
- **Best Time:** Monday-Friday, 6-9 PM
- **Series Tag:** "Real User Stories" (#StyleSwapStories)
- **Engagement Hook:** "What's your return rate? Comment below 👇"
- **Duet/Stitch:** Enable for user responses

## A/B Testing Plan

Test different elements:
