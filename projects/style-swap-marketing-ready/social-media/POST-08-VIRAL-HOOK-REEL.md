# Social Media Post #8: Viral Hook Reel (TikTok/Instagram Reels)

## Platform
TikTok Primary, Instagram Reels Secondary

## Format
1080x1920px (9:16 ratio)
Duration: 10-15 seconds

## Concept
"Addictive App" viral hook with rapid-fire try-ons

---

## Video Structure

### Hook (0-2 seconds)
**Visual:** Close-up of user's face, slightly shocked/amused expression
**Text Overlay:** "This app knows my body better than I do 💀"
**Voiceover/Audio:** "Wait what?!"

### Build-Up (2-8 seconds)
**Visual:** Rapid-fire montage of AI try-on results
- 10-15 different outfits flashing quickly (0.5 sec each)
- Each perfectly fitted to user's body
- Mix of styles (casual, formal, edgy, romantic)
- User's face reactions between outfits (amazement, delight, laughter)

**Text Overlays (appearing/disappearing fast):**
- "Outfit 1... ✓"
- "Outfit 5... ✓"
- "Outfit 10... ✓"
- "Outfit 20... 😳"
- "I CAN'T STOP"

**Audio:** Trending fast-paced sound (upbeat, energetic)

### Peak (8-12 seconds)
**Visual:**
- User scrolling frantically through more outfits
- Split screen: Phone showing app + user's excited face
- Counter ticking up rapidly

**Text:** "47 outfits tried and it's only noon"
**Audio:** Music reaches climax

### Punchline (12-15 seconds)
**Visual:** User looks at camera, holding phone
**Text:** "Help. I can't stop. Send help. (jk this is amazing)"
**Voiceover:** "Actually don't send help, I'm having the time of my life"

**End Screen:**
- Style Swap logo
- "Try at your own risk 😂"
- "Link in bio"

## Production Requirements

### Filming
- **Camera:** High-quality smartphone (4K preferred)
- **Lighting:** Bright, even (ring light or natural window)
- **Takes:** Film 20-30 different outfit reactions (use best 10-15)
- **Expressions:** Genuine surprise, delight, excitement
- **Energy:** High energy throughout (infectious enthusiasm)

### Editing
- **Software:** CapCut, Adobe Premiere Rush, Final Cut Pro
- **Pacing:** 0.5 seconds per outfit reveal (very fast)
- **Transitions:** Quick cuts, zoom ins, snap effects
- **Sound Design:**
  - "Whoosh" sounds for outfit changes
  - "Ding" sounds for each try-on complete
  - Background music synced to beat
- **Effects:**
  - Speed ramping (slow-mo on reactions, fast-forward on scrolling)
  - Zoom punches for emphasis
  - Shake effect on "can't stop" moment

### Text Animation
- Bouncy, energetic fonts
- Quick fade in/out
- Count-up animation for outfit numbers
- Emoji integration (seamless, not overdone)

## Trending Sound Strategy

### Primary Option: Original Voiceover
- User's authentic reaction (genuine, relatable)
- Add trending background music
- Better for brand building

### Secondary Option: Trending Sound
- Use current viral TikTok sound (check weekly)
- Replace voiceover with text
- Higher viral potential
- **Update sound every 7-14 days based on trends**

## Viral Mechanics

### Why This Works
1. **Relatable Hook:** "This app knows my body better than I do" (curiosity)
2. **Fast Pacing:** Keeps attention, rewatchable
3. **Humor:** Self-aware humor (can't stop)
4. **FOMO:** Others want to try too
5. **Aspirational:** 47 outfits = endless possibilities

### Engagement Drivers
- **Question in Caption:** "How many outfits would YOU try?"
- **Challenge Element:** "Beat my record"
- **Relatability:** Everyone loves shopping
- **Shareability:** Send to friends who love fashion

## A/B Testing Variations

### Version A: Counter Focus
Prominent on-screen counter showing outfit count increasing

### Version B: Split Screen
User reaction on one side, outfit try-on on other (simultaneous)

### Version C: Time-Lapse
Show actual time passing (clock in corner) vs. outfits tried

### Version D: Friend/Partner React
