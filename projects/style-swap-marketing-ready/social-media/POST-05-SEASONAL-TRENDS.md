## Production Notes

### Photography Requirements
- **Models:** 3-5 diverse body types (rotate across 9 panels)
- **Lighting:** Consistent across all panels (studio or bright natural)
- **Backgrounds:** Uniform white or complementary pastels
- **Poses:** Dynamic but not overly styled (relatable)

### Design Consistency
- **Border:** 20px white border around each panel (creates grid effect when posted)
- **Typography:** Montserrat Bold for trend labels
- **Label Background:** Semi-transparent white box (80% opacity)
- **Watermark:** Small Style Swap logo (15% opacity, white)

### Color Grading
- Vibrant but natural (avoid over-saturation)
- Consistent warmth across all panels
- Highlight seasonal colors (pastels for spring, brights for summer)
