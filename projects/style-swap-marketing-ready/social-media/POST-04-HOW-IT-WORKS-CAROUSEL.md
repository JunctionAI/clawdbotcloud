## Design System

- **Primary Purple:** #6B46C1
- **Primary Pink:** #EC4899
- **Success Green:** #10B981
- **Background White:** #FFFFFF
- **Text Dark:** #1F2937
- **Text Medium:** #6B7280
- **Text Light:** #9CA3AF

### Typography Hierarchy
- **Hero Text:** Montserrat Black (84-120pt)
- **Main Headlines:** Montserrat Bold (58-72pt)
- **Subheadlines:** Montserrat SemiBold (48-64pt)
- **Body Text:** Montserrat Regular (40-52pt)
- **Small Text:** Montserrat Light (32-44pt)

### Spacing & Layout
- **Margin:** 80px all sides
- **Text Padding:** 40px between elements
- **Image Padding:** 60px from edges
- **Consistent grid:** 12-column layout

## Production Checklist

### Design Assets Required
- ✅ Phone mockup (iPhone 14 Pro, modern design)
- ✅ Stock photos (diverse, relatable models)
- ✅ Brand logo (vector, high-res)
- ✅ App screenshots (updated, actual UI)
- ✅ Icon set (custom or from library)
- ✅ Return package photos (authentic, not stock)

### Design Software
- **Preferred:** Figma (collaborative, template-ready)
- **Alternative:** Adobe Photoshop, Canva Pro
- **Export:** PNG (high quality, RGB color mode)
- **File Naming:** `slide-[number]-[description].png`

### Brand Consistency
- Use exact brand colors (no variations)
- Consistent phone mockup style across all slides
- Same text positioning structure (predictable layout)
- Unified icon style (outlined vs filled - pick one)

## Paid Promotion Strategy

### Instagram Feed Ads
- **Objective:** Traffic (link clicks to app landing page)
- **Budget:** $100-200 per carousel
- **Targeting:**
  - **Age:** 22-45
  - **Gender:** Women (primary), All (secondary test)
  - **Interests:** Online shopping, fashion, sustainable living, ASOS, Zara, H&M

## Accessibility Considerations

### Alt Text (Required for Each Slide)
- Slide 1: "Woman surrounded by return packages looking frustrated"
- Slide 2: "Style Swap logo with sparkles on white background"
- Slide 3: "Phone screen showing photo upload interface"
- Slide 4: "Phone displaying outfit catalog with brand logos"
- Slide 5: "Phone with AI processing visualization and before-after preview"
- Slide 6: "Happy woman holding phone next to matching outfit"
- Slide 7: "Style Swap app icon with download buttons and free trial offer"

### Text Contrast
- Ensure 4.5:1 contrast ratio minimum (WCAG AA standard)
- Use text shadows or background overlays for readability
- Avoid text over busy backgrounds

### Font Sizing
- Minimum 32pt for body text (readable on mobile)
- High contrast for better visibility
- Avoid all-caps for long sentences (harder to read)
