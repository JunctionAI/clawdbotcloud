## Challenge Mechanics

### Weekly Outfit Feature
- **Monday:** Announce "Outfit of the Week" (trending/seasonal piece)
- **Tuesday-Sunday:** Users try on the outfit and tag #StyleSwapChallenge
- **Next Monday:** Feature best submissions, pick winner

### Prizes
- **Winner:** $100 shopping credit + Featured on main account
- **Runner-ups (3):** 6 months premium free
- **All Participants:** Entry into monthly grand prize ($500 gift card)

### Engagement Boosters
1. Create outfit shortcut link (easy for users to find exact outfit)
2. Pin comment with direct outfit link
3. Repost best submissions to Stories (with credit)
4. Create highlight reel of all submissions monthly

## Trending Outfit Selection Strategy

### How to Find Viral Outfits:
1. **TikTok Trends:** Search "outfit of the day" + sort by views
2. **Pinterest:** Look for pins with 10K+ saves
3. **Instagram Explore:** Check fashion hashtags for high engagement
4. **Runway Trends:** Translate high fashion to affordable alternatives
5. **Celebrity Sightings:** What are influencers wearing this week?

### Outfit Criteria:
- Available in multiple price points ($-$$$)
- Clear visual appeal (photographs well)
- Versatile (works for multiple body types)
- Trendy but not fleeting (2-4 week relevance window)

### Featured Outfit Types (Rotate Weekly):
- **Week 1:** Dress (cocktail, casual, formal)
- **Week 2:** Two-piece set (matching sets, co-ords)
- **Week 3:** Statement piece (jacket, coat, blazer)
- **Week 4:** Full outfit (head-to-toe look)

## Production Specs

### Filming Setup
- **Camera:** iPhone 14+ in 4K 60fps
- **Stabilization:** Gimbal or tripod (avoid shaky footage)
- **Lighting:** Bright, even (ring light recommended)
- **Location:** Clean background, minimal distractions

### Editing Requirements
- **Software:** CapCut, Adobe Premiere Rush, or Final Cut Pro
- **Pace:** Fast cuts (0.5-1 second per clip maximum)
- **Transitions:** Quick cuts, snap zooms, whip pans
- **Sound Design:**
  - Sync cuts to beat drops
  - Add "whoosh" sounds for transitions
  - Include UI click sounds for app interactions
- **Color Grading:** Bright, vibrant, slightly saturated (Instagram-friendly)

### Text Overlay Guidelines
- Keep text on screen 1.5-2 seconds minimum
- Use high contrast (white text on dark, or outlined text)
- Avoid center-bottom (caption area)
- Animate in/out (fade, slide, bounce)

## Posting Strategy

### Timing
- **Instagram:** Thursday-Saturday (weekend shopping mindset)
- **TikTok:** Wednesday-Friday (mid-week engagement peak)
- **Time:** 7-9 PM local time

### Cross-Promotion
- Post to IG Reels first (better for link traffic)
- Post to TikTok 2-4 hours later (fresh content, not duplicate)
- Share Reel to IG Stories with poll: "Should I buy it? Yes/No"
- Share TikTok to Twitter with outfit link
- Pin TikTok to profile for 7 days (featured challenge)

### Series Branding
- Consistent intro animation
- Recognizable format (users know what to expect)
- Specific hashtag: #StyleSwapChallenge
- Weekly announcement post (building anticipation)

## Paid Promotion Strategy

### Organic First, Then Boost
1. Post organically
2. Monitor performance for 24 hours
3. If engagement rate >5%, boost with $50-150 budget
4. Target lookalike audience (app users)

### TikTok Ads
- **Format:** Spark Ads (boost original post)
- **Budget:** $100-200 per challenge
- **Targeting:**
