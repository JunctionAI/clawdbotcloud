# Social Media Post #6: Before/After Shopping Comparison (Instagram Reel)

## Platform
Instagram Reels / TikTok

## Format
1080x1920px (9:16 ratio)
Duration: 15 seconds

## Concept
Split-screen comparison showing "Old Way vs. New Way" of online shopping

## Production Specs

### Filming
- **Single Actor:** Can play both sides (different outfit/setting for each)
- **Locations:** Two contrasting setups (messy/cluttered vs. clean/organized)
- **Props:** Delivery boxes, return labels, calculator, phone, packages
- **Expressions:** Exaggerated emotions (frustration vs. joy)

### Editing
- **Split Screen:** Perfect vertical divide throughout
- **Color Grading:** Slight red tint (left), slight green tint (right)
- **Text:** Large, bold, easy to read
- **Pacing:** Fast cuts (match actions on both sides simultaneously)
- **Sound Design:** Contrasting sounds (sighs vs. happy sounds)

---

## Variations

### Version A: Time-Lapse Style
Show entire process sped up (old way takes forever, new way instant)

### Version B: Calculator Focus
Emphasize money saved (cost breakdown overlays)

### Version C: Environmental Angle
Show packaging waste (old way) vs. zero waste (new way)
