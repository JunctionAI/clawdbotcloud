# Social Media Post #10: "POV: You Discovered..." Reel (TikTok/Instagram Reels)

## Platform
TikTok Primary, Instagram Reels Secondary

## Format
1080x1920px (9:16 ratio)
Duration: 12-15 seconds

## Concept
"POV: Discovered the app saving everyone hundreds" - Relatable storytelling format

---

## Video Structure

### Hook (0-2 seconds)
**Visual:** User looking at camera with knowing smile
**Text Overlay:** "POV: You discovered the app that's saving everyone HUNDREDS 💸"
**Audio:** Trending relatable/emotional sound (update weekly)

---

### Act 1: Before (2-5 seconds)
**Visual:** Fast montage showing the "old way"
- Pile of Amazon/delivery boxes
- User frustrated, surrounded by clothes
- Printing return labels
- Calendar showing "waiting for refund"

**Text Overlay:** "Me before Style Swap 📦😭"
**Mood:** Chaotic, stressful, relatable frustration

---

### Act 2: Discovery (5-8 seconds)
**Visual:**
- User scrolling on phone (seeing Style Swap ad/post)
- Eyes widen with interest
- Downloads app
- Uploads photo for first time

**Text Overlay:** "Wait... I can try clothes on BEFORE buying?"
**Mood:** Curiosity, hope, excitement building

---

### Act 3: Transformation (8-12 seconds)
**Visual:** Fast montage of using app
- Trying on outfit 1, 2, 3... (rapid fire)
- Reactions getting more excited with each try-on
- Confidence building
- Deleting items from cart (things that don't work)
- Adding one perfect item (that does work)

**Text Overlays:**
- "47 outfits tried ✓"
- "$0 wasted on returns ✓"

**Mood:** Empowering, confident, smart

---

### Act 4: Results (12-15 seconds)
**Visual:**
- Split screen: Old pile of returns vs. One perfect outfit
- User happy, confident, wearing the outfit
- Shows phone with $0 returns, $347 saved

**Text Overlays:**
- "Before: Return rate 80% 😭"
- "After: Return rate 0% 💅"

**Final Screen:**
- Style Swap logo
- "It's giving: IDENTICAL"
- "Link in bio"

## Production Specifications

### Filming
**Equipment:**
- iPhone 13+ (or equivalent 4K camera)
- Tripod + optional gimbal (smooth panning shots)
- Ring light or natural window light

**Locations:**
- Home setting (relatable, authentic)
- 2-3 areas: Living room, bedroom, front door (for package delivery scene)

**Props:**
- Delivery boxes (Amazon, ASOS, etc.)
- Return labels (printed)
- Calendar or planner
- Phone (for app demo)
- Clothes (various items for "before" chaos scene)

**Wardrobe:**
- Casual, relatable outfit for "before" scenes
- Elevated, confident outfit for "after" scenes
- Final scene: Perfect outfit that was "ordered via app"

---

### Editing

**Software:** CapCut, Adobe Premiere Rush, Final Cut Pro

**Pacing:**
- Act 1 (Before): Fast, chaotic cuts (0.5-1 sec per clip)
- Act 2 (Discovery): Medium pace (1-2 sec per clip)
- Act 3 (Using app): Fast, exciting (0.5 sec per try-on)
- Act 4 (Results): Slower, triumphant (2-3 sec holds)

**Transitions:**
- Quick cuts for Before section
- Smooth transitions for Using app section
- Split screen for comparison

**Text Animation:**
- Bounce in for hook text
- Fade in/out for emotional beats
- Count-up animation for savings number
- Side-by-side comparison text (Before/After)

**Color Grading:**
- Before scenes: Slightly desaturated, cooler tones (convey frustration)
- After scenes: Vibrant, warm tones (convey happiness)

---

## Sound Design

### Audio Layers
1. **Music Track:** Trending TikTok sound (emotional/relatable vibe)
2. **Sound Effects:**
   - Sigh/frustration sounds (Before scenes)
   - "Ding" or "whoosh" (App interaction)
   - Uplifting sound (Transformation)
   - Cash register "cha-ching" (Savings reveal)
3. **Optional Voiceover:** User narrating key moments

---

## Trending Sound Strategy

### Current Trending Options (Update Weekly)
Research TikTok "Trending Sounds" page for:
- **Relatable sounds:** Storytelling, "before and after" themes
- **Emotional sounds:** Journey, transformation themes
- **Comedy sounds:** Lighthearted, self-deprecating humor

### Sound Selection Criteria
- Has 500K+ uses (proven viral potential)
- Matches pacing of video (tempo fits editing)
- Emotion aligns with message (relatable struggle → triumph)
- Not overly saturated (still room for visibility)
