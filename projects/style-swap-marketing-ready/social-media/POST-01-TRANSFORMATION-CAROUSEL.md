# Social Media Post #1: Transformation Carousel (Instagram/TikTok)

## Platform
Instagram Feed (Carousel Post)

## Format
1080x1350px (4:5 ratio) - 4 slides

## Visual Specifications

### Slide 1: The Hook
- **Background:** Soft gradient (blush pink to lavender)
- **Image:** User's casual selfie (authentic, relatable lighting)
- **Text Overlay:** "Watch this..." 
  - Font: Bold, modern sans-serif (Montserrat Black)
  - Size: 72pt
  - Color: White with subtle drop shadow
  - Position: Top center

### Slide 2: AI Result Reveal
- **Background:** Clean white
- **Image:** Same user in stunning designer outfit (AI-generated result)
- **Text Overlay:** "60 seconds later..."
  - Font: Montserrat Black
  - Size: 64pt
  - Color: Deep purple (#6B46C1)
  - Position: Bottom right
- **Badge:** "AI Try-On" small label in corner

### Slide 3: Side-by-Side Comparison
- **Layout:** Split screen (vertical divide)
- **Left:** Original photo | **Right:** AI try-on
- **Text Overlay:** "Same person. Different outfit. Zero fitting rooms."
  - Font: Montserrat SemiBold
  - Size: 48pt
  - Color: White
  - Position: Center bottom
- **Background bar:** Semi-transparent dark overlay for text legibility

### Slide 4: Call to Action
- **Background:** Brand gradient (purple to pink)
- **Logo:** Style Swap logo (center)
- **App Screenshot:** iPhone mockup showing interface (subtle tilt)
- **Text Overlay:** "This is how we did it."
  - Font: Montserrat Bold
  - Size: 56pt
  - Color: White
- **CTA Button:** "Try FREE →" 
  - Rounded rectangle, white background
  - Purple text (#6B46C1)
  - Position: Bottom center

---

## Posting Schedule
- **Best Days:** Monday, Wednesday, Friday
- **Best Times:** 6-9 PM local time
- **Frequency:** 2x per week

---

## Engagement Strategy
1. **First Hour:** Respond to all comments within 60 minutes
2. **Ask Question:** "What's your biggest online shopping frustration? 👇"
3. **Pin Top Comment:** Feature user testimonial or FAQ answer
4. **Story Re-share:** Share to Stories with poll: "Would you try this?"

---

## Paid Promotion Specs
- **Budget:** $50-100 per post
- **Audience:** Women 18-35, interest in fashion, online shopping
- **Objective:** Traffic (drive to link in bio)
- **Placement:** Instagram Feed + Explore
- **Duration:** 7 days

---

## A/B Testing Variables
- Test different opening hooks:
  - Version A: "Watch this..."
  - Version B: "Wait for it..."
  - Version C: "You won't believe this..."
- Test final CTA:
  - Version A: "Try FREE"
  - Version B: "Get 10 FREE Try-Ons"
  - Version C: "Start Free Trial"

---

## Asset Production Notes
- **Photography:** Use real user photos (with permission) OR hire relatable models
- **AI Try-On Examples:** Must be high quality, photorealistic
- **Before/After:** Ensure good lighting match for credibility
- **Brand Colors:** Purple (#6B46C1), Pink (#EC4899), White (#FFFFFF)
- **Typography:** Montserrat (all weights), fallback: Helvetica Neue

---

## Success Metrics
- **Target Engagement Rate:** 4-6%
- **Target Link Clicks:** 3-5% of reach
- **Target Shares:** 100+ per post
- **Target Saves:** 200+ per post

---

## Alternative Versions

### TikTok Adaptation (9:16 vertical video)
**Duration:** 15 seconds

**0-3 sec:** User holds phone, skeptical look, text: "Trying this viral app..."
**3-7 sec:** Fast montage of uploading photo, selecting outfit, AI processing
**7-10 sec:** REVEAL - dramatic pause, face reaction
**10-15 sec:** Show final result, zoom on phone, text: "ORDERING IT NOW"

**Audio:** Trending suspense-to-payoff sound (update weekly)

**Caption:** Same as Instagram but add trending hashtags
