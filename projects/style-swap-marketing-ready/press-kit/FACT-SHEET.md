## Mission & Vision

**Mission:**  
Eliminate the waste, cost, and frustration of online shopping returns by empowering consumers to see exactly how clothes will look on their body before making a purchase.

**Vision:**  
A world where every online shopper can confidently buy clothes that fit perfectly the first time, reducing fashion waste and creating a more sustainable future.

## The Problem

### Fashion Returns Crisis
- **10 billion pounds** of returned clothing end up in landfills annually (USA alone)
- **$550 billion** in annual return costs globally (shipping, processing, waste)
- **Average return rate:** 30-40% for online fashion purchases
- **Environmental impact:** 15 million tons of CO2 from return shipping yearly

### Consumer Pain Points
- Ordering multiple sizes and returning what doesn't fit
- Waiting weeks for delivery only to be disappointed
- Wasting time and money on return shipping
- Uncertainty about fit, color, and style
- Guilt about environmental impact

---

## The Solution

**Style Swap** is an AI-powered virtual try-on app that shows users photorealistic images of themselves wearing any outfit before they purchase online.

### How It Works
1. **Upload a photo** (any selfie, 10 seconds)
2. **Browse 10,000+ brands** (Zara, ASOS, Nordstrom, H&M, and more)
3. **See yourself wearing it** (AI-generated photorealistic result in 3 seconds)
4. **Buy with confidence** (only order what you know works)

### Key Technology
- **Computer Vision AI:** Maps body shape, proportions, and posture
- **Generative AI:** Creates photorealistic clothing overlay
- **Color Matching:** Adapts to user's skin tone and lighting
- **Fabric Simulation:** Accurately represents material drape and texture
- **Processing Time:** Under 3 seconds per try-on
