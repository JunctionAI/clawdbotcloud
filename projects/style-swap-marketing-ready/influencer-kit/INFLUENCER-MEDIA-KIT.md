## Content Guidelines

### What We're Looking For

**Authentic Storytelling:**
- Real experiences with the app
- Genuine reactions to AI try-on results
- Personal style journey integration
- Honest testimonial (not scripted)

**High-Quality Production:**
- Good lighting and audio
- Clear, engaging visuals
- Professional editing (but not overly polished)
- Vertical video optimized for mobile

**Educational + Entertaining:**
- Show how the app works (onboarding viewers)
- Demonstrate multiple try-ons (variety)
- Share surprising/delightful moments
- Compare to old shopping methods

---

### Content Angles That Perform Well

1. **"POV: You Discovered the App Saving Everyone Hundreds"**
   - Relatable hook
   - Before/after comparison (your shopping habits)
   - Transformation reveal

2. **"Trying This Viral Outfit on MY Body First"**
   - Trend participation
   - Personal body type representation
   - Confidence-building message

3. **"I Tried On 50 Outfits in 10 Minutes"**
   - Fast-paced montage
   - Showcases app speed and variety
   - Entertainment value

4. **"Zero Returns for 3 Months: Here's How"**
   - Sustainability angle
   - Money-saving testimonial
   - Lifestyle integration

5. **"Get Ready With Me (But Make It Virtual)"**
   - Lifestyle content
   - Natural product integration
   - Aspirational but relatable

---

### Required Disclosures

**FTC Compliance:**
All sponsored content must include clear disclosure:
- Instagram/TikTok: #ad or #sponsored in caption
- YouTube: "This video includes a paid partnership with Style Swap"
- Verbal disclosure for video content: "This video is sponsored by Style Swap"

**Placement:**
- Disclosure must be upfront (first 3 seconds for video, first line of caption)
- Must be clearly visible (not hidden in "...more")

## FAQs

**Q: Can I be honest if the app doesn't work for me?**  
A: Yes! We value authenticity. If you have concerns, reach out to your partnership manager first. We'd rather address issues than have inauthentic content.

**Q: Do I need to show my face?**  
A: Not required, but face + voiceover content tends to perform better. We're open to creative approaches.

**Q: Can I promote competitors?**  
A: We don't require exclusivity for one-off campaigns. For long-term partnerships, we discuss non-compete terms.

**Q: How long does content need to stay live?**  
A: Minimum 90 days for sponsored posts. Longer is appreciated but not required.

**Q: Can I repurpose the content?**  
A: Yes, you retain rights to your content. We may request rights to repurpose for our marketing (with credit and approval).

**Q: What if my content doesn't perform well?**  
A: No penalties. We understand not every post goes viral. We evaluate partnerships on overall body of work, not single posts.
