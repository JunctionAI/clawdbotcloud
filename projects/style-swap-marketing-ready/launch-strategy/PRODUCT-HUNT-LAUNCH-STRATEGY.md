## 1. Pre-Launch Preparation (4-6 Weeks Before)

### Week 1-2: Build Product Hunt Presence

**Create Product Hunt Account (If New):**
- Use personal founder account (not company account)
- Complete profile with photo, bio, social links
- Start engaging: Upvote products, leave thoughtful comments (5-10/day)
- Build reputation score (aim for 50+ before launch)

**Research Competitors:**
- Search Product Hunt for "virtual try-on," "fashion AI," "shopping app"
- Analyze top performers:
  - What headlines worked?
  - What taglines resonated?
  - How did they structure descriptions?
  - What questions did users ask?
- Create swipe file of best practices

**Identify Your Hunter:**
- Look for top hunters in fashion/tech category
- Check their recent launch success rates
- Reach out 4 weeks before (see Hunter Selection section)

---

### Week 3-4: Create Launch Assets

**1. Product Hunt Thumbnail (240x240px)**
- Eye-catching icon/logo
- High contrast, vibrant colors (Style Swap purple/pink)
- Clear and recognizable at small size
- PNG with transparent background

**2. Gallery Images (4-6 images, 1270x760px)**
- Image 1: Hero shot (app interface with before/after)
- Image 2: Key feature #1 (Upload photo process)
- Image 3: Key feature #2 (AI try-on result)
- Image 4: Key feature #3 (Browse brands)
- Image 5: Social proof (testimonials, user stats)
- Image 6: Call-to-action (Download now)

**Design Tips:**
- High resolution, professional quality
- Consistent branding (colors, fonts)
- Minimal text (visuals speak louder)
- Show actual app UI (not mockups)

**3. Video/GIF Demo (30-60 seconds)**
- Screen recording of app in action
- Narrated walkthrough OR text overlays
- Show complete user journey:
  - Upload photo
  - Select outfit
  - See AI result
  - Compare options
  - Make purchase decision
- End with clear CTA

**4. First Comment (Prepared in Advance)**
- Founder introduction
- Problem statement
- Solution overview
- Key stats/achievements
- Exclusive Product Hunt offer
- Ask for feedback

---

### Week 5-6: Build Launch Support Network

**Internal Team Mobilization:**
- Brief all team members on launch plan
- Assign specific roles:
  - Founder: Main engagement, answering questions
  - CTO: Technical questions
  - Marketing: Community management, sharing
  - Design: Asset updates if needed
- Create shared Slack channel for real-time coordination

**Rally Early Supporters:**
- Email existing users (2-3 days before launch)
  - "We're launching on Product Hunt! Support us?"
  - Include exact launch time and link
  - Offer exclusive Product Hunt promo code
- Reach out to investors, advisors, friends
  - Personal DM/email
  - Ask for upvote + comment (not just upvote)
- Prepare social media posts for:
  - Personal accounts (founders)
  - Company accounts (Style Swap)
  - Team members

## 3. Product Hunt Page Optimization

### Headline (60 characters max)

**Formula:** [What It Does] + [Key Benefit]

**Examples:**
- "Try on any outfit before you buy—virtually" ✅ (Clear, benefit-focused)
- "AI virtual try-on for 10,000+ fashion brands" ✅ (Specific, compelling)
- "See yourself in clothes before clicking 'buy'" ✅ (Relatable problem/solution)

**Avoid:**
- "The future of online shopping" ❌ (Vague)
- "Revolutionary fashion tech platform" ❌ (Buzzwords)

---

### Tagline (120 characters max)

**Formula:** [Problem] + [Solution] + [Result]

**Recommended:**
"Stop guessing if clothes will fit. Upload a photo, try on any outfit in seconds, and only buy what works. 95% accurate AI."

**Alternatives:**
- "Reduce returns by 87%. See photorealistic AI try-ons of yourself wearing ANY outfit from 10K+ brands in 3 seconds."
- "The AI stylist that shows you how clothes look on YOUR body—not a model's. 2.5M users, zero returns."

<a name="launch-day"></a>
## 4. Launch Day Strategy

### Timeline (All Times PST)

**12:01 AM - Launch Goes Live**
- Hunter posts product
- Founder posts "First Comment" immediately
- Tweet launch announcement (tag @ProductHunt)
- Post to LinkedIn, Instagram Stories, Facebook

**6:00 AM - Morning Push**
- Email blast to user base (Subject: "We're #1 on Product Hunt!")
- Post to all social channels again
- Message Slack communities, Discord servers (where relevant)

**9:00 AM - Peak Engagement Period**
- Monitor comments religiously (respond within 5 minutes)
- Upvote every comment
- Thank every supporter
- Answer all questions thoroughly

**12:00 PM - Midday Check-In**
- Assess ranking (goal: Top 5)
- If slipping, activate reserve supporters
- Share updates on social ("We're #3! Help us get to #1!")

**3:00 PM - Afternoon Rally**
- Second email to users who haven't engaged yet
- Personal DMs to close network (ask for comments, not just upvotes)
- Engage with OTHER Product Hunt launches (give to get)

**6:00 PM - Evening Push**
- Final social media push
- Thank-you posts for supporters
- Share milestone updates ("500 upvotes! You're amazing!")

**11:00 PM - Final Hour**
- Last-ditch effort for upvotes
- Engage with every new comment
- Prepare for next-day follow-up

<a name="engagement"></a>
## 5. Community Engagement Tactics

### Responding to Comments

**Best Practices:**
- Respond to EVERY comment within 5 minutes
- Use the commenter's name
- Provide thoughtful, detailed answers
- Ask follow-up questions (engagement begets engagement)
- Upvote their comment (they'll feel valued)
- Be genuine, not robotic

---

### Sample Response Templates

**For General Praise:**
```
Thanks so much, [Name]! 🙏 We're really proud of what we've built. Have you had a chance to try it yet? Would love to hear your feedback if you do!
```

**For Constructive Criticism:**
```
Great point, [Name]. We've actually been thinking about [their concern]. Here's our current approach: [explain]. What would your ideal solution look like?
```

**For Technical Questions:**
```
Excellent question, [Name]! Here's how it works: [detailed explanation]. Our CTO [tag them] can dive deeper if you're curious about the tech stack!
```

**For Feature Requests:**
```
Love this idea, [Name]! We've had [X number] of users ask for this. It's on our roadmap for Q[X]. Want to be a beta tester when it's ready?
```

---

### Driving Comments (Not Just Upvotes)

**Why Comments Matter More:**
- Product Hunt algorithm weighs comments heavily
- Comments = engagement = higher ranking
- Comments provide social proof

**How to Drive Comments:**
- Ask specific questions in description
- Reply to every comment (encourages others to comment)
- Run a "best comment wins Premium free for a year" contest
- Post teasers: "Big announcement at 500 upvotes 👀"

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ Launching on Monday or Friday (low traffic days)  
❌ Not having a hunter (less visibility)  
❌ Asking for upvotes too directly (violates PH rules)  
❌ Ignoring comments (hurts ranking algorithm)  
❌ Poor quality gallery images (first impressions matter)  
❌ Not preparing team in advance (scrambling on launch day)  
❌ Forgetting to engage with other launches (community karma)
