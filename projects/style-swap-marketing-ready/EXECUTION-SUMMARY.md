## 🚨 Common Mistakes to Avoid

### Week 1 Mistakes
❌ Launching everything at once (you can't monitor effectively)  
✅ Do: Launch ads first, then add social, then influencers

❌ Changing ad campaigns after 2 days (not enough data)  
✅ Do: Wait 7 days minimum before optimizing

❌ Posting social content without engagement plan  
✅ Do: Respond to every comment within 1 hour

### Week 2-4 Mistakes
❌ Scaling budgets too fast (>50% increases)

❌ Ignoring metrics (flying blind)  
✅ Do: Daily dashboard review (15 min)

❌ Creating new content from scratch  
✅ Do: Use the templates provided!

### Technical Issues
- **Meta Ads Setup:** Read `/ad-campaigns/META-ADS-COMPLETE-SETUP-GUIDE.md` section 2

### Strategic Questions
- **Which ad to run first:** Start with user testimonial (highest conversion)
- **Which social post first:** Transformation carousel (highest engagement)
- **Which blog first:** Fashion AI guide (highest traffic potential)

### Resource Constraints
- **Limited budget:** Focus on ads + top 3 social posts only
- **Limited time:** Use templates as-is, minimal customization
- **Limited team:** Outsource creative, focus on strategy/optimization
