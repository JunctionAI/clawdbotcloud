## What is Virtual Try-On?

**Virtual try-on** is AI-powered technology that creates realistic images of you wearing clothes, accessories, or makeup without physically trying them on.

### How It Works (Simple Explanation)

1. **Upload a photo** of yourself (selfie, full-body pic, any casual photo)
2. **AI analyzes your body** (shape, proportions, skin tone, posture, lighting)
3. **Select a garment** from thousands of brands and millions of products
4. **AI overlays the clothing** onto your image, simulating fabric texture, drape, and fit
5. **See the result** in 3-5 seconds—a photorealistic image of YOU wearing the outfit

## Tips for Getting the Most Accurate Results

### 1. Use a Recent, High-Quality Photo
- Taken within the last 6 months (if your body/hair has changed significantly)
- Minimum 1080px resolution
- Clear facial features and body outline

### 2. Wear Form-Fitting Clothing in Your Photo
- Leggings/jeans + fitted top = AI sees your true shape
- Avoid baggy sweaters or oversized clothes
- AI needs to understand your proportions

### 3. Try Multiple Sizes
- If you're between sizes, try on both
- AI shows fit differences clearly
- Make informed decisions (order the right size first time)

### 4. Check Fabric Type
- Structured fabrics (denim, blazers) = very accurate
- Flowy fabrics (silk, chiffon) = slightly less precise (but still helpful)
- Knitwear and stretchy materials = AI shows approximate fit

### 5. Read Reviews After Trying On
- Virtual try-on shows YOU, reviews show crowd-sourced fit data
- Combination = highest confidence purchase
