## Body Positive Shopping: Tips for All Body Types

### For Petite Body Types (Under 5'4")

**What to Try Virtually:**
- Cropped pants and high-waisted styles
- Vertical stripes and monochrome outfits
- Fitted silhouettes (avoid overwhelming volume)

**Virtual Try-On Benefit:**
- See if proportions work before buying
- Check if "petite" sizing is necessary
- Find brands that fit YOUR specific height

---

### For Tall Body Types (Over 5'9")

**What to Try Virtually:**
- Maxi lengths and longer inseams
- Wide-leg pants and oversized silhouettes
- Layered looks and statement pieces

**Virtual Try-On Benefit:**
- Confirm dress/pant length before ordering
- See if "tall" sizing is needed
- Discover brands with longer cuts

---

### For Curvy Body Types

**What to Try Virtually:**
- Wrap dresses and defined waists
- Structured fabrics that drape well
- V-necks and strategic color blocking

**Virtual Try-On Benefit:**
- See accurate fit (no more guessing)
- Avoid clothes that gap or pull
- Find brands that celebrate curves

---

### For Athletic/Straight Body Types

**What to Try Virtually:**
- Peplum tops and fit-and-flare dresses
- Ruffles and embellishments (add dimension)
- Belts and cinched waists

**Virtual Try-On Benefit:**
- Visualize how texture and structure enhance your shape
- Try bold styles risk-free
- Find surprising fits
