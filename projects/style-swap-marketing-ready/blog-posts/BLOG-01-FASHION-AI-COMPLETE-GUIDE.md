## What is Fashion AI?

**Fashion AI** refers to the application of artificial intelligence technologies—including machine learning, computer vision, and generative AI—to the fashion and retail industry.

### The Core Technologies

**1. Computer Vision**
- Analyzes images to understand garments, colors, styles, and fit
- Powers visual search ("find me outfits like this photo")
- Enables virtual try-on by mapping clothes onto body shapes

**2. Machine Learning**
- Learns your style preferences over time
- Predicts what you'll love based on past choices
- Recommends personalized outfits and brands

**3. Generative AI**
- Creates photorealistic images of you wearing clothes
- Generates new design concepts
- Produces personalized styling suggestions

**4. Natural Language Processing (NLP)**
- Powers chatbot stylists
- Understands text-based style queries ("show me boho summer dresses")
- Analyzes fashion trends from social media and blogs

### 4. Size & Fit Prediction: Get the Right Size, First Time

**The Problem:**  
Sizing inconsistency across brands. A size Medium at Zara doesn't fit the same as a Medium at H&M, leading to constant returns and frustration.

**The AI Solution:**  
AI-powered fit technology analyzes your body measurements, brand-specific sizing charts, and thousands of user reviews to predict your perfect size—for every brand.

**How It Works:**
- **Body Measurement:** Input your measurements once (or use AI body scanning via your phone camera)
- **Brand Analysis:** AI knows that Brand X runs small, Brand Y runs large, Brand Z has inconsistent sizing
- **Crowd-Sourced Data:** Millions of user reviews train the AI ("This dress ran small," "Perfect fit for my body type")
- **Personalized Prediction:** AI recommends your exact size for every specific item

**Leading Platforms:**
- **True Fit** - Powers fit recommendations for 17,000+ brands
- **Fit Analytics** - Used by ASOS, Zalando, and major retailers
- **Bold Metrics** - AI body measurement from photos

**Impact:**
- 70% reduction in size-related returns
- Increased purchase confidence (shoppers more likely to buy when size is accurate)
- Works across international sizing systems (US, UK, EU, Asia)

### 5. Trend Forecasting: Know What's Next

**The Problem:**  
Fashion trends move fast. By the time something's "in," it's often already on its way out.

**The AI Solution:**  
AI analyzes millions of data points—runway shows, social media, search trends, weather patterns, cultural events—to predict what'll be trending next season (or next week).

**How It Works:**
- **Data Sources:** Instagram hashtags, Pinterest saves, Google searches, TikTok trends, runway shows
- **Pattern Recognition:** AI identifies emerging patterns before they go mainstream
- **Predictive Analytics:** Forecasts which styles, colors, and items will surge in popularity

**Who Uses It:**
- **Fashion Brands:** Decide what to design and manufacture (H&M, Zara use AI forecasting)
- **Retailers:** Stock inventory based on predicted demand
- **Shoppers:** Get ahead of trends (buy items before they sell out or prices spike)

**Real Example:**  
In 2025, AI predicted the "coastal grandmother" trend would peak in Spring 2026. Brands that used this data stocked linen sets and neutral tones early—and sold out immediately.
