# Junction Media — Client Acquisition Playbook
*Created: February 2026 | Goal: 4 clients @ $5–10k/month*

---

## Target Profile

**Ideal client:**
- NZ or AU business, $1M–$10M revenue
- Has a marketing budget but isn't using it well
- Ecommerce, B2B SaaS, professional services, or hospitality
- Founder/CEO who thinks strategically (not just "run some ads")
- Growth plateau — things are working but not accelerating
- Open to AI/systems thinking (not looking for a "campaign")

**Anti-client (do not pursue):**
- Under $500k revenue (can't afford the engagement)
- Already has a large in-house marketing team
- Wants cheap, wants fast, or is purely tactical
- Requests work by the hour
- Doesn't have a real product/service (just idea-stage)

## LinkedIn Content Calendar (for inbound)

Post 3–4x/week as Tom Hall-Taylor from his personal LinkedIn (not the company page). Personal > brand for B2B.

### Post Types That Convert

**1. Behind-the-scenes (most engagement)**
> "Here's what we actually built for a NZ supplement brand to hit +30% revenue..."
> *(Share the DBH case study, step by step, with honest numbers)*

**2. Contrarian takes (high reach)**
> "Most marketing agencies track the wrong things. Here's what we track instead."
> "AI won't save your marketing. Here's what will."
> "Why I only work with 4 clients at a time."

**3. Education posts (trust-building)**
> "The 5-part marketing stack we build for every client"
> "Why email automation pays for itself in month 1"
> "What 'AI marketing' actually means (vs the hype)"

**4. Social proof (converts)**
> "Client update: [result]"
> "Application closed — we're at capacity. Next slot: [date]"

### Post Format (works on LinkedIn)

```
[Hook — 1 line that stops the scroll]

[2–3 short paragraphs of insight]

[Specific, honest result or example]

[Call to action — link to apply or DM]
```

## Objection Handling

**"We already have a marketing agency"**
> "That's actually our most common starting point. Most clients come from an existing agency relationship — just one that's gotten stale. Happy to share how we're different, but no pressure."

**"We can't afford $5-10k/month"**
> "Totally understand. We're not for everyone on budget. The clients we work with typically see $5-10k/month pay for itself within 60-90 days — but only if your business has the right foundation. Worth a call to figure out if you're at that point?"

**"We tried AI marketing before and it didn't work"**
> "Almost everyone says that. What they tried was AI tools — we build AI systems. Big difference. One replaces tasks, the other multiplies output. Would love to show you the difference — 20 minutes?"

**"I need to think about it"**
> "Of course. We only take 4-5 clients at a time though, so if you're interested I'd apply sooner than later. Happy to hold a spot for 48 hours if that helps. What's holding you back?"
