## What a Well-Run Google Ads Campaign Looks Like

Many Nelson businesses have tried Google Ads and concluded it doesn't work. In most cases, the problem wasn't the platform — it was the execution. Here's what separates effective Google Ads management from wasted spend.

### Keyword Strategy That Matches Buying Intent

Not all keywords are equal. A broad keyword like "landscaping" captures people at all stages of the purchase journey — many of them not ready to buy. A specific keyword like "landscaper Nelson residential quote" captures someone at the bottom of the funnel, ready to engage.

We build keyword strategies around purchase intent, not search volume. We identify the terms your customers use when they're ready to act, write ads that speak directly to those terms, and build campaigns that maximise conversion from high-intent clicks.

### Negative Keywords: The Most Underused Lever in Google Ads

Google Ads campaigns bleed budget through irrelevant searches when negative keyword lists are neglected. We build comprehensive negative keyword lists from day one and refine them constantly using search term reports. Every dollar saved from an irrelevant click is a dollar that can be spent on a customer who's actually ready to buy.

### Ad Copy That Converts

Google search ads have limited real estate. Every character counts. We write ad copy that speaks to the specific intent behind each keyword, uses compelling calls to action, includes relevant ad extensions (call extensions, sitelinks, structured snippets), and tests variations systematically to improve click-through rate.

Higher click-through rate improves your Quality Score, which reduces your cost-per-click. Better ad copy is cheaper ad spend — it compounds.

### Landing Pages That Convert Clicks into Customers

The ad gets the click. The landing page makes the sale. We optimise landing pages to convert — clear value propositions, trust signals, frictionless contact forms, and strong calls to action. A landing page optimised for conversion can double or triple the revenue from the same ad spend.

### Conversion Tracking That Shows What's Working

You can't manage what you can't measure. We set up comprehensive conversion tracking — form submissions, phone calls, live chat engagements — so you know exactly which campaigns, keywords, and ads are driving revenue. That data drives continuous optimisation.
