## How Meta Ads Work (And Why They're Different from Google)

Understanding the difference between Meta Ads and Google Ads is essential for knowing when and how to use each.

### Google Ads: Demand Capture

Google Ads intercepts people who are already searching for what you offer. High intent, ready to buy. Great for services people search for when they need them — plumbing, dentistry, accounting.

### Meta Ads: Demand Generation

Meta Ads reach people who haven't searched for anything yet — but who match the profile of your ideal customer. You define who they are (demographics, interests, behaviours, location), and Meta shows your ad to them while they're scrolling Facebook or Instagram.

This is powerful for:

- **Products and services people discover rather than search for** — Boutique clothing, interior design, specialty food, health and wellness
- **Building an audience before purchase intent is active** — Retargeting people who visited your website but didn't convert
- **Brand awareness at scale** — Making your business known to Nelson consumers before they need your category
- **Event promotion** — Reaching Nelson locals about upcoming events, openings, and launches

## What Effective Meta Ads Look Like

The gap between a Meta Ads campaign that works and one that doesn't is almost always in the creative and the audience targeting. Here's how we approach it.

### Creative That Stops the Scroll

Meta Ads compete for attention in a feed full of friends' photos, news, and entertainment. Your ad has a fraction of a second to earn a pause. We create scroll-stopping creative — video, carousel, static image — that communicates your value proposition instantly and compels the click.

For Nelson businesses, effective creative often leans into the local: Nelson landscapes, real local customers, authentic business stories. Meta's algorithm rewards creative that generates genuine engagement, and locally resonant content consistently outperforms generic.

### Audience Targeting That Reaches the Right People

Meta's targeting capabilities are extensive. We use a combination of:

- **Interest-based targeting** — Reaching people who follow relevant pages, have relevant interests, or exhibit relevant behaviours
- **Lookalike audiences** — Meta's AI finds people who look like your existing customers
- **Custom audiences** — Retargeting your website visitors, email list, or social media engagers
- **Location targeting** — Nelson city, Nelson-Marlborough region, or specific postcodes

The right targeting mix depends on your business, your budget, and your objective.

### Campaign Structure for the Full Funnel

Effective Meta Ads work across the full customer journey:

**Awareness** — Reach new people who match your target profile. Build brand familiarity.

**Consideration** — Re-engage people who've shown interest. Drive website visits, video views, lead form fills.

**Conversion** — Capture people ready to act. Drive purchases, bookings, or inquiry submissions.

We structure campaigns to work across all three stages, allocating budget according to where the opportunity is greatest for your specific business.

### Measurement That Proves ROI

Meta Ads have become more complex to measure since iOS privacy changes, but we use a combination of Meta's Conversions API, UTM tracking, and multi-touch attribution modelling to give you a genuine picture of what your campaigns are producing. We report on leads, cost-per-lead, and revenue impact — not just reach and impressions.
