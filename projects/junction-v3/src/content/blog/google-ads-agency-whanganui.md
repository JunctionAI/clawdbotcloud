## The Whanganui Search Landscape

To understand the Google Ads opportunity in Whanganui, you need to understand the search market.

### A City With Real Search Volume

Whanganui has a population of around 45,000 and a regional catchment that extends to the Ruapehu and Rangitīkei districts. The combined population base generates meaningful daily search volume across virtually every category of local service — trades, healthcare, professional services, food and hospitality, retail, automotive, and more.

This isn't a market too small for Google Ads to work. It's a market where Google Ads is underutilised, which creates an advantage for businesses willing to use it properly.

### Low Competition = Better Economics

Whanganui has fewer businesses actively running Google Ads campaigns than Wellington or Hamilton. That means:

- **Lower average cost-per-click** — You pay less for each visitor than businesses in larger cities pay
- **Higher impression share** — Your ads appear more frequently for the same budget
- **Less competition for top placements** — Positions 1–3 in Google Search results are achievable at lower bids

A $1,500/month Google Ads budget in Whanganui can achieve results that would require $4,000–$6,000/month in Auckland. The economics of paid search in smaller NZ cities are genuinely favourable.

### The Regional Service Area

Many Whanganui-based businesses serve not just the city but the wider region — Marton, Bulls, Taihape, Raetihi, Waiouru, Ohakune. Google Ads geographic targeting lets you precisely define your service area, showing ads to people searching from the specific locations you serve and excluding areas you don't.

This means your budget isn't wasted on clicks from people outside your delivery radius. Every click is a potential customer you can actually help.

## What Makes Google Ads Work in Whanganui

The fundamentals of good Google Ads management are the same everywhere. But the specific tactics and priorities look different in a Whanganui context.

### Keyword Strategy Built for Local Intent

The keywords that generate revenue in Whanganui are different from national keyword lists. "Plumber Whanganui", "dentist Whanganui", "accountant Whanganui" — these are the searches that matter. We build keyword strategies around the specific local terms your customers use, including common variants, service modifiers, and geographic qualifiers.

We also conduct thorough negative keyword research — identifying the searches that look relevant but don't convert for your business. Wasted clicks on irrelevant searches are one of the biggest reasons Google Ads campaigns fail to deliver ROI.

### Ad Copy That Speaks to Whanganui Customers

Generic national ad copy doesn't resonate with local searchers. We write ad copy that speaks directly to the Whanganui context — referencing local knowledge, local availability, and the specific advantages of working with a Whanganui-based business over a national provider.

For many services, local trust is a genuine differentiator. Whanganui customers often prefer a local provider over an out-of-town alternative, all else being equal. Good ad copy makes that preference visible in the moments it matters.

### Campaign Structures That Scale

We build campaigns with clean, logical structures that are easy to optimise and scale. Ad groups organised by keyword intent, single keyword ad groups for high-value terms, and campaign budgets allocated according to ROI performance — not set-and-forget.

As we gather performance data, we shift budget toward what's working and pause what isn't. Google Ads accounts improve continuously under active management. A campaign that generates 10 leads per month in month one should be generating 15–20 per month by month four, from the same budget, through systematic optimisation.

### Conversion Tracking That Shows the Full Picture

Setting up conversion tracking properly is one of the highest-value things we do for new clients. Without it, you don't know which clicks became customers. With it, you can see exactly which keywords, ads, and campaigns are driving real business results.

We track phone calls from ads, form submissions, booking completions, and live chat initiations — building a complete picture of how Google Ads is contributing to your pipeline.
