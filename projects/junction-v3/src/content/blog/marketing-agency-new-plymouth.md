## Taranaki's Business Landscape

The region's economy is more diverse than most outsiders realise:

- **Energy and engineering** — New Plymouth is NZ's energy capital, home to the oil and gas sector and a dense cluster of engineering, inspection, and technical services firms
- **Manufacturing and trades** — precision engineering, fabrication, and construction businesses with B2B marketing needs
- **Agriculture** — Taranaki's dairy sector is world-class; agribusiness and rural services have growing digital needs
- **Tourism** — Mount Taranaki, surf beaches, and the arts/culture scene drive domestic tourism
- **Professional services and retail** — a tight-knit local market serving a high-income professional population
