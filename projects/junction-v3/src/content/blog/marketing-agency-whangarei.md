## Northland's Business Landscape

Whangarei and the wider Northland region has a distinctive mix:

- **Marine industry** — Whangarei is NZ's superyacht capital; the marine sector (boat building, refits, chandlery) has both B2B and premium consumer marketing needs
- **Tourism** — the Bay of Islands, Northland beaches, and Māori heritage attract significant domestic and international visitors
- **Trades and construction** — major residential and infrastructure growth across Northland
- **Agriculture and horticulture** — a major growing region (avocados, kiwifruit, citrus) with agribusiness services
- **Professional services** — healthcare, legal, accounting, and specialist services for a large regional population
- **Retail and hospitality** — a revitalised Whangarei CBD and growing café/dining culture
