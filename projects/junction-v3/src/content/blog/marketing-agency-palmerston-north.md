# Marketing Agency Palmerston North

Palmerston North — Palmy to locals — is one of New Zealand's most underrated cities from a business perspective. It's a logistics hub, home to Massey University (one of NZ's largest universities), a significant food and AgriTech sector, and a dense professional services market. For marketing, this is fertile ground.

Junction Media works with NZ businesses across the country. We build AI-native marketing systems that generate leads, build authority, and compound results over time.

## Palmerston North's Economy

The Manawatū region has several distinct business clusters:

- **Education and research** — Massey University and associated research institutes create a knowledge economy with professional services demand
- **Food and AgriTech** — a world-class food innovation hub, including New Zealand Food Innovation Network
- **Logistics and transport** — the geographic centre of NZ's North Island makes Palmy a logistics pivot point
- **Healthcare** — MidCentral DHB (now Health NZ) and a dense allied health market
- **Professional services and retail** — a strong local economy serving a large student and professional population
