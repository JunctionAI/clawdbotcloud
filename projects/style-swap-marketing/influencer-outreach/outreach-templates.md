## OUTREACH STRATEGY

### Research Phase:
1. **Identify** potential influencers via:
   - Instagram/TikTok search (#fashion, #OOTD, etc.)
   - Creator databases (Aspire IQ, Upfluence, Creator.co)
   - Competitor analysis (who works with similar brands?)
   - Google search for fashion bloggers

2. **Vet** candidates:
   - Engagement rate (>3% is good)
   - Audience demographics (age, location, interests)
   - Content quality and authenticity
   - Previous brand partnerships
   - Comments quality (real engagement vs. bots)

3. **Prioritize** by:
   - Tier (micro/mid/macro)
   - Niche alignment (body positivity, sustainability, etc.)
   - Audience fit (matches our target demographic)
   - Budget constraints

### Outreach Phase:
1. **Personalize** every email (reference specific content)
2. **Send** in batches (10-20 per day)
3. **Track** in CRM/spreadsheet:
   - Contact info
   - Outreach date
   - Response status
   - Follow-up dates
   - Partnership details

### Follow-Up Schedule:
- **Day 0:** Initial outreach
- **Day 3-5:** First follow-up (if no response)
- **Day 10-12:** Second follow-up (if still no response)
- **Day 20:** Final follow-up or move to "not interested" list

### Relationship Building:
- **Engage** with their content (likes, meaningful comments)
- **Share** their content on your brand channels
- **Support** them even if they don't partner with you
- **Stay top of mind** for future opportunities

## MEASUREMENT & TRACKING

Track performance of each influencer partnership:

- **Engagement metrics:** Likes, comments, shares, saves
- **Traffic:** Click-throughs to landing page
- **Conversions:** Sign-ups, trial starts, purchases
- **Promo code usage:** Redemptions from unique codes
- **ROI:** Revenue generated vs. cost of partnership
- **Audience growth:** Follower increase during campaign
- **Brand mentions:** UGC created, hashtag usage
- **Qualitative feedback:** DMs, comments about brand

Calculate:
- **Cost Per Acquisition (CPA):** Partnership cost ÷ conversions
- **Return on Ad Spend (ROAS):** Revenue ÷ partnership cost
- **Engagement Rate:** (Likes + comments + shares) ÷ follower count

Optimize future partnerships based on data.
