## TikTok-Specific Strategy

### Best Posting Times:
- **Peak:** 6-9 PM (evenings when people are scrolling)
- **Secondary:** 7-9 AM (morning scroll), 12-1 PM (lunch break)

### Optimal Video Length:
- **Viral potential:** 7-15 seconds (quick, rewatchable)
- **Educational:** 30-60 seconds (more depth)
- **Testimonials:** 15-30 seconds (long enough for story)

### Hook Timing:
- **First 1 second** must stop the scroll
- Use text overlay immediately
- Fast movement or visual interest

### Audio Strategy:
- Use trending sounds (check TikTok Creative Center)
- Original voiceover content can also go viral
- Mix 70% trending audio + 30% original

### Hashtag Strategy:
- 3-5 relevant hashtags (TikTok prioritizes content quality over hashtag stuffing)
- Always include #StyleSwap
- Mix broad (#FashionTok) + niche (#VirtualTryOn)
- Add trending challenge hashtags when relevant

### Engagement Tactics:
- Ask questions in captions
- Create "Part 1" content (drives comments asking for Part 2)
- Respond to comments with video replies
- Duet and Stitch user content
- Run challenges with specific hashtags

### Series Ideas:
1. **"Try-On Tuesdays"** - Weekly outfit try-on challenge
2. **"Trend Test"** - Testing if viral trends work on real bodies
3. **"Cart Clean-Out"** - Trying items in shopping cart
4. **"Brand Battle"** - Comparing similar items from different brands
5. **"Style Swap vs Reality"** - Ordering items and comparing AI to actual

## Collaboration Opportunities

### UGC Creators:
- Pay creators $100-300 per video
- Provide talking points, not scripts
- Encourage authentic reactions
- Use their videos as ads

### Influencer Collabs:
- Micro: 10-50K followers ($200-500 per video)
- Mid: 50-200K followers ($500-2000 per video)
- Macro: 200K-1M+ ($2000-10000 per video)

### Challenge Partnerships:
- Partner with fashion brands for co-branded challenges
- "Try [Brand]'s new collection with Style Swap"
- Cross-promote for mutual benefit

---

## TikTok Ad Strategy

### Spark Ads:
- Boost high-performing organic content
- Looks native, performs better
- Target lookalike audiences

### TopView Ads:
- For major launches or campaigns
- Full-screen on app open
- High cost but massive reach

### In-Feed Ads:
- Looks like regular TikTok
- Use UGC-style creative
- Strong CTA to app download or trial signup

## Metrics to Track

- **View-through rate** (goal: >50%)
- **Engagement rate** (goal: >8%)
- **Share rate** (viral indicator - goal: >3%)
- **Follower growth** (goal: 5-10% monthly)
- **Link clicks** (traffic to app - goal: 3-5% CTR)
- **Hashtag performance** (#StyleSwap usage growth)

## Crisis/Negative Comment Strategy

**Don't delete negative comments** (unless abusive)

**Respond professionally:**
- "Thanks for the feedback! Can you DM us so we can help?"
- "We're always improving - what would make it better?"
- "Sorry it didn't work for you! Here's a tip that might help..."

**Turn negatives into content:**
- "Responding to your concerns about AI accuracy"
- "You asked, we answer: Does it really work?"
