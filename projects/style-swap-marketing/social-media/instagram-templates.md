## Content Calendar Recommendations

### Daily Posting Schedule:
- **Monday:** Educational (How It Works, Tips)
- **Tuesday:** User Testimonial / UGC Repost
- **Wednesday:** Transformation Before/After
- **Thursday:** Trend Alert / Seasonal Content
- **Friday:** Fun/Viral Hook Content
- **Saturday:** Community Challenge
- **Sunday:** Behind-the-Scenes / Company Culture

### Stories Daily (3-5 stories/day):
- Morning: Poll or Question
- Midday: User feature or repost
- Afternoon: Quick tip or hack
- Evening: Behind-the-scenes

### Reels Schedule (3-4 per week):
- 2x Transformation/testimonials
- 1x Educational/how-to
- 1x Viral trend participation

## Engagement Tactics

### Comments:
- Respond to ALL comments within 2 hours
- Ask follow-up questions
- Use emojis (but not excessively)
- Tag users when reposting their content

### Stories:
- Use interactive stickers (polls, questions, quizzes)
- Repost user mentions
- Share user-generated content
- Behind-the-scenes of team/AI

### Community Building:
- Feature "User of the Week"
- Run monthly style challenges
- Create shareable templates for users to fill out
- Encourage #StyleSwap hashtag use with incentives
