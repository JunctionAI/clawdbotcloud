## Content Strategy for Twitter/X

### Posting Frequency:
- **3-5 tweets per day**
- Mix of original tweets and retweets
- 1 thread per week
- Respond to ALL mentions within 2 hours

### Best Times to Post:
- **8-9 AM** (morning coffee scroll)
- **12-1 PM** (lunch break)
- **5-7 PM** (evening commute/unwinding)

### Tone/Voice:
- Casual, conversational
- Minimal capitalization (internet casual)
- Self-aware humor
- Data-driven but not boring
- Authentic, not corporate

### Engagement Tactics:
- Reply to every mention/comment
- Quote tweet user testimonials
- Run polls weekly
- Ask questions that drive replies
- Participate in trending conversations (where relevant)

### Thread Strategy:
- 1 educational thread per week
- 1 story thread per month
- Use threads for complex ideas, single tweets for quick wins

### Hashtag Usage:
- Minimal (Twitter algorithm doesn't favor hashtag stuffing)
- #StyleSwap on brand tweets
- Trending hashtags only when genuinely relevant
