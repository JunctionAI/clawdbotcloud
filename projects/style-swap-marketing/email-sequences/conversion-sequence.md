## Technical Setup

**Triggers:**
- Emails 1-3: Try-on count thresholds
- Emails 4-6: Time-based after depletion

**Exclusions:**
- Stop if user upgrades at any point
- Stop if user unsubscribes from promotional emails
- Don't send email 4 & 6 during first month of membership (wait for established users)

**Segmentation:**
- High engagers (10+ try-ons used) → More aggressive conversion messaging
- Low engagers (3-5 try-ons used) → Education-focused, softer sell
- Power savers (saved 8+ looks) → Emphasize unlimited combinations

**Success Metrics:**
- Overall free→paid conversion: 15-25%
- Average time to conversion: 14-30 days
- Preferred plan: 70% monthly, 30% annual
- Trial→paid conversion: 65-75%

**Next Steps After Conversion:**
- Move to "Retention & Engagement" sequence
- Send "Welcome to Unlimited" email
- Track usage patterns for upsell to Pro plan (after 3+ months)
