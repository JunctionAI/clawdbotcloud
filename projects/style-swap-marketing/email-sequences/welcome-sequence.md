## Technical Setup

**Triggers:**
- Email 1: User signs up
- Email 2: No photo upload after 6 hours
- Email 3: All users 24 hours after signup
- Email 4: No photo upload after 72 hours
- Email 5: No photo upload after 7 days

**Stops when:**
- User uploads photo and tries first outfit
- User unsubscribes
- User clicks "stop activation emails"

**Tag users for segmentation:**
- Opened but didn't click → "interested but hesitant"
- Clicked but didn't convert → "high intent, needs nurturing"
- No opens → "low engagement, exclude from future campaigns"
