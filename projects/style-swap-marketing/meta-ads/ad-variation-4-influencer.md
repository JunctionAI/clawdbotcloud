## Influencer Partnership Strategy

### Tier 1: Micro-Influencers (10K-100K followers)
- **Deliverables:** 1 feed post, 3 stories
- **Payment:** $500-2,000 + affiliate commission
- **Tracking:** Unique promo codes
- **Best for:** Authentic engagement, niche audiences

### Tier 2: Mid-Tier (100K-500K followers)
- **Deliverables:** 1 feed post, 1 reel, 5 stories
- **Payment:** $2,000-8,000 + affiliate
- **Tracking:** Unique codes + swipe-up links
- **Best for:** Scaling awareness

### Tier 3: Macro (500K-2M followers)
- **Deliverables:** 1 feed post, 1 reel, dedicated story highlight
- **Payment:** $8,000-30,000 + performance bonus
- **Tracking:** Custom landing pages
- **Best for:** Mass awareness, brand credibility

---

## Content Guidelines for Influencers

### DO:
✅ Show genuine reaction to seeing yourself in AI try-on
✅ Demonstrate the process (upload → select → see results)
✅ Share before/after with real outfit you purchased
✅ Be authentic—if you're excited, show it!
✅ Use your natural voice and style
✅ Include clear CTA (link in bio, promo code)

### DON'T:
❌ Make scripted, overly promotional content
❌ Show only screenshots—show yourself using it
❌ Make unrealistic claims
❌ Forget to disclose partnership (when applicable)

## Boosted Post Strategy

Once influencer posts organically:
1. Request permission to boost as ad
2. Run as "Partnership Ad" (influencer's account + Style Swap page)
3. Target influencer's followers + lookalikes
4. Budget: $30-100/day per influencer post
5. Run for 7-14 days

---

## Targeting (When Boosting)
- **Demographics:** Followers of partnered influencer
- **Interests:** Fashion, Online shopping, Influencer's niche
- **Lookalike:** 1-3% lookalike of influencer's followers
- **Placements:** Feed + Stories (where influencer content performs)

---

## Budget Recommendation
- **Influencer Payment:** $500-30,000 (depending on tier)
- **Boost Budget:** $30-100/day per post
- **Total Campaign:** $5,000-50,000/month (10-20 influencers mixed tiers)

---

## Success Metrics
- Engagement Rate on Influencer Post: >5%
- Promo Code Usage: >100 per influencer
- CPA from Influencer Traffic: <$12
- Brand Mentions/UGC Generated: >50 per influencer
