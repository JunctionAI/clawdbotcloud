## Ad Type: Video Ad (15-30 seconds)
## Objective: Conversions
## Audience: Women & Men 18-35, Fashion Early Adopters

---

## Video Script

### Scene 1 (0-3 sec)
**Visual:** Phone screen recording - user uploading selfie to Style Swap app
**Text overlay:** "Me trying this viral app everyone's talking about"
**Voiceover:** [None - trending audio track playing]

### Scene 2 (3-8 sec)
**Visual:** Fast montage - user selecting different outfits (5-6 quick taps)
**Text overlay:** "Trying on outfits from Zara, ASOS, Urban Outfitters..."
**Voiceover:** [Gasp of excitement]

### Scene 3 (8-15 sec)
**Visual:** Split screen showing 4 AI-generated try-ons of user in different outfits
**Text overlay:** "Wait... this actually looks REAL?"
**Reaction shot:** User's face showing genuine surprise/excitement
**Voiceover:** "Oh my god, this is insane!"

### Scene 4 (15-22 sec)
**Visual:** Before/After comparison - AI try-on vs. actual outfit received
**Text overlay:** "Ordered this based on the AI... IT'S IDENTICAL"
**User holding actual dress next to phone with AI version
**Voiceover:** "I'm never shopping the old way again"

### Scene 5 (22-30 sec)
**Visual:** App interface showing "10 Free Try-Ons" offer
**Text overlay:** "Try it FREE" + "styleswap.com"
**Final shot:** User wearing outfit, twirling happily
**Voiceover:** "Download Style Swap, link in bio!"

## Video Style
- **Format:** 9:16 vertical (Stories/Reels optimized)
- **Aesthetic:** Raw, authentic UGC-style (not polished)
- **Music:** Trending TikTok audio or upbeat pop instrumental
- **Captions:** On-screen text + closed captions (85% watch without sound)

---

## Creator Brief for UGC
**We need:** Real users showing genuine reactions
**Deliverables:** 
- Upload selfie on camera
- Show scrolling through outfits
- GENUINE surprise reaction when seeing results
- Before/after comparison with real outfit (if available)
- Call to action

**Pay rate:** $150-300 per video + usage rights

## Targeting
- **Demographics:** Women & Men 18-35
- **Interests:** TikTok creators, Fashion bloggers, Early tech adopters, Online shopping
- **Behaviors:** Engaged shoppers, Video viewers
- **Placements:** Instagram Reels, Facebook Feed, Stories
