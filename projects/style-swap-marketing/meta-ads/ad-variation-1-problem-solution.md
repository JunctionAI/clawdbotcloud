# Meta Ad Variation 1: Problem-Solution (Image Ad)

## Ad Type: Single Image Ad
## Objective: Conversions
## Audience: Women 25-45, Fashion & Online Shopping Interests

---

## Visual Concept
**Split-screen image:**
- LEFT (red/frustrated): Woman surrounded by delivered boxes, holding up ill-fitting dress, disappointed expression
