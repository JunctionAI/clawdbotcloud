## Design Specifications

### Image Specs
- **Ratio:** 1:1 (square)
- **Resolution:** 1080x1080px minimum
- **File type:** JPG or PNG
- **Text on image:** <20% of image area

### Branding
- Consistent Style Swap logo on each card (top right corner)
- Color scheme: Modern, clean (whites, soft pastels, accent color)
- Font: Sans-serif, highly readable

### Visual Flow
Cards should tell a story from left to right:
Problem → Solution Step 1 → Step 2 → Step 3 → Social Proof

---

## Targeting
- **Demographics:** Women & Men 25-50
- **Interests:** Online shopping, Fashion & beauty, Technology, Mobile apps
- **Behaviors:** Frequent online shoppers, Mobile shoppers
- **Placements:** Facebook Feed, Instagram Feed (carousel performs best in feed)

---

## Budget Recommendation
- **Testing:** $60/day
- **Scaling:** $250-600/day

---

## Success Metrics
- Carousel Engagement Rate: >3%
- CPA Target: <$9
- Link Clicks: >500/day at scale

---

## Pro Tips
- Users often don't swipe through all cards—make Card 1 strong enough to convert alone
- Each card should have a CTA (not just the last one)
- Test card order variations (social proof first vs. last)
- Monitor which cards get the most engagement
