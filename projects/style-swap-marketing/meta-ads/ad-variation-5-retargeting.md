## Dynamic Creative Elements

### Automatic Personalization
- Show outfits user tried on in ad creative (where available)
- Display number of try-ons used ("You've tried 10 outfits...")
- Countdown timers for offers
- Personalized headlines with user's saved brands

### Ad Format Options
- **Image Ads:** For quick, scroll-stopping hooks
- **Video Ads:** Show user's previous try-ons in montage
- **Collection Ads:** Display saved outfits + "See More" CTA

---

## Retargeting Funnel Strategy

**Top of Funnel:**
Landing page visitors → Sign-up focused ads

**Middle of Funnel:**
Signed up users → Photo upload + engagement ads

**Bottom of Funnel:**
Active free users → Upgrade to paid

**Retention:**
Trial users → Prevent churn
Cancelled users → Win-back campaigns

---

## Budget Allocation

- **Segment 1 (Landing visitors):** 25% of budget
- **Segment 2 (Signed up, no photo):** 15%
- **Segment 3 (Free users):** 35% (highest intent)
- **Segment 4 (Trial users):** 20%
- **Segment 5 (Churned):** 5%

**Total Retargeting Budget:** $100-500/day (scales with traffic)

---

## Targeting Specs

- **Pixel Events:** PageView, AddToCart, Purchase, TrialStart, TrialEnding
- **Frequency Cap:** Max 3 impressions per day per user
- **Placements:** Feed, Stories, Audience Network
- **Optimization:** Conversions (Purchase, Trial Start)

---

## Success Metrics

- **Segment 1:** 15%+ sign-up conversion rate
- **Segment 2:** 25%+ photo upload completion
- **Segment 3:** 8-12% upgrade to paid
- **Segment 4:** <10% churn rate
- **Segment 5:** 5-8% reactivation rate

**Overall Retargeting ROAS:** >4:1

---

## Creative Testing

Test variations of:
- Urgency (timer vs. no timer)
- Discount depth (50% vs. 25% vs. no discount)
- Social proof (testimonials vs. user count)
- Visual style (lifestyle vs. app screenshots)

Run A/B tests for 7 days before scaling winners.
