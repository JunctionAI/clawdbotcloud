## 🎨 Design Philosophy

**What Makes This a "$10,000 Landing Page"**:

1. **Color Theory**: Deep navy-black (#0a0a0f) instead of pure black creates OLED-friendly, premium feel
2. **Layering**: Glassmorphism creates physical depth perception
3. **Motion**: Subtle floating animations add life without being distracting
4. **Contrast**: White text on dark background with purple accents for visual hierarchy
5. **Spacing**: Generous padding and margins for breathing room
6. **Typography**: Heavy font weights (800) create authority
7. **Micro-interactions**: Hover effects on buttons and cards reward exploration
8. **Consistency**: Unified border radius (24px) and spacing system
