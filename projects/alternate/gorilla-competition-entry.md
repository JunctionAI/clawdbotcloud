## CONCEPT

**Logline:** A lone silverback gorilla stands in the centre of an industrial wasteland. 100 soldiers close in from all sides. One by one, they fall.

**Tone:** Epic. Visceral. Almost reverent of the gorilla. The men are a wave — the gorilla is a force of nature. Think *300* meets *King Kong* meets *Planet of the Apes*.

**Why this wins:**
- Instantly viral concept ("did you just see 100 guys get wrecked by a gorilla?")
- Emotional — you root for the gorilla
- Technically impressive — crowd simulation + creature + action in AI
- Unique — nobody else has attempted this scale

## SHOT BREAKDOWN (12 shots, ~60-90 seconds)

### SHOT 1 — ESTABLISH (5 sec)
**Prompt:**
```
Cinematic aerial drone shot, dawn light, vast industrial wasteland, 100 soldiers forming a perfect circle around a single massive silverback gorilla in the center, dust rising, scale overwhelming, ultra-wide lens, IMAX quality, muted earth tones with golden sunrise rim light
```
**Purpose:** Show the scale. The gorilla is tiny — surrounded. But you know.

---

### SHOT 2 — GORILLA CLOSE-UP (4 sec)
**Prompt:**
```
Extreme close-up of a massive silverback gorilla's eyes, calm, intelligent, ancient. Low angle looking up. Morning light. Breathing visible. Scarred face. Not afraid. Still. Photorealistic, cinematic depth of field, desaturated tones
```
**Purpose:** Establish the gorilla as the protagonist. Give him soul.

---

### SHOT 3 — SOLDIERS ADVANCE (4 sec)
**Prompt:**
```
Ground-level tracking shot, 100 armoured soldiers marching inward in formation, boots crunching gravel, tactical gear, helmets, rifles raised, industrial backdrop, dust clouds, dramatic tension, golden hour back-lighting, wide shot slowly pushing in
```
**Purpose:** Build dread. The wave is coming.

---

### SHOT 4 — FIRST CONTACT (5 sec)
**Prompt:**
```
The gorilla charges. Explosive movement. A soldier is launched 15 feet through the air, tactical gear scattering. Slow-motion impact. Dust eruption. The gorilla is a blur of muscle and fury. Cinema 4K, motion blur on hands, sharp on face, dramatic sound implied by visual
```
**Purpose:** Payoff begins. First explosion of violence.

### SHOT 5 — CHAOS MONTAGE (8 sec)
**Prompt sequence (3 cuts):**
```
1. Soldiers firing rifles, gorilla moving through them like water, rounds barely grazing, tactical chaos
2. Bird's eye view: gorilla clearing a 20-foot radius of soldiers with one sweeping arm, domino effect
3. Close-up: gorilla's hand slamming two soldiers together, cinematic impact frame
```
**Purpose:** Escalation. Show the impossible becoming possible.

---

### SHOT 6 — THE TIDE TURNS (5 sec)
**Prompt:**
```
Wide shot: half the soldiers are down. Remaining 50 hesitate. Some begin backing away. The gorilla stands tall, chest heaving, surrounded by fallen soldiers, roars into the dust-filled sky. Slow motion. Backlit. Epic. God-like framing from below.
```
**Purpose:** The turn. The gorilla is winning. Emotional peak.

Extreme low-angle close-up, gorilla opens mouth and roars, the shockwave visibly ripples the air, teeth, power, fury, slow motion dust explosion around his head, cinematic lighting, warm tones

The final 20 soldiers charge desperately, sheer numbers, chaotic formation, some on vehicles, some on foot, the gorilla braces. Wide angle from the gorilla's perspective looking at the incoming wave. Ultra-cinematic.
