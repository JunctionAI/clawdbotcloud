## FORMAT RULES

1. **Raw > polished.** No fancy transitions. iPhone quality is fine.
2. **Show the actual AI.** Screen record the Claude conversations — that's the differentiator.
3. **Real numbers.** If it costs $39/mo, say $39/mo. If it makes $0, say $0.
4. **Fast pacing.** 60-90 seconds max. Cut anything that's just filler.
5. **One idea per video.** Don't try to show everything. One lesson = one video.
