# Episode 4 Script — "Launch Day — Will Anyone Buy?"
## Build-in-Public Series | Day 22

*Created: Feb 18, 2026 | ALTERNATE Campaign Agent*

---

## FORMAT
- Length: 90-120 seconds (TikTok/Reels — longer allowed for drama/results)
- Visual: Reaction-style talking to camera + real-time phone screen
- Tone: Unfiltered, nervous energy → genuine reaction, no acting

---

## SCRIPT

**[0:00-0:06] HOOK — TALKING TO CAMERA**
> "Day 22. The store is live. I haven't told anyone yet."
> *[phone screen visible — Shopify app, $0 revenue]*
> "This is what zero looks like."

---

**[0:06-0:20] THE CONTEXT**
> "I've been building this for 22 days. An AI-powered merch store for a sci-fi film I co-created. No agency. No team. Just me and my AI agent."

> *[quick flash: previous episode montage — 2-3 second cuts]*
> "Last episode I had no product. No marketing. No launch plan."

> *[back to camera]*
> "Now I do. And in 10 minutes, I'm posting the launch."

---

**[0:20-0:50] THE LAUNCH MOMENT (real-time)**
> *[screen recording: posting to Instagram/TikTok — shows caption, hashtags]*
> "Okay. Posting now."

> *[30 seconds of nothing — sits there, watching phone]*
> "This is the worst part."

> *[notification sound]*
> "Someone saved it."

> *[another ping]*
> "Someone clicked the link."

> *[phone screen: Shopify — 1 visitor]*
> "There's one person on the store right now. Right now."

---

**[0:50-1:10] THE RESULT (whatever actually happens)**

*[Version A — First sale]*
> *[Shopify notification: order #1001]*
> "Oh. Oh wait."
> *[hold phone to camera — shows first order]*
> "Someone bought a t-shirt. Day 22. First sale."
> *[genuine pause — doesn't fake hype]*
> "That's a real person who found this, decided they wanted it, and bought it."
> "I don't know who you are. But thank you."

*[Version B — No sale yet, but traffic]*
> "14 visitors. 0 sales."
> *[not devastated — matter of fact]*
> "That's actually useful data. People are clicking. They're not converting. So something's wrong with the page — or the price, or the product."
> "I know what I'm fixing tomorrow."

---

**[1:10-1:25] THE HONEST TAKE**
> "Here's what I'll tell you after Day 1:"

> "Building the store with AI? Easy. That part worked."
> "Making strangers care? That's the actual job."
> "It's always been the actual job."

> *[beat]*
> "The AI didn't make marketing easy. It just made the execution fast enough that I could actually get to the hard part."

---

**[1:25-1:35] SETUP FOR FINALE**
> "Day 30 is the real test. I'll show you the full numbers — revenue, ad spend, what worked, what bombed."

> "Follow if you want to see."

> *[ALTERNATE film logo]*

## B-ROLL / VISUAL NOTES

- **Hook:** Shopify app on phone — real, not mocked up. Zero revenue = vulnerability = trust
- **Launch moment:** Actual screen recording of posting — authenticity is everything here
- **The wait:** Real silence. Don't cut it. The awkwardness is the content.
- **First sale / Version A:** Don't scream. Don't celebrate. Just... be real. That's the content.
- **Version B:** Also don't spiral. Data-mode is interesting.
- **End card:** ALTERNATE logo + "Day 30: Full results" teaser text

## PRODUCTION NOTE — TWO VERSIONS

Film **both endings** on launch day:
- Version A (first sale) → use if sale comes in Day 1
- Version B (no sale) → use if still zero at end of Day 1

The honest no-sale version may actually perform better — more relatable, more trust-building.

## WHY THIS EPISODE WORKS

- **Stakes are clear:** 22 days of build → this moment is the payoff
- **Real-time tension:** Watching phone with viewer = shared experience
- **Authenticity:** Not a "look at my win" — it's "here's what actually happened"
- **Hooks forward:** Day 30 full numbers → pulls to finale
- **The honest take:** Reframes AI honestly (fast execution, not magic) = credibility
