# Episode 3 Script — "Setting Up Automated Marketing in 2 Hours"
## Build-in-Public Series | Day 15

*Created: Feb 18, 2026 | ALTERNATE Campaign Agent*

---

## FORMAT
- Length: 75-90 seconds (TikTok/Reels optimised)
- Visual: Split between talking-to-camera + screen recording AI workflow
- Tone: Matter-of-fact, slightly mind-blown, not hype

---

## SCRIPT

**[0:00-0:05] HOOK — TALKING TO CAMERA**
> "Day 15. I have no marketing team. But I do have this—"
> *[cut to screen: AI agent running automation setup]*

---

**[0:05-0:20] THE PROBLEM (relatable setup)**
> "Most people spend months building out their email flows, their abandoned cart sequences, their welcome series. Hire someone. Pay $2k. Wait 6 weeks."

> *[text overlay: "OR..."]*

---

**[0:20-0:45] THE REVEAL (screen recording)**
> *[screen recording: Claude prompt visible]*
> "I gave my AI agent one job. Write me a 5-email welcome sequence, an abandoned cart flow, and a post-purchase upsell series. For a sci-fi merch store. Niche: people who think AI will change the world."

> *[fast scroll through AI output — email subjects visible]*
> "Done. In 8 minutes."

---

**[0:45-1:05] REAL TALK**
> "Now — is it perfect? No. I tweaked maybe 20% of it. But the 80% that I would have stared at a blank screen for? Done."

> *[back to camera]*
> "And it's actually good. Like... actually good."

> *[hold up phone showing one of the emails]*
> "This subject line? I wouldn't have written that. It's better than what I would've done."

---

**[1:05-1:20] THE NUMBER**
> "Total time to set up the entire marketing automation for this store: 2 hours, 14 minutes."

> "That includes uploading to Klaviyo, setting the triggers, testing it."

> *[text overlay: "2 hrs 14 min. No agency. No freelancer. No $2k invoice."]*

---

**[1:20-1:30] CTA**
> "Day 22 is launch day. Follow if you want to see if anyone actually buys."

> *[ALTERNATE logo flash at end]*

---

## B-ROLL / VISUAL NOTES

- **Hook cut:** Jarring — mid-sentence cut to screen, creates pattern interrupt
- **Screen recording:** Show actual Claude conversation (don't narrate it — let them read it)
- **Email scroll:** Fast, just subjects + first line visible — enough to believe it's real
- **Hold-up shot:** Real phone with email open — authenticity signal
- **End card:** ALTERNATE film logo + store URL when live

## WHY THIS EPISODE WORKS

- **Credibility:** Specific numbers (2 hrs 14 min, 5 emails, 8 minutes for output) = believable
- **Relatability:** Everyone has felt the "staring at blank screen" problem
- **FOMO:** "It's actually good" — curiosity about the output quality
- **Stakes:** Episode ends on "will anyone buy?" — pulls to Ep 4
