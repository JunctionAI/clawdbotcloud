## THE BRAND IN ONE SENTENCE

ALTERNATE is an AI-native creative studio making films, building community, and turning film concepts into real products — the first proof that AI can generate a full entertainment brand from scratch.

---

## THREE PILLARS

### 1. 🎬 Films & Video Production
- AI-generated short films (Higgsfield, KLING, Runway)
- Film competitions (gorilla comp = first output)
- Long-term: feature-length AI cinema
- **Content driver** — generates attention, press, community

### 2. 🎉 Events & Community
- IRL events around AI creativity
- Online community (Discord/Telegram)
- Workshops: "Make Your First AI Film"
- Collabs with other AI creatives
- **Community driver** — loyalty, word-of-mouth, repeat buyers

### 3. 🛍️ Products & Merchandise
- Physical products from film universe (props, apparel, collectibles)
- Print-on-demand (Printful + Shopify)
- Limited editions tied to film releases
- Digital products: presets, prompts, film templates
- **Revenue driver** — turns attention into money

---

## BUSINESS MODEL

```
Films → Attention → Community → Products → Revenue
              ↓
       Junction Media documents everything
              ↓
       Junction's marketing content = proof of concept
```

**Why this works:**
- Films are free to make (AI tools)
- Attention is organic (social sharing)
- Community is free to build (Discord/Telegram)
- Products have margin (Printful ~30-50% margin)
- Junction gets case study content while building it
