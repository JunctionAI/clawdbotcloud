# ALTERNATE — Logo & Brand Visual Brief

*Created: Feb 18, 2026 | ALTERNATE Campaign Agent*
*Purpose: Ready for AI generation (Midjourney/DALL-E) when store setup begins*

---

## BRAND ESSENCE

ALTERNATE is a sci-fi short film made entirely with AI. The brand should feel:
- **Technological** — but not cold
- **Cinematic** — widescreen, high contrast
- **Glitch aesthetic** — reality breaking down at the edges
- **Underground cool** — not corporate, not try-hard

**The feeling:** You've just discovered something that shouldn't exist yet.

---

## LOGO OPTIONS (3 directions to generate)

### Direction A: Wordmark + Glitch
- Clean sans-serif letterforms (Neue Haas, or similar)
- "ALTERNATE" in all caps, letter-spaced
- Subtle glitch effect on 1-2 letters (offset pixel, RGB split)
- Colourway: White on black, OR electric blue (#00D4FF) on near-black
- Optional: thin horizontal scanline behind text (CRT reference)

**Midjourney prompt:**
> `minimalist wordmark logo, "ALTERNATE", clean sans serif, letter spacing, subtle rgb glitch effect on letters, sci-fi film, dark background, electric blue accent, scanline texture, cinematic, underground cool, high contrast --ar 3:1`

---

### Direction B: Symbol Mark (Icon only)
- Abstract symbol that suggests a portal, parallel universe, or signal breach
- Works as favicon / sticker / embossed on merch
- Options: two overlapping circles (parallel worlds), broken circle, infinity loop with gap
- Monochrome first, colour variant second

**Midjourney prompt:**
> `minimalist abstract logo mark, parallel universe portal concept, two overlapping circles breaking apart, sci-fi aesthetic, clean geometric, black and white, icon design, works at small size, no text --ar 1:1`

---

### Direction C: Stacked Wordmark (Square format)
- "ALTERNATE" stacked vertically OR with "FILM" below in smaller text
- Works on merch (embroidery, screen print)
- More structured/geometric than Direction A
- Slight distortion effect at top or bottom of text block

**Midjourney prompt:**
> `stacked wordmark logo design, "ALTERNATE" text, clean bold sans serif, sci-fi film branding, slight warp distortion at edges, high contrast black and white, premium merch ready, minimal, cinematic --ar 1:1`

## TYPOGRAPHY

**Primary (Display):** Space Grotesk Bold / Neue Haas Grotesk Display
- Used for headlines, film title, hero text
- Letter-spaced at 0.05-0.1em
- Always uppercase

**Secondary (Body):** Inter / DM Sans
- Used for product descriptions, UI text
- Regular weight, good legibility

**Accent (Code/Data):** JetBrains Mono
- Used for technical details, behind-the-scenes data
- Creates "system" aesthetic

## MERCH APPLICATIONS

### T-Shirt (front)
- Direction A logo centred on chest, 10cm wide
- White on black preferred
- Alternative: black on off-white for premium feel

### Hoodie
- Direction B symbol (icon only) on chest, small (~5cm)
- "ALTERNATE" text on back, large across shoulders

### Sticker Pack (5-piece set)
1. Full Direction A logo horizontal
2. Direction B icon only
3. "REALITY IS JUST A GLITCH" text art
4. Film still (best frame) with ALTERNATE watermark
5. "Made with AI" badge (for meta appeal)

### Poster (A2/A3)
- Hero frame from film
- "ALTERNATE" as film title treatment
- Tagline: "Reality is just a glitch"
- Release year: 2026
- Subtle: "Entirely AI-Generated" credit line at bottom

---

## WHAT TO AVOID

❌ Generic sci-fi (planets, spaceships, stars)
❌ Purple/blue gradient = too "AI startup"
❌ Over-designed = should feel like someone found it in 2040
❌ Comic Sans, bubble letters, anything ironic
❌ Too many colours — restraint is prestige

---

## NOTES FOR GENERATION

- Generate 4-6 variations per direction
- Export: SVG preferred, PNG fallback
- Test at: 16px (favicon), 32px (icon), 300px (web), print-ready (vector)
- Priority order: Direction A first → Direction B → Direction C
