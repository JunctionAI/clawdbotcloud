## Introduction

Welcome to **Style Swap** - your AI-powered virtual fashion try-on assistant! 

Style Swap allows you to:
- 👔 Try on clothing virtually before buying
- 🎨 Experiment with different styles
- 📸 See how outfits look on your body type
- 💡 Get AI-powered style recommendations

No physical changing rooms needed - just upload a photo and start exploring!

## Getting Started

### System Requirements

- **Device**: Desktop, tablet, or mobile phone
- **Browser**: 
  - Chrome 90+ (recommended)

1. Open your web browser
2. Navigate to: `https://style-swap.vercel.app` (or your deployed URL)
3. No login required - start immediately!

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### 📸 Step 1: Upload Your Photo

**Option A: Drag & Drop**
1. Take or locate a clear front-facing photo of yourself
2. Drag the image file into the upload zone
3. Release to upload

**Option B: Click to Select**
1. Click anywhere in the upload zone
2. Browse your device's files
3. Select your photo
4. Click "Open"

**What happens next:**
- Your photo appears in the preview area
- The clothing catalog becomes active
- You're ready to browse!

![Upload Zone](assets/screenshot-upload.png)

### 👕 Step 2: Browse the Catalog

1. **Explore Categories**: Click category tabs to filter items
   - Tops (shirts, t-shirts, blouses)
   - Bottoms (pants, shorts, skirts)
   - Dresses (casual, formal)
   - Outerwear (jackets, coats)
   - Accessories (hats, scarves)

2. **View Item Details**: Hover over items to see:
   - Item name
   - Description
   - Style tags

3. **Search**: Use the search bar (if available) to find specific styles

![Catalog](assets/screenshot-catalog.png)

### 🤖 Step 3: Try On Clothing

1. Click on any clothing item that interests you
2. Wait for the AI to process (typically 3-8 seconds)
3. Loading indicator appears with status messages

**Processing Steps:**
```
1. Analyzing your photo...
2. Applying clothing style...
3. Generating visualization...
4. Complete! ✓
```

![Processing](assets/screenshot-processing.png)

### 🔍 Step 4: Compare Results

Once processing completes:

1. **Interactive Slider Appears**
   - Left side: Your original photo
   - Right side: AI-generated try-on
   
2. **Drag the Slider**
   - Move left to see original
   - Move right to see try-on result
   - Compare seamlessly

3. **View AI Analysis**
   - Style recommendations appear below
   - Color matching insights
   - Fit suggestions

![Comparison](assets/screenshot-comparison.png)

### 💾 Step 5: Save or Share (Optional)

**Download Your Result:**
1. Right-click on the result image
2. Select "Save Image As..."
3. Choose location and filename
4. Click "Save"

**Try Another Item:**
1. Click "Try Another Style" button
2. Returns to catalog view
3. Select a new item to try

## Tips for Best Results

### 📸 Photography Tips

**Lighting:**
- ✅ Natural daylight (near window)
- ✅ Evenly lit, no harsh shadows
- ❌ Avoid direct sunlight
- ❌ No backlighting

**Background:**
- ✅ Plain, solid colors (white, light gray)
- ✅ Uncluttered
- ❌ Busy patterns
- ❌ Similar colors to your outfit

**Pose & Positioning:**
- ✅ Stand straight, arms slightly away from body
- ✅ Face camera directly (front view)
- ✅ Full upper body visible
- ❌ Side angles or turned poses
- ❌ Sitting or lying down

**Clothing:**
- ✅ Form-fitting base layer
- ✅ Solid colors work best
- ❌ Extremely baggy clothes
- ❌ Busy patterns (may confuse AI)

### 🎯 Selection Tips

**Best Item Choices:**
- Start with tops (easiest to visualize)
- Try similar styles first
- Experiment with colors
- Progress to full outfits

**AI Works Best With:**
- Classic clothing styles
- Solid color items
- Clear garment boundaries
- Standard fits (not oversized)

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### ❌ "Upload Failed"
**Possible Causes:**
- File too large (> 10 MB)
- Unsupported file type
- Network connection issue

**Solutions:**
1. Compress image (use online tools)
2. Convert to JPEG format
3. Check internet connection
4. Refresh page and try again

#### ❌ "Processing Timeout"
**Possible Causes:**
- Server overload
- Poor internet connection
- Image complexity too high

**Solutions:**
1. Wait 30 seconds, try again
2. Use simpler background photo
3. Try during off-peak hours
4. Refresh browser

#### ❌ Poor Try-On Quality
**Possible Causes:**
- Low-quality input photo
- Poor lighting/background
- Unusual pose or angle

**Solutions:**
1. Retake photo with better lighting
2. Use plain background
3. Stand in standard front-facing pose
4. Upload higher resolution image

#### ❌ Catalog Not Loading
**Possible Causes:**
- JavaScript disabled
- Ad blocker interference
- Browser cache issue

**Solutions:**
1. Enable JavaScript
2. Disable ad blockers for site
3. Clear browser cache
4. Try incognito/private mode

### Browser-Specific Issues

**Safari (iOS/Mac):**
- If upload fails, allow camera/photo access in Settings
- Use "Open in Safari" if using in-app browser

**Chrome (Android):**
- Grant storage permissions when prompted
- Update to latest Chrome version

**Firefox:**
- Check privacy settings aren't blocking uploads
- Allow site to use device storage

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Is Style Swap free to use?**  
A: Yes! The core try-on feature is completely free.

**Q: Do I need to create an account?**  
A: No account required. Upload and try-on immediately.

**Q: Where are my photos stored?**  
A: Photos are processed in real-time and NOT stored on our servers. Your privacy is protected.

**Q: Can I try multiple items at once?**  
A: Currently, one item at a time. Multi-item try-on coming soon!

### Technical Questions

**Q: How accurate is the AI?**  
A: The AI provides a realistic visualization but may not be 100% perfect. Results depend on photo quality and lighting.

**Q: What AI model powers this?**  
A: We use Google's Gemini 2.5 Flash for intelligent style analysis and visualization.

**Q: Can I use this on mobile?**  
A: Absolutely! Style Swap is fully mobile-optimized.

**Q: How long does processing take?**  
A: Typically 3-8 seconds, depending on server load and image complexity.

### Privacy & Security

**Q: Is my data safe?**  
A: Yes. All processing happens via secure API calls. Images are not stored permanently.

**Q: Can others see my try-ons?**  
A: No. All sessions are private and temporary.

**Q: Do you use my photos for training?**  
A: No. We do not retain or use your images for any purpose beyond your current session.

### Shopping & Commerce

**Q: Can I buy items directly?**  
A: Not yet! This is coming in future updates with e-commerce integration.

**Q: Where can I find the real clothing?**  
A: Item descriptions include brand/style info when available. Check linked retailers.

**Q: Are sizes accurate?**  
A: The visualization shows general fit. Always check sizing charts before purchasing.
