# Junction Service Capability Matrix

*Based on Tom's DBH delivery experience*

## Service Breakdown

| Service | AI Capability | Human Needed | Notes |
|---------|--------------|--------------|-------|
| **Video Production** | Higgsfield prompts, scripting | External agency for shoots | Building out with AI video gen |
| **Influencer Management** | Hire, organize shoots, outreach | Relationship layer | Agents can do most |
| **Retailer Listings** | ✅ Full AI | - | International management |
| **DLE Production** | AI briefs designers | Designer executes | AI handles creative direction |
| **Website Development** | AI briefs, some coding | Dev still needed (for now) | Will improve as agents get better |
| **SEO/GEO/AEO** | ✅ Full AI | - | Doing in co-work now |
| **Shopify Updates** | Mostly AI | Quick fixes faster with human | Minor account manager tasks |
| **Google Ads** | ✅ Full AI | - | Management + optimization |
| **Strategy** | ✅ Full AI | - | Research, planning, roadmaps |
| **Meta Ads** | AI manages campaigns | Graphic design briefs | Creative is the bottleneck |
| **Branding** | AI assists | Designer needed | Strategy AI, execution human |
| **Email & Loyalty** | ✅ Full AI | Custom graphics need designer | Platform management, flows |

## Key Insight

**AI can BRIEF humans even when it can't execute.**

The bottleneck is almost always **graphic design**. Everything else is either:
1. Full AI capability
2. AI briefs → Human executes

## Service Tiers

### Tier 1: Full AI (No human execution needed)
- SEO/GEO/AEO
- Google Ads
- Strategy
- Email & Loyalty (standard)
- Retailer listings

### Tier 2: AI + Brief (Human executes from AI brief)
- Meta Ads (graphic design)
- DLE production
- Branding
- Video (shoot coordination)
- Website development

### Tier 3: Human-Heavy (AI assists only)
- Complex website builds
- Custom branding projects
- High-end video production

**Current DBH approach:** "Ongoing improvement, employee model" — loose, continuous delivery rather than fixed deliverables.
