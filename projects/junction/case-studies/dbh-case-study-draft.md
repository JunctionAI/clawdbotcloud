# Deep Blue Health Case Study (DRAFT)

**Client:** Deep Blue Health  
**Industry:** Natural Health Supplements  
**Location:** Mairangi Bay, Auckland, NZ  
**Partnership Duration:** Ongoing (2025-Present)

---

## The Challenge

Deep Blue Health (DBH) is a New Zealand-based natural supplement company with a strong product line — including their flagship Deer Blood Iron supplement and Ginkgo Biloba range. Despite quality products and excellent customer reviews, they faced challenges:

- **Untapped Vietnamese diaspora market** in NZ and Australia
- **Limited digital marketing expertise** in-house
- **Content production bottlenecks** — traditional video production was slow and expensive
- **Seasonal sales volatility** requiring more consistent marketing presence

## The Solution

Junction Media partnered with DBH to implement an AI-augmented marketing approach:

### 1. AI-Powered Video Content Pipeline
- Developed scripts optimized for Vietnamese cultural preferences
- Created talking-head explainer videos at 10x the speed of traditional production
- Built a library of product education content

### 2. Culturally Targeted Messaging
- Research into Vietnamese health philosophy ("Bổ máu" = blood nourishment)
- Positioning around NZ origin as quality/trust signal
- Family gifting culture integration (products as gifts for parents/elders)

### 3. Social Media Strategy
- Facebook-first approach (primary platform for Vietnamese diaspora)
- Testimonial-style content that builds word-of-mouth trust
- Consistent posting schedule vs. sporadic campaigns

### 4. Influencer Partnerships
- Connected DBH with Vietnamese health influencers
- Managed filming days and content production
- Provided detailed product talking points and scripts

## The Approach

**Week 1-2:** Deep research into Vietnamese health beliefs, competitor analysis, DBH product differentiators

**Week 3-4:** Content strategy development, script writing, video production pilot

**Ongoing:** Regular content production, campaign optimization, influencer coordination

## Results

**Qualitative Wins:**
- ✅ Consistent content production pipeline established
- ✅ Vietnamese market messaging framework created
- ✅ Multiple filming days coordinated with influencers
- ✅ Product education content library built
- ✅ Sales team equipped with culturally-relevant talking points

**Performance Indicators:**

## Key Learnings

1. **Cultural context matters** — Generic health marketing doesn't resonate with specific communities
2. **Speed compounds** — AI-assisted production means more content, more testing, more learning
3. **Trust through education** — Vietnamese customers want to understand WHY a product works, not just what it does
4. **NZ origin = premium positioning** — "Made in New Zealand" carries significant trust in Asian markets
