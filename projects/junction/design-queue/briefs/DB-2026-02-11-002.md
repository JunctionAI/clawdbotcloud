## 🎯 Project Overview

### What is this for?
Visual graphics for a new website section showing the exponential AI capability curve and positioning Junction as the partner to ride this wave.

### Context
Junction's website needs a section that visually communicates the exponential growth of AI capabilities and what it means for businesses. The message: AI capability is going vertical — you can either be disrupted or partner with someone who's riding the wave. Junction is that partner.

This section should create urgency without being alarmist, and position Junction as the sophisticated choice for forward-thinking business owners.

## 🎨 Creative Direction

### Visual Concept
**The Exponential Curve:**
- A classic exponential growth curve (hockey stick shape)
- Past is relatively flat, recent years curve sharply upward
- Future projection continues the steep climb
- Use gradient color transition from blue → purple → pink as it goes up

**Timeline Integration:**
- X-axis represents time (2020 → 2026+)
- Y-axis represents AI capability (abstract, not specific metrics)
- Key milestones marked with subtle dots and labels

**Interactive/Animation Ideas (optional):**
- Curve draws itself as user scrolls
- Milestone dots pulse when in view
- "You are here" marker animates in
- Gradient shimmers subtly

### Mood/Tone
- **Awe-inspiring but grounded** — Not sci-fi hyperbole
- **Urgent but not fear-based** — Opportunity, not threat
- **Data-informed feel** — Looks authoritative, like a real insight

### Reference Inspiration
1. Kurzweil's singularity charts (the exponential visual language)
2. Apple keynote data visualizations (clean, impactful)
3. Stripe's animated SVG graphics (sophisticated motion)

### What to Avoid
- ❌ Cheesy "to the moon" rocket imagery
- ❌ Overly complex charts with too much data
- ❌ Scary/dystopian AI imagery
- ❌ Generic "growth" stock graphics
- ❌ Cluttered composition
