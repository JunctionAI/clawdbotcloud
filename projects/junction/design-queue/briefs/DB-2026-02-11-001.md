## 🎨 Creative Direction

### Visual Style
- **Modern and premium** — Not startup-y or cheap
- **Dark mode aesthetic** — Midnight background with gradient accents
- **Geometric/abstract** — Subtle shapes, not literal AI imagery
- **Professional** — This is B2B, not consumer flashy

### Mood/Tone
Viewers should feel: *"This is the premium option. These people know what they're doing."*

### Design Approach
- Use subtle geometric patterns or mesh gradients in brand colors
- Headlines should be prominent and readable
- Leave breathing room — don't crowd the composition
- Each post should be distinct but clearly part of a series

### Reference Inspiration
1. Vercel's blog headers (clean, dark, typographic)
2. Linear.app aesthetic (subtle gradients, premium feel)
3. Stripe's content imagery (refined, not flashy)

### What to Avoid
- ❌ Stock photo clichés
- ❌ Blue glowing brains / robot hands / AI clichés
- ❌ Busy or cluttered compositions
- ❌ Clip art or generic icons
- ❌ Overly bright or garish colors
