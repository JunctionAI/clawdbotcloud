# Junction Media AI Influencer Content Strategy

**Mission:** Position Junction Media as THE agency for businesses who want speed, innovation, and results through AI-powered development.

**Target Clients:** Businesses ready to invest $10k-50k+/month on digital presence (e-commerce, SaaS, professional services)

---

## Core Messaging Pillars

### 1. **Speed Advantage**
- "What takes agencies weeks, we do in days"
- AI-powered development = faster time-to-market
- Show the clock, make speed tangible

### 2. **OpenCore Philosophy**
- Transparency about AI-assisted development
- Not replacing quality—enhancing it
- Human creativity + AI execution = best of both

### 3. **Results Over Process**
- Clients pay for outcomes, not hours
- Show ROI, conversions, business impact
- De-mystify the "agency experience"

### 4. **Future-Proof Partnership**
- Clients working with Junction are ahead of the curve
- AI literacy transfer—we teach as we build
- Long-term partnership, not one-off projects

## Platform Strategy

### LinkedIn (Primary B2B Channel)
**Posting:** 5x/week
**Format:** Text posts, carousels, short videos (<90 sec)
**Tone:** Professional but human, thought leadership

**Content Mix:**
- 40% Educational (AI + business insights)
- 30% Case studies & results
- 20% Behind-the-scenes / process
- 10% Personal stories / opinions

### Twitter/X (Tech Credibility)
**Posting:** Daily threads, quick updates
**Format:** Threads, screenshots, quick tips
**Tone:** Technical but accessible, meme-aware

**Content Mix:**
- 50% Tech insights & AI development tips
- 30% Quick wins & tutorials
- 20% Hot takes & engagement

### TikTok (Viral Discovery)
**Posting:** 3-5x/week
**Format:** 30-60 sec vertical videos
**Tone:** Energetic, pattern-interrupt, entertaining

**Content Mix:**
- 60% "Wow" moments (speed demos, transformations)
- 30% Quick tips & hacks
- 10% Trending formats adapted to agency life

### YouTube (Deep Authority)
**Posting:** 1-2x/week
**Format:** 5-15 min case studies, tutorials
**Tone:** Detailed, educational, high production value

**Content Mix:**
- 50% Full case study breakdowns
- 30% Tutorial/educational
- 20% Vlogs & behind-the-scenes

## AI Influencer Execution Notes

### For Higgsfield/AI Avatar Content
- **Keep scripts tight:** 30-60 seconds max for AI-generated video
- **Visual consistency:** Same "Tom" avatar, same setting/background
- **Voice:** Confident, knowledgeable, slightly casual
- **Expressions:** Natural gestures, avoid robotic delivery

### Mixing Real + AI Content
- Use AI Tom for:
  - Quick tips and hooks
  - Consistent daily posting
  - A/B testing different scripts
- Use Real Tom for:
  - Live Q&As
  - Client testimonials
  - High-stakes thought leadership

### Content Repurposing Flow
```
YouTube Long-form (10 min)
    ↓
Cut into 3-5 TikToks/Reels
    ↓
Extract quotes for Twitter threads
    ↓
Rewrite key points as LinkedIn posts
    ↓
AI avatar delivers condensed version
```
