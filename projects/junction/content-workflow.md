# Junction Content Workflow

## The Voice Note → Content Pipeline

### How It Works

1. **Agent asks specific questions** (not vague ideas)
2. **Tom records voice note** when he has time
3. **Agent transcribes + turns into script**
4. **Agent produces in Higgsfield** using @Tom Influencer
5. **Content ships** without Tom touching production

### Higgsfield Setup
- **Character:** @Tom Influencer
- **Use for:** Agency content, thought leadership, case studies

## Standup Protocol

When Junction team does standups:
1. Review what content has shipped
2. Identify content gaps
3. Generate 2-3 specific questions for Tom
4. Post questions to Discord
5. When Tom voice notes back → produce immediately

**Rule:** Never give Tom vague ideas. Give him specific questions he can answer in 60 seconds.
