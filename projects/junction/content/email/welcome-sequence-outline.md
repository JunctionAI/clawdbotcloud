# Junction Media — Email Welcome Sequence

**Purpose:** Convert new subscribers into engaged prospects ready to book a call  
**Trigger:** New email signup (lead magnet download or newsletter join)  
**Sequence:** 5 emails over 10 days

*Status: OUTLINE — Ready for full draft*

---

## Email 1: Welcome + Immediate Value
**Send:** Immediately after signup  
**Subject:** Welcome to Junction (+ what makes us different)  
**Goal:** Set expectations, deliver promised content, establish authority

**Structure:**
1. Thank them for joining
2. Deliver the lead magnet/promised resource
3. Quick intro: Who we are, why AI-powered marketing matters
4. What to expect from this list (frequency, content type)
5. One quick win tip they can use today
6. CTA: Reply and tell us your biggest marketing challenge

**Tone:** Warm, professional, value-first

---

## Email 2: The Problem We Solve
**Send:** Day 2  
**Subject:** The marketing agency model is broken (here's why)  
**Goal:** Agitate pain points, position Junction as the solution

**Structure:**
1. Hook: Common frustration with agencies
2. The old model problems:
   - Slow turnarounds
   - Hourly billing incentivizes inefficiency
   - Black box processes
   - Results that don't match investment
3. The shift: AI changes everything
4. How we're different (brief, not salesy)
5. CTA: Read our latest blog post on [relevant topic]

**Tone:** Empathetic, slightly provocative

---

## Email 3: How We Work
**Send:** Day 4  
**Subject:** AI does the work. Humans do the thinking.  
**Goal:** Explain our methodology, build trust through transparency

**Structure:**
1. Our OpenCore philosophy explained
2. The AI + Human workflow:
   - AI: Speed, scale, consistency
   - Human: Strategy, creativity, judgment
3. What this means for clients:
   - Faster delivery
   - Lower costs (we pass savings on)
   - Better results (more time on strategy, less on grunt work)
4. Brief case study or example
5. CTA: See our services

**Tone:** Educational, confident

---

## Email 4: Social Proof / Case Study
**Send:** Day 7  
**Subject:** How [Client/We] got [Result] in [Timeframe]  
**Goal:** Demonstrate results, reduce skepticism

**Structure:**
1. Quick story of a win (use Junction as Client #001 initially)
2. The challenge
3. The approach
4. The results (specific numbers)
5. Key takeaway they can apply
6. CTA: Want similar results? Let's talk

**Tone:** Results-focused, specific

---

## Email 5: The Invitation
**Send:** Day 10  
**Subject:** Quick question for you  
**Goal:** Convert interested readers to booked calls

**Structure:**
1. Check-in: Have the emails been helpful?
2. Restate the core value proposition
3. Who we work best with (qualify the reader)
4. Clear CTA: Book a 15-minute discovery call
5. What happens on the call (no pitch, just fit assessment)
6. PS: Reply if you have questions

**Tone:** Direct, low-pressure, helpful
