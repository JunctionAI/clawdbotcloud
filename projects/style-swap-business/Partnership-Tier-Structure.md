# Style Swap Partnership Tier Structure

## Overview

Our tiered partnership model is designed to scale with your business needs while providing clear value at every level. Choose the tier that matches your current inventory volume and growth ambitions.

---

## 🥉 Bronze Tier
### **The Starter Partnership**

**Best For:**
- Brands testing the circular economy model
- Small to medium brands with moderate excess inventory
- Companies wanting to validate market demand
- Brands with <100 items/month available

### Commission Structure
- **70% Brand Partner / 30% Style Swap**
- Most favorable revenue split for partners
- Ideal for price-testing and market validation

### Features & Benefits

#### ✅ Core Features
- **Inventory Capacity:** Up to 100 items per month
- **Standard Marketplace Listing:** Professional photography and listing creation
- **Dashboard Access:** Real-time sales tracking and inventory management
- **Monthly Reports:** Detailed performance analytics
- **Email Support:** Response within 48 business hours
- **Payment Processing:** Monthly payouts, 15 days after month-end
- **Quality Control:** Professional intake and listing standards

#### 📢 Marketing Support
- Inclusion in general email campaigns (2-4x monthly)
- Organic social media mentions
- Standard search and category visibility
- Access to promotional calendar

#### 🛠️ Operational Support
- Style Swap handles all customer service
- Professional product photography (4-6 images per item)
- Returns and refunds management
- Shipping and fulfillment (if using Style Swap warehouse)

### Investment
- **No Upfront Fees**
- **No Monthly Fees**
- Commission-only model: We succeed when you succeed

### Ideal Scenarios
✓ "We have 50-80 returns each month we currently discount heavily"  
✓ "We want to test circular fashion without major commitment"  
✓ "We're a small brand building our sustainability story"  
✓ "We have samples and discontinued items taking up warehouse space"

## 💡 Tier Upgrade Path

### How to Upgrade

**Performance-Based Upgrades:**
- Achieve sell-through targets at your current tier
- Demonstrate consistent monthly inventory volume
- Request upgrade at any renewal period (30 days notice)

**Upgrade Benefits:**
- Retain your history and performance data
- Immediate access to new tier benefits
- No gap in service during transition

**Trial Periods:**
- Test higher tiers for 60 days before committing
- Evaluate ROI with enhanced features
- Downgrade if it doesn't meet expectations (no penalty)

### Typical Progression
```
Month 1-3:   Bronze → Test and validate
Month 4-9:   Silver → Scale and grow
Month 10+:   Gold → Strategic partnership
```

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I change tiers mid-contract?**  
A: After your initial term, you can upgrade/downgrade with 30 days notice. Upgrades can happen anytime during initial term.

**Q: What if I exceed my tier's inventory limit?**  
A: Bronze → Silver: We'll proactively recommend upgrading. You can temporarily exceed limits with account manager approval, or upgrade your tier.

**Q: Do I pay anything beyond the commission?**  
A: No. Zero upfront fees, no monthly fees, no hidden costs. Pure commission-only model.

**Q: How is the commission calculated?**  
A: On NET revenue after payment processing and any shipping subsidies. See our Revenue Calculator for details.

**Q: What's included in each tier's support?**  
A: Bronze: Email support. Silver: Email + phone + account manager. Gold: Priority hotline + dedicated team.

**Q: Can I negotiate a custom commission split?**  
A: Gold tier has flexibility for high-volume partners. Bronze and Silver splits are standard to ensure fairness.

**Q: What happens to my data if I downgrade or leave?**  
A: You retain all your historical data. We provide export options. Your products are delisted, but data is yours.
