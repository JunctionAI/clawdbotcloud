## 2. COMMISSION STRUCTURE

### 2.1 Revenue Share

**Selected Tier Commission:**
- ☐ Bronze: 70% Brand Partner / 30% Style Swap
- ☐ Silver: 65% Brand Partner / 35% Style Swap
- ☐ Gold: 60% Brand Partner / 40% Style Swap

### 2.2 Calculation Method
```
Gross Sale Price (customer pays)
- Payment Processing Fees (actual cost)
- Shipping Costs (if subsidized)
= Net Revenue

Net Revenue × Commission % = Partner Share
```

### 2.3 Example Transaction
- Item sells for: $100.00
- Payment processing (2.9% + $0.30): -$3.20
- Net Revenue: $96.80
- Silver Tier (65/35 split):
  - Brand Partner receives: $62.92
  - Style Swap receives: $33.88

### 2.4 Payment Terms
- **Payment Schedule:** Monthly, within 15 days of month-end
- **Minimum Payout:** $100 (balances under $100 roll to next month)
- **Payment Method:** Bank transfer / PayPal / [Other]
- **Currency:** [USD / EUR / GBP / NZD]

## 3. INVENTORY & PRODUCT LISTING

### 3.1 Eligible Inventory
Brand Partner may list the following product categories:
- ☐ Returns (in sellable condition)
- ☐ End-of-season excess inventory
- ☐ Samples
- ☐ Display items
- ☐ Discontinued lines
- ☐ Other: _________________

### 3.2 Quality Standards
All items must:
- Be authentic Brand Partner products
- Meet minimum quality standards (no damage, stains, or defects unless disclosed)
- Include accurate descriptions and sizing
- Comply with all applicable safety and labeling regulations

### 3.3 Pricing
- Brand Partner provides recommended pricing
- Style Swap has final pricing authority based on market analysis
- Price adjustments require mutual agreement for items >$500

### 3.4 Photography & Content
- Style Swap provides photography and listing creation services
- Brand Partner may provide approved marketing materials
- All listing content subject to Style Swap quality standards

### 3.5 Inventory Limits (Monthly)
- Bronze: Up to 100 items
- Silver: Up to 500 items
- Gold: Unlimited

## 4. FULFILLMENT & LOGISTICS

### 4.1 Inventory Storage
- ☐ **Style Swap Storage:** Brand ships inventory to Style Swap warehouse
- ☐ **Brand Storage:** Brand fulfills orders directly (Gold tier only)

### 4.2 Shipping Responsibilities
**If Style Swap Storage:**
- Style Swap handles all customer shipping
- Shipping costs deducted from gross revenue before commission split

**If Brand Storage:**
- Brand ships within 48 hours of order notification
- Brand covers shipping costs (factored into pricing)

### 4.3 Returns & Refunds
- Standard return window: 14 days
- Return shipping: Customer responsibility (unless item defect)
- Refund processing: Style Swap issues refund, no commission charged on returned items
- Returned inventory: Returned to available stock or shipped back to Brand
