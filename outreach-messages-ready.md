## BEST PRACTICES

### Personalization Tips
1. **Reference recent context** - Meeting, project, mutual connection
2. **Use their language** - Match tone (formal vs. casual)
3. **Specific pain points** - Don't generic ("busy"), get tactical ("email triage")
4. **Social proof** - "Last client in [THEIR INDUSTRY] saved X hours"

### Timing Strategy
- **WhatsApp:** Send 9am-11am or 6pm-8pm (commute times)
- **Email:** Send Tue-Thu, 8am-10am (highest open rates)
- **LinkedIn:** Send Mon-Wed, 10am-2pm

### Response Handling
- **Interested:** Book call immediately (send Calendly)
- **Maybe:** Send case study, follow up in 2 days
- **Not now:** Add to nurture, follow up in 2-4 weeks
- **Not interested:** Thank them, ask for referral
