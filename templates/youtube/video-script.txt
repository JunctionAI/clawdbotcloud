[HOOK - First 15 seconds]

This is CRITICAL. Viewer decides to stay or leave.

[Show the outcome/result visually]

OR

[Ask a compelling question]
"What if I told you [surprising fact about topic]?"

OR

[Show proof/transformation]
"This is what happened when I [did X for Y days]..."

[Quick preview of what's coming]

- [Thing 1]
- [Thing 2]
- [Thing 3]"

"So let's dive in."

---

[CONTENT - Main sections]

[SECTION 1 - Timestamp: 0:45]

[B-ROLL: Show relevant visuals]

[Main point 1]

[Example or demonstration]

[On-screen text: Key point]

"Now, here's where most people mess this up..."

[Common mistake and how to avoid it]

---

[PATTERN INTERRUPT - Timestamp: 4:00]

Every 3-4 minutes, shift the energy:
- Ask a question
- Show something unexpected
- Change camera angle
- Add humor
- Tease what's coming next

"Before we move on to [next thing], quick question..."

OR

"Now here's where it gets interesting..."

---

[SECTION 2 - Timestamp: 5:00]

[Continue pattern from Section 1]

[Engagement prompt]
"Drop a comment if you've experienced this too."

---

[MID-ROLL CTA - Timestamp: 7:00]

"By the way, if you're enjoying this, hit that like button and subscribe."

[Specific reason to subscribe]
"I post new [topic] videos every [schedule], and I've got some amazing content coming up on [teaser]."

---

[SECTION 3 - Continue content]

---

[RECAP/SUMMARY - Final 2 minutes]

"Alright, let's quickly recap what we covered today:"

- [Key point 1]
- [Key point 2]
- [Key point 3]

[Action steps]
"Here's what you should do next:"

1. [Immediate action]
2. [Follow-up action]

---

[OUTRO - Final 30 seconds]

"If you found this helpful, check out this video next where I [related topic]."

[Show end screen with video recommendation]

"Thanks for watching, and I'll see you in the next one!"

YOUTUBE BEST PRACTICES:

✅ DO:
- Hook in first 15 seconds
- Pattern interrupts every 3-4 min
- Ask viewers to like/subscribe (but give a reason)
- Reply to comments in first 24 hours
- Use end screens and cards
- Include timestamps in description
- Engage with community tab

❌ DON'T:
- Ramble or waste time at the start
- Forget to ask for engagement
- Make videos too long (under 15min unless deep-dive)
- Ignore analytics (watch time, CTR)

MONETIZATION:
- Ad revenue (enabled at 1k subs, 4k watch hours)
- Affiliate links in description
- Sponsorships (mention products naturally)
- Course/product promotion (soft sell)
