NEWSLETTER BEST PRACTICES:

✅ DO:
- Write like you're emailing a friend
- Keep paragraphs short (2-3 sentences)
- Use "you" not "I"
- Include actionable takeaways
- Reply to responses (builds relationship)
- Test subject lines (A/B test)
- Be consistent (same day, same time)
- Add personality and voice

❌ DON'T:
- Over-promote (80% value, 20% promotion)
- Use too many links (confuses reader)
- Make it too long (500-1000 words ideal)
- Forget mobile readers (most will read on phone)
- Ignore analytics (open rate, click rate)

MONETIZATION:
- Affiliate links (naturally integrated)
- Sponsorships (once you hit 1k+ subscribers)
- Paid tier (exclusive content for premium subs)
- Product launches (courses, ebooks, etc.)

GROWTH:
- Lead magnet (free guide, checklist, template)
- Content upgrades on blog posts
- Social media CTAs
- Collaborations with other newsletters
- Testimonials on landing page
