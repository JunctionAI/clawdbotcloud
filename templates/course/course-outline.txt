---

PRO TIPS:

✅ DO:
- Start with a beta cohort (get feedback, testimonials)
- Make it outcome-focused, not just information
- Include worksheets and action steps
- Over-deliver on value
- Build community aspect
- Show your face (builds trust)

❌ DON'T:
- Make it too long (focus on essentials)
- Over-complicate the tech
- Launch without validation
- Forget to collect testimonials
- Ignore student questions

---

NEXT STEPS:

1. Validate idea (survey audience, pre-sell if possible)
2. Create beta version (MVP)
3. Run beta cohort (get results and testimonials)
4. Refine based on feedback
5. Launch full course
6. Build evergreen funnel
