**Primary Text:**
```

😰 "Wait, who was that person from last Tuesday?"
😓 "Did I already message them?"
😱 "They replied 5 days ago and I never responded..."

Stop losing deals because you forgot.

LinkedIn Tracker auto-tracks every message, reminds you to follow up, and shows you who's hot vs. cold.

Free for 10 leads. Try it now. 👇
```

**Headline:** Stop Losing LinkedIn Leads

**Description:** Auto-track messages. Get follow-up reminders. Free Chrome extension.

**CTA:** Learn More

**Creative:** 
- Video (15 seconds):

  - Zoom into Chrome extension
  - Clean dashboard with organized leads
  - Happy SDR closing a deal
- OR Static image:
  - Split screen: Chaos (sticky notes) vs. Organized (dashboard)

## A/B Testing Plan

### Week 1: Test Headlines

**Variant A:** "Never Lose a Lead Again"
**Variant B:** "Stop Losing LinkedIn Deals"
**Variant C:** "Track LinkedIn Messages Automatically"

**Metric:** CTR (Click-Through Rate)
**Winner:** Highest CTR

### Week 2: Test Creative

**Variant A:** Video (The Forgetful SDR)
**Variant B:** Static image (Dashboard)
**Variant C:** Carousel (Features)

**Metric:** CPC (Cost Per Click)
**Winner:** Lowest CPC

### Week 3: Test Audience

**Variant A:** Sales Reps only
**Variant B:** Recruiters only
**Variant C:** Both (broad)

**Metric:** CPA (Cost Per Acquisition - install)
**Winner:** Lowest CPA

### Week 4: Test CTA

**Variant A:** "Learn More"
**Variant B:** "Install Now"
**Variant C:** "Try It Free"

**Metric:** Conversion Rate (landing page → install)
**Winner:** Highest conversion rate
