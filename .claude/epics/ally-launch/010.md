## Technical Approach

### Documentation Platform Options
1. **Mintlify** (Recommended)
   - Beautiful out of the box
   - MDX support
   - Search built-in
   - Analytics

2. **Docusaurus**
   - React-based
   - Versioning
   - Self-hosted

3. **GitBook**
   - Easy to use
   - Good for non-technical
   - Limited customization

4. **Built-in** (Next.js + MDX)
   - Full control
   - Part of main site
   - More work

## Key Pages Content

### Getting Started
```markdown
# Quick Start

Get up and running with Ally in 2 minutes.

## 1. Create your account
Visit ally.ai and sign up with email or Google.

## 2. Complete onboarding
Tell us a bit about yourself so Ally can personalize your experience.

## 3. Start chatting
Send your first message! Try:
- "What can you help me with?"
- "Tell me a fun fact"
- "Help me brainstorm ideas for..."

## Next steps
- Explore [Skills](/docs/features/skills)
- Customize your [Settings](/docs/account/settings)
- Check out [Use Cases](/docs/examples)
```
