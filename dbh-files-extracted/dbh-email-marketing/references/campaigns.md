# Campaign Types & Templates

## Flash Sales / Promotions

### Weekend Flash Sale
**When:** Limited time (24-72 hours)
**Goal:** Drive urgency and volume

**Subject line formulas:**
- "This weekend only: [offer]"
- "[X]% off everything – ends Sunday"
- "Flash sale starts now 🔥"

**Structure:**
```
HERO: Bold offer headline
BODY: What's included, exclusions
URGENCY: Countdown or deadline
CTA: Shop the sale
REMINDER: Free shipping threshold
```

**Example:**
```
Hey {{ first_name }},

This weekend only: spend $150 and get a FREE Pure Stem Cell Moisturiser.

No code needed – it'll be added to your cart automatically.

Why are we doing this? We want more people to try this product. Once you do, we think you'll love it.

Ends Sunday midnight.

[SHOP NOW]

No secrets,
Deep Blue Health
```

### Free Gift with Purchase
**When:** Product launch, excess inventory, building loyalty
**Goal:** Increase AOV

**Subject:** "Free [product] when you spend $[X]"

## Product Launches

### New Product Announcement
**When:** Launching new SKU
**Goal:** Generate excitement and early sales

**Email sequence:**
1. **Teaser (1 week before):** "Something new is coming..."
2. **Launch (Day 0):** Full reveal with launch offer
3. **Reminder (Day 3):** Last chance for launch pricing

**Launch email structure:**
```
HERO: Product image + name
STORY: Why we made this
BENEFITS: Top 3 benefits
PROOF: Early reviews or testing results
OFFER: Launch discount or bonus
CTA: Be first to try
```

## Educational Content

### Ingredient Spotlight
**When:** Monthly or bi-weekly
**Goal:** Build trust, position as experts

**Subject formulas:**
- "What makes NZ Green Lipped Mussel different"
- "The truth about [ingredient]"
- "Why we source from [location]"

**Structure:**
```
HOOK: Surprising fact or question
EDUCATION: The science/story (keep it accessible)
PRODUCT TIE-IN: How our product delivers this
CTA: Learn more or shop
```

**Example:**
```
Hey {{ first_name }},

Ever seen "proprietary blend" on a supplement label?

It sounds scientific. But here's what it really means: they don't want to tell you how much of each ingredient is inside.

We do things differently.

Every Deep Blue Health product lists exactly what's in it – and where it comes from.

Our Green Lipped Mussel? Marlborough Sounds.
Our Deer Velvet? Wanaka Alps.
Our Bee Pollen? Nelson.

We can tell you the name of the farmers we work with.

[SEE OUR FULL RANGE]

No secrets,
Deep Blue Health
```

## VIP / Loyalty Campaigns

### Early Access
**Who:** VIP segment (top 10% by spend or engagement)
**What:** First access to sales, new products

**Subject:** "VIP early access: [offer] starts now"

### Exclusive Offers
**Who:** Loyal customers
**What:** Offers not available to general list

## Re-engagement Campaigns

### We Miss You
**Who:** Inactive subscribers (90+ days no open)
**Goal:** Re-engage or clean list

**Subject formulas:**
- "Is this goodbye, {{ first_name }}?"
- "We haven't heard from you in a while"
- "Should we stop emailing you?"

**Structure:**
```
ACKNOWLEDGE: We noticed you haven't opened our emails
VALUE PROP: Here's what you're missing
OFFER: Incentive to come back
OPTIONS: Update preferences or unsubscribe
```

## Campaign Checklist

Before sending:
- [ ] Subject line A/B test set up
- [ ] Preview text written (doesn't repeat subject)
- [ ] Mobile preview checked
- [ ] Links tested
- [ ] Unsubscribe link present
- [ ] Segment correctly selected
- [ ] Exclusions set (recent purchasers, etc.)
- [ ] Send time optimised (typically 10am local)
- [ ] UTM parameters added to links
