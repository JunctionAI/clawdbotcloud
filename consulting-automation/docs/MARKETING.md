## 📧 Cold Outreach (Optional)

### **Email Outreach** (50-100/month)

**Target:** Small agencies, consultants, ecommerce brands

**Template:**
```
Subject: Quick question about [Company]'s automation

Hi [Name],

I noticed [Company] is [specific observation - e.g., "growing fast" / "launching new products"].

I help businesses like yours automate workflows with AI. One of my clients (similar size to you) saved 15+ hours/week by automating [specific task].

Would it make sense to chat for 15 minutes about what this could look like for [Company]?

Best,
Tom
```

**Tools:**
- Hunter.io (find emails)
- Instantly.ai or Lemlist (outreach automation)
- Response rate: 5-10%
- Conversion: 20-30% of responses

### **LinkedIn Outreach** (30-50/month)

**Strategy:**
1. Find target profiles (use Sales Navigator if budget allows)
2. Send connection request with note:
   ```
   Hi [Name], I help [role] automate [task] with AI. 
   I'd love to connect and share ideas.
   ```
3. After acceptance (wait 2-3 days), send message:
   ```
   Thanks for connecting! I work with [similar companies] 
   to automate [specific workflow]. Would you be open to 
   a quick 15-min chat about how this could work for [Company]?
   ```

**Conversion:** 5-10% of connections → calls

## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Lead Response Time:** Reply within 1 hour = 7x higher conversion
2. **Follow-Up:** 80% of sales happen after 5+ follow-ups
3. **Social Proof:** Add testimonials to every touchpoint
4. **Scarcity:** "Only taking 2 new clients this month" (creates urgency)
5. **Value First:** Give free value (audit, consultation) before asking for sale
6. **Niching:** Start with Clawdbot (less competition) → expand later
7. **Automation:** Use the backend system to track everything (no manual spreadsheets)
8. **Retargeting:** 95% don't convert first visit - retarget them!

## 📞 Sales Process

### **1. Lead Capture** (Landing page form)
- Name, email, company, budget, urgency
- Auto-scored (70+ = immediate alert)

### **2. Immediate Response** (Auto-email)
- Thank you + what to expect
- Link to book consultation
- Case study or testimonial

### **3. Consultation Call** (30 min)
- Understand needs
- Ask discovery questions
- Provide rough estimate
- Book follow-up if needed

### **4. Proposal** (Within 24 hours)
- Custom pricing
- Scope of work
- Timeline
- Payment terms
- CTA: Accept & pay deposit

### **5. Follow-Up** (If no response)
- Day 3: Gentle check-in
- Day 7: Address objections
- Day 14: Final nudge (expires soon)

### **6. Close** (Accept proposal)
- Auto-trigger onboarding sequence
- Send payment link
- Schedule kickoff call

**Average time to close:** 5-10 days
