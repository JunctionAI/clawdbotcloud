**Pros:**

- Compound growth of AUM over time
- Build brand loyalty early
- Future estate planning clients

**Cons:**
- Low AUM today ($10k-$50k per client)
- Takes 20+ years to mature
- High acquisition cost vs. immediate return
- Competitive (many funds chase young investors)

### **Tom's Insight: "Win the will signature battle"**

**Pros:**
- Immediate large AUM ($500k-$5M per client)
- Locks in decades of fees

- Defensible moat (once executor, hard to replace)

**Cons:**
- Death-dependent (timing uncertain)
- Older demographic (less "sexy" marketing)
- Slower growth (fewer wealthy people than young people)
- Referral-dependent (trust-based sales)
