Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

What's the difference between SimpleClaw and Premium?

SimpleClaw is the open-source DIY version—perfect if you're technical and enjoy tinkering. Premium Setup includes a 1-hour personalized onboarding where we configure everything specifically for your workflow, connect all your integrations, and get you productive immediately. Think of it as buying a custom-built PC vs building one yourself.

Does this run on my infrastructure or yours?

Clawdbot runs on YOUR infrastructure—whether that's your laptop, a home server, or your cloud account. Your data never touches our servers. This gives you complete control and privacy. We just help you set it up and configure it perfectly.

How long does setup take?

SimpleClaw can be running in minutes, but configuring all integrations and skills takes most people 4-8 hours. With Premium Setup, we do a 1-hour guided session and you're productive immediately. We handle the complex stuff so you don't have to.

Are there any ongoing costs?

The Premium Setup is a one-time fee. You'll need your own API keys (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.) which typically cost $20-50/month depending on usage. We help you optimize this during onboarding. No hidden subscriptions.

How secure is my data?

Extremely secure. Because Clawdbot runs on your infrastructure, your data never leaves your control. It's self-hosted, open-source, and you can audit every line of code. No cloud dependencies for your sensitive information.

Can I connect my phone?

Yes! Premium Setup includes mobile node configuration for iOS and Android. This lets Clawdbot send you notifications, take photos on command, access your location, and more—all with your explicit permission.

What if I'm not satisfied?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on Premium Setup. If you're not seeing the productivity gains we promised, we'll refund you in full—no questions asked.

Can I use this for my team?

Absolutely! Check out our Enterprise tier. We can deploy Clawdbot for multiple users, build custom skills for your business processes, and provide ongoing support. Book a consultation to discuss your needs.
