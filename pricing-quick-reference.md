## 💰 Why These Prices?

### $29 Starter ✓
- **Psychology:** Under $30 = impulse-friendly
- **Competition:** Matches Zapier Pro ($19.99)
- **Purpose:** Qualification tier (filters serious users)

### $99 Professional ★ (Change from $79)
- **Psychology:** Magic B2B SaaS price point
- **Annual:** $990 ≈ $1k mental model (easy budget approval)
- **Target:** 60-70% of customers (volume tier)
- **Why not $79?** Awkward number, leaves money on table

### $299 Business ★ (Change from $199)
- **Psychology:** 3x Professional (premium positioning)
- **Annual:** ~$3k (requires manager approval = qualified buyers)
- **Purpose:** Scale tier (unlimited users, priority support)
- **Headroom:** Leaves space for Enterprise pricing

### Custom Enterprise ★ (New)
- **Starting point:** $999-$1,999/mo minimum
- **Psychology:** Anchor effect (makes $299 look reasonable)
- **Purpose:** Capture large customers + custom deals
- **Features:** SSO, SAML, SLA, account manager, audit logs

## 📈 Annual Billing Strategy

### Discount: **17% (2 Months Free)**

**Why 17% not 33%?**
- 33% = great for acquisition, but sacrifices revenue
- 17% = balances incentive + revenue
- "Save 2 months" messaging clearer than "%"
- Alternative: 25% first year only (land-and-expand)

```

- Year 1 revenue: $1,108,800 (7% lower)
- Year 2 revenue: $887,040 (24% higher!)

- LTV improvement: +67%
```

## ⚠️ Risk Mitigation

### Risk: Existing customer backlash
**Solution:** Grandfather at current price forever
```
Email: "You're locked in at $79 forever 🎉"
New customers pay $99, you keep $79.
```

### Risk: Lower conversion at $99
**Solution:** 
- Extend trial: 14 → 30 days
- Add value: New features to Professional
- Money-back guarantee: 30 days

**Monitor:** If conversion drops >15%, test $89

### Risk: No one picks annual
**Solution:**
- Launch promo: First 100 get 25% off
- Test messaging: "2 months free" vs "Save $198"
- Add perks: Early access for annual customers

**Monitor:** If <20% pick annual after 2mo, increase to 25%

### Risk: Enterprise tier doesn't convert
**Solution:**
- Set low bar: $999/mo minimum
- Productize: Clear features (SSO, SAML, SLA)
- Dedicated sales: Founder calls for >$10k deals

**Monitor:** If no traction in 6mo, remove tier

## ✅ Decision Matrix

### Should I add a free tier?
- **Yes if:** Need viral growth, low CAC, high volume strategy
- **No if:** High-touch sales, low churn on $29 tier
- **Test:** Make Starter free for 3 months, measure activation

### Should I switch to per-user pricing?
- **Yes if:** Product value scales with team size (collaboration tools)
- **No if:** Product is individual-focused or infrastructure
- **Test:** Offer per-user option alongside flat pricing

### Should I do usage-based pricing?
- **Yes if:** Clear usage metric (API calls, tasks, storage)
- **No if:** Hard to predict costs, usage unpredictable
- **Start with:** Hybrid (base + overages)

### Annual discount: 17% vs 25% vs 33%?
- **17%:** Balanced revenue + incentive ✓ (Recommended)
- **25%:** Aggressive annual adoption (if need cash now)
- **33%:** Very aggressive (Zapier model, high volume)
