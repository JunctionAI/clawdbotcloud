## Color Scheme
- **Primary:** Purple gradient (#667eea → #764ba2)
- **CTA Buttons:** Purple (#667eea) with hover effect
- **Background:** Light gray (#f8f9fa)
- **Cards:** White with subtle shadows
- **Accent:** Yellow for guarantee box

## Typography
- **Headlines:** Bold, 2.5em → 1.8em (mobile)
- **Body:** 1.1em, easy to read
- **Font:** System fonts (fast loading)

## Mobile Responsiveness
- ✅ Stacks vertically on small screens
- ✅ Touch-friendly button sizes
- ✅ Readable text (no zooming needed)
- ✅ Fast loading (<1 second)

## Key Conversions Points
1. **Top hero CTA** - First impression
2. **Mid-page CTA** - After value proposition
3. **Calendly embed** - Low-friction booking
4. **Final CTA** - Last chance to convert

## Page Performance
- **File size:** ~10KB (tiny!)
- **Load time:** <1 second
- **Mobile score:** 95+ (fast)
- **No external dependencies** (except Stripe/Calendly)
