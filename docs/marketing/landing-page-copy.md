## 5. FAQ Section

### Section Headline
**Questions? Handled.**

---

**How is Ally different from ChatGPT?**

ChatGPT is a chatbot—it answers questions and generates text. Ally is an assistant—it takes action. When you say "book me a haircut," ChatGPT gives you a list of salons. Ally calls, books, and adds it to your calendar.

---

**What can Ally actually do?**

Right now: Send messages, manage calendars, set reminders, make calls, do research, draft emails, track tasks, and remember everything about your life. We're adding new capabilities every week—bookings, purchases, and more coming soon.

---

**Which platforms does Ally support?**

Discord, WhatsApp, and SMS today. Slack, Telegram, and a native app are coming soon. Ally goes where you go.

---

**Is my data safe?**

Yes. We don't sell your data or use it to train AI models. Your memory is encrypted and you can delete it anytime. We're building trust, not ad profiles.

---

**Can Ally access my accounts?**

Only if you connect them. Ally can integrate with Google Calendar, email, and more—but only with your explicit permission. You control what Ally can see and do.

---

**What if Ally gets something wrong?**

Tell it! Ally learns from corrections. Say "Actually, I meant..." and it adjusts. The more you use it, the fewer mistakes it makes.

---

**Is there a limit on messages?**

Free tier: 50/month. All paid plans: unlimited. Talk to Ally as much as you want.

---

**Can my kids use it?**

Yes! Family plan includes kid-safe mode with content filters and parental controls. Great for homework help, scheduling, and building good delegation habits.

## Implementation Notes

### Tone Guidelines
- Confident but not arrogant
- Warm but efficient
- Simple language, no jargon
- Active voice, short sentences
- "You" focused, not "we" focused

### Visual Suggestions
- Clean, minimal design
- Conversation UI mockups showing Ally in action
- Before/after comparisons (ChatGPT response vs Ally response)
- Platform icons (Discord, WhatsApp, SMS) prominent
- People-focused imagery (not robots)

### A/B Test Candidates
- Hero headline: "The AI that actually does things" vs "Your life, handled by AI"
- CTA: "Get Ally Free" vs "Start Free" vs "Try Ally"
- Pricing emphasis: Lead with Free vs Lead with $9 Personal
