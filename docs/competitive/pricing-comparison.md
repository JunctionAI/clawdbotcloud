## Price Point Analysis

### The $20/mo Sweet Spot

Both ChatGPT Plus and Claude Pro have converged on $20/mo:
- **Established anchor price** for premium AI
- **Consumer-friendly** but still meaningful revenue
- **5x free tier** value typically

### The $200/mo Premium Tier

ChatGPT Pro at $200/mo represents:
- **10x Plus pricing** for "unlimited"
- **Power user capture** strategy
- **Anchor that makes $20 look cheap**

### The Enterprise Gap

$20/mo → Enterprise ($500+/mo per seat)
- **Massive gap** in pricing
- **Team features** locked behind enterprise
- **Opportunity** for $50-100/mo team tier

### The Abandoned Middle

Marketing AI (Jasper, Copy.ai):
- **$69-1,000/mo minimum** paid tiers
- **Prosumer market abandoned**
- **Huge opportunity** in $20-100 range

## Pricing Model Comparison

### Per-User (Seat-Based)
**Who uses it:** Notion AI, ChatGPT Business, Jasper Business

| Pros | Cons |
|------|------|
| Predictable revenue | Penalizes team growth |
| Scales with adoption | Creates seat-counting friction |
| Enterprise-standard | Discourages collaboration |

### Flat Rate
**Who uses it:** ChatGPT Plus/Pro, Claude Pro

| Pros | Cons |
|------|------|
| Simple to understand | Doesn't scale with teams |
| No counting friction | May undermonetize power users |
| Consumer-friendly | Hard to grow ARPU |

### Usage-Based (Credits)
**Who uses it:** Copy.ai, Jasper

| Pros | Cons |
|------|------|
| Pay for what you use | Unpredictable bills |
| Aligns cost with value | Anxiety about usage |
| Captures power users | Complex to communicate |

### Hybrid Models
**Who uses it:** ChatGPT (tiers + limits)

| Pros | Cons |
|------|------|
| Captures all segments | Confusing |
| Flexible monetization | Hard to explain |
| Multiple upsell paths | Customer frustration |

- Multiple upgrade paths ($8, $20, $200)

## Pricing Opportunity Analysis

### Underserved Price Points

```
$0 ─────── $20 ─────── $50 ─────── $100 ─────── $200+
    ↑           ↑           ↑            ↑
    │           │           │            │
    Free        Plus        Team?        Power
    tier        tier        (EMPTY)      user

THE GAP: $20 - $100 for team features
```

### Competitive Positioning Options

#### Option 1: Undercut Plus
- **Price:** $10-15/mo
- **Strategy:** Same features, lower price
- **Risk:** Race to bottom, perceived as inferior

#### Option 2: Match Plus
- **Price:** $20/mo
- **Strategy:** Same price, differentiate on features/experience
- **Risk:** Head-to-head competition with giants

#### Option 3: Fill the Team Gap
- **Price:** $30-50/mo for teams
- **Strategy:** Features between Plus and Enterprise
- **Risk:** Complex to position

#### Option 4: Usage-Based
- **Price:** Pay-per-use
- **Strategy:** Align with actual value delivered
- **Risk:** Unpredictable, harder to communicate

## Recommended Pricing Strategy

### Simple Three-Tier Model

| Tier | Price | Target | Key Value |
|------|-------|--------|-----------|
| **Free** | $0 | Trial/Light users | Generous but limited |
| **Pro** | $15-20/mo | Individuals | Full features |
| **Team** | $10/seat/mo | Small teams | Collaboration + admin |

### Why This Works:

1. **Free tier is generous**
   - Actually useful (unlike Copy.ai)
   - Clear limits (unlike Jasper trial)
   - Conversion driver

2. **Pro is competitive**
   - At or below ChatGPT Plus
   - No confusing sub-tiers
   - All features included

3. **Team fills the gap**
   - Self-serve (no sales call)
   - Affordable (not $500/seat)
   - Growing with customer

### Pricing Psychology

| Principle | Implementation |
|-----------|----------------|
| Anchor high | Show Team price first |
| Round numbers | $15, $20, $10 |
| No hidden costs | All features included |
| Annual discount | 20% off for yearly |
| No per-message limits | Generous soft limits |

## What to Avoid

### Pricing Mistakes Competitors Make

| Mistake | Who Does It | Why It's Bad |
|---------|-------------|--------------|
| Too many tiers | ChatGPT (6 tiers) | Confusion |
| No free tier | Jasper | Barrier to entry |
| Enterprise-only | Copy.ai | Excludes most users |
| Credit systems | Copy.ai, Jasper | Anxiety |
| Per-seat small teams | Notion | Penalizes growth |
| Vague limits | Everyone | Trust erosion |

### What We Should Do

- **Transparent limits** - Tell users exactly what they get
- **Soft limits** - Slow down, don't hard-stop
- **Simple tiers** - 3 max, clearly differentiated
- **Self-serve** - No sales calls for standard plans
- **Monthly option** - No annual lock-in required
