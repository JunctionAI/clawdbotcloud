# Jasper Competitive Teardown

**Company:** Jasper AI  
**Product:** Jasper Marketing Platform  
**URL:** jasper.ai  
**Market Position:** Enterprise marketing AI, content automation

---

## Pricing Structure (Feb 2026)

Jasper has moved aggressively upmarket:

| Tier | Price | Target |
|------|-------|--------|
| **Trial** | 7 days free | Individuals |
| **Pro** | $59-69/mo | Individuals, freelancers |
| **Business** | Custom (est. $500+/mo) | Marketing teams |

### Pricing Analysis

**Major shift observed:**
- **Abandoned prosumer market** - No affordable team tier
- **Pro is limited** - Only 2 brand voices, 5 knowledge assets
- **Business is enterprise-only** - Custom pricing, sales call required
- **No free tier** - 7-day trial only

**Implications:**
- Jasper is betting on enterprise
- SMBs and individuals are underserved
- Huge gap between $69/mo and "custom pricing"

## Onboarding Flow

### Sign-up Process

1. **Landing:** jasper.ai - marketing-heavy, feature-focused
2. **Trial signup:** Email, company info required
3. **Use case selection:** Marketing role, team size
4. **Workspace setup:** Brand voice, knowledge upload
5. **Guided tour:** App walkthrough with templates

### Friction Points Identified

| Friction | Severity | Notes |
|----------|----------|-------|
| Company info required | HIGH | Feels enterprise-y, not individual |
| Complex setup | HIGH | Brand voice, knowledge before using |
| Marketing focus | MEDIUM | Excludes non-marketing use cases |
| 7-day limit | HIGH | Not enough time for real evaluation |

### Time to Value

- **Sign-up to first output:** 5-10 minutes (setup heavy)
- **Full setup with brand voice:** 30+ minutes
- **Learning curve:** Days for full platform

## Upgrade Prompts & Conversion Tactics

### When Upgrade Prompts Appear

1. **Trial expiration:** "Your trial ends in X days"
2. **Brand voice limit:** "Upgrade to Business for unlimited voices"
3. **Knowledge limit:** "Add more knowledge assets with Business"
4. **Feature access:** "This feature requires Business plan"
5. **Seats:** "Add team members with Business"

### Upgrade Copy Examples

```
"Your 7-day trial is ending"
Don't lose access to Jasper. Upgrade now to keep creating.

Pro: $59/mo - For individuals
Business: Talk to sales - For teams

[Upgrade to Pro] [Talk to Sales]
```

### Conversion Psychology

**Tactics used:**
- **Enterprise playbook:** Sales calls, custom pricing
- **Fear of loss:** "Don't lose your work"
- **Team pressure:** Can't add teammates on Pro
- **Feature gating:** Best features behind Business

## Weaknesses & Vulnerabilities

### 1. Pricing Cliff
- $69/mo to "talk to sales" is massive gap
- No self-serve team tier
- Alienates SMBs and growing teams

### 2. Marketing-Only Focus
- Can't use for general purpose
- Developers, support teams excluded
- Limited use case flexibility

### 3. Complex Setup
- Brand voice, knowledge assets required
- Not plug-and-play
- Enterprise onboarding mentality

### 4. Enterprise-Only Features
- Best features (Studio, Agents, API) locked
- Pro tier feels crippled
- Forces sales conversation

### 5. Slow Innovation
- Feature set similar to 2023
- ChatGPT has caught up on marketing features
- No unique moat

5. **Free tier** - They have none

### Target Their Weaknesses:

| Their Weakness | Our Counter |
|----------------|-------------|
| Marketing-only | General purpose |
| $69/mo minimum | Free + affordable paid |
| Complex setup | Instant start |
| Enterprise-focused | Individual-friendly |

### Who to Target:

- **Jasper trial churns:** People who can't afford $69/mo

- **Freelancers:** Pro features at accessible prices
