# Notion AI Competitive Teardown

**Company:** Notion Labs  
**Product:** Notion AI  
**URL:** notion.so/product/ai  
**Market Position:** Workspace-integrated AI, productivity focus

---

## Pricing Structure (Feb 2026)

Notion AI is **bundled with Notion plans**, not sold separately:

| Plan | Price | AI Access |
|------|-------|-----------|
| **Free** | $0 | Limited trial (then blocked) |
| **Plus** | $12/mo | Limited trial (then blocked) |
| **Business** | $18/mo/user | **Full AI included** |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | Full AI + zero data retention |

### Pricing Analysis

**Key insight:** Notion AI used to be $10/mo add-on. Now it's included in Business+ only.

**This means:**
- Free/Plus users get a taste, then must jump to $18/mo
- No way to get AI without full Business upgrade
- Forces workspace-wide upgrade decision

**Observations:**
- **Bundling strategy** reduces AI as line item
- **Trial-to-paywall** creates urgency
- **No standalone AI product** - must use Notion
- **30-day data retention** except Enterprise (security concern)

## Onboarding Flow

### Sign-up Process

1. **Landing:** notion.so - workspace signup, not AI-specific
2. **Auth Options:** Google, Apple, Email, SSO
3. **Workspace creation:** Required before any usage
4. **Template selection:** Onboarding includes AI templates
5. **AI discovery:** Prompted to try AI features

### Friction Points Identified

| Friction | Severity | Notes |
|----------|----------|-------|
| Must create workspace | HIGH | Can't just chat, need Notion |
| Learning curve | HIGH | Notion itself is complex |
| AI is secondary | MEDIUM | Buried in workspace context |
| Trial limits unclear | HIGH | Users don't know when they'll hit wall |

### Time to Value

- **Sign-up to first AI use:** 3-5 minutes (slower due to setup)
- **Learning Notion itself:** Hours to days
- **AI is NOT the primary product** - it's an enhancement

## Upgrade Prompts & Conversion Tactics

### When Upgrade Prompts Appear

1. **AI trial exhausted:** "Upgrade to Business to continue using Notion AI"
2. **AI feature attempt:** "This AI feature requires Business plan"
3. **Meeting notes:** "AI Meeting Notes require Business or Enterprise"
4. **Research mode:** "Research mode requires Business plan"

### Upgrade Copy Examples

```
"Your AI trial has ended"
Upgrade to Notion Business to unlock:
• Unlimited AI features
• Meeting notes with transcription
• Research mode for detailed docs

$18/month per member
[Upgrade Workspace]
```

### Conversion Psychology

**Tactics used:**
- **Trial addiction:** Let users fall in love, then cut off
- **Workspace lock-in:** Data is already in Notion
- **Per-member pricing:** Expensive for teams
- **Feature breadth:** AI touches many workflows

## Weaknesses & Vulnerabilities

### 1. Platform Lock-in Required
- Must use Notion to use Notion AI
- Can't use AI independently
- Switching cost is all your data

### 2. Pricing Barrier
- $18/mo/user is expensive for teams
- No AI-only option for Plus users
- Enterprise pricing opaque

### 3. Limited AI Scope
- Great for docs, bad for everything else
- No creative generation (images, video)
- No voice interface
- No mobile-first AI experience

### 4. Learning Curve
- Notion itself is complex
- AI features scattered through UI
- Not intuitive for new users

### 5. Data Concerns
- 30-day retention with LLM providers
- Only Enterprise gets zero retention
- Trust issues for sensitive data

---

## What They Do Well

### 1. Contextual AI
- AI knows your workspace
- Can reference specific docs
- Connected to your actual work

### 2. Workflow Integration
- AI built into existing workflows
- Database autofill is powerful
- Meeting notes are genuinely useful

### 3. Multi-Model Approach
- Uses GPT-4.1, Claude 4, others
- "Best model for the job" philosophy
- Reduced single-vendor risk

### 4. Enterprise Features
- Zero data retention option
- SSO, SCIM, audit logs
- Compliance focus (SOC 2, GDPR)

## Opportunities to Exploit

### Beat Them On:

1. **Standalone product** - AI that works anywhere
2. **Simplicity** - No complex workspace required
3. **Price** - $18/user is expensive for AI
4. **Scope** - Images, voice, code - not just docs
5. **Privacy** - Zero retention by default

### Target Their Weaknesses:

| Their Weakness | Our Counter |
|----------------|-------------|
| Notion-only | Works everywhere |
| Complex setup | Instant value |
| Expensive per-seat | Affordable pricing |
| Docs-focused | Multi-purpose AI |
| 30-day retention | Privacy-first |

### Who to Target:

- **Notion users hitting limits:** Offer complementary AI
- **Non-Notion users:** We work without Notion
- **Price-sensitive teams:** Cheaper than $18/user
- **Privacy-conscious:** Better data practices
