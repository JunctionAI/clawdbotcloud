**Goal:** Get payment link live in 5 minutes.

## 🚀 Step 1: Sign Up (1 minute)

1. Open browser: https://nowpayments.io/
2. Click **"Get Started"** (top right)
3. Enter your email
4. Create password
5. Verify email (check inbox)
6. **DONE** - You're in the dashboard

---

## 💰 Step 2: Create Payment Link (2 minutes)

1. In dashboard, click **"Payment Links"** (left sidebar)
2. Click **"Create Payment Link"**
3. Fill in:
   - **Title:** "AI Quick Win Audit"
   - **Price:** 97
   - **Currency:** USD
   - **Description:** "Personalized AI implementation audit - 48hr delivery"
   - **Success URL:** https://yourdomain.com/booking (or leave blank for now)
4. Click **"Create Link"**
5. **COPY THE LINK** - looks like: `https://nowpayments.io/payment/?iid=5678901234`

---

## ✅ Step 3: Test (1 minute)

1. Open the payment link in new tab
2. You should see:
   - Product name: "AI Quick Win Audit"
   - Price: $97.00
   - Multiple crypto options (BTC, ETH, USDT, etc.)
3. **DON'T PAY YET** - just verify it loads correctly

## 📧 Step 4: Set Up Notifications (1 minute)

1. Back in NOWPayments dashboard
2. Go to **Settings** → **Notifications**
3. Add your email for payment confirmations
4. Toggle ON: "Email me when payment received"
5. **SAVE**

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

**Link doesn't work?**
- Make sure you verified your email
- Check the link copied correctly
- Try creating a new payment link

**Not receiving notifications?**
- Check spam folder
- Verify email in Settings → Notifications
- Test with $1 payment

**Customer confused about crypto?**
- NOWPayments has built-in help
- Shows QR code + instructions
- Supports 200+ cryptocurrencies
