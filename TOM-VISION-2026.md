## THE TWO-COMPANY STRUCTURE

### 1. ALTERNATE (The Brand)
A creative brand/business operating across three pillars:

#### Pillar 1 — Video Production & Films
- AI-generated films using cutting-edge tools (Higgsfield, Seedance, etc.)
- Creative competitions (e.g. Higgsfield gorilla project = first major output)
- The attention engine: films get eyes, eyes become community
- Long-term: become a recognised creative production house powered by AI

#### Pillar 2 — Events & Community
- Bring people together around AI creativity
- Excite creative minds about what's possible with next-gen AI tools
- Build a loyal community of creators, fans, and collaborators
- Events (IRL + online) that fuel the brand and generate content

#### Pillar 3 — Products & Merchandise
- Physical merch derived from items and characters in the films
- Real eCom: real products, real sales, real revenue
- Shopify + Printful as the engine
- The films → the merch: seamless creative-to-commerce pipeline

**Business entity:** eCom store at alternate.ai or alternate.io
**Partner:** Zach (50/50 — TBC, formalisation needed)

### 2. JUNCTION MEDIA (The Engine)
Junction is NOT a standalone agency hunting $10k/month clients.

Junction = the AI service delivery engine that powers:
- **ALTERNATE** (primary client, first test case, live proof of concept)
- **DBH** (base income client — ~$4k/month, stable)
- **PG Investments** (base income client)

**The genius of this model:**
- Building ALTERNATE = Junction's marketing
- Every AI system we build for ALTERNATE = a case study
- Every result we get = portfolio proof
- The "Build in Public" content about ALTERNATE IS the Junction content strategy

**No cold outreach. No chasing clients. Results speak. ALTERNATE is the ad.**
