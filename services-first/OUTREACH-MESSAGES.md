## Pricing in Outreach (When to Mention)

### Don't mention pricing in initial message:
- Let curiosity drive the call booking
- Pricing makes sense after understanding their workflows

### Mention pricing in follow-ups if they haven't replied:
- Transparency can overcome "this is going to be expensive" objection
- Show ROI calculation to justify cost

### Always mention pricing on the discovery call:
- Don't waste anyone's time
- Be transparent about investment

## Best Practices

### Do:
- ✅ Personalize first line (reference their work)
- ✅ Focus on outcomes (time saved, money made)
- ✅ Include specific examples (social proof)
- ✅ Make it easy to book a call (Calendly link)
- ✅ Follow up 2-3 times (most conversions happen in follow-ups)

### Don't:
- ❌ Generic copy-paste messages
- ❌ Talk about features (talk about outcomes)
- ❌ Be pushy or salesy
- ❌ Over-explain in the message (save for call)
- ❌ Give up after one message (persistence pays)

## Tracking & Optimization

**Track these metrics:**
- Messages sent (per week)
- Reply rate (% who respond)
- Call booking rate (% who book after replying)
- Close rate (% who buy after call)
- Overall conversion (% of initial messages → customers)

**Optimize based on data:**
- If reply rate <10%: Test new subject lines/messaging
- If booking rate <30%: Make CTA clearer
- If close rate <30%: Improve discovery call process
- If overall <3%: Revisit target audience
