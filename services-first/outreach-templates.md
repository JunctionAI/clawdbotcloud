## Key Messaging Elements (Use in All Outreach)

### 1. Outcome, Not Features
❌ "Our AI can integrate with 50+ tools"
✅ "Get 10 hours back every week"

### 2. Service, Not Product
❌ "Sign up for our platform"
✅ "We'll build your AI team for you"

### 3. Social Proof
Always include a client result:
- "One of our clients saved 12 hours/week"
- "Founder went from 3 hours/day on admin to 30 minutes"

### 4. Low-Friction CTA
❌ "Let's schedule a demo"
✅ "Worth a quick 15-minute call?"

### 5. Specificity
❌ "We automate workflows"
✅ "We handle email triage, calendar scheduling, and CRM data entry"

---

## Tone Guidelines

- **Conversational, not corporate:** Write like you're talking to a friend
- **Confident, not arrogant:** "We build AI teams" not "We're the best AI company"
- **Results-focused:** Lead with outcomes, not tech
- **Human:** Use contractions, short sentences, personality
