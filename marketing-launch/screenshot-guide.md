## Design Guidelines

**Colors:**
- Primary: #7C3AED (purple – AI theme)
- Secondary: #10B981 (green – active/healthy)
- Accent: #F59E0B (orange – notifications)
- Background: #1F2937 (dark) or #F9FAFB (light)
- Text: #E5E7EB (dark mode) or #1F2937 (light mode)

**Fonts:**
- Headings: Inter Bold
- Body: Inter Regular
- Code: JetBrains Mono

**Style:**
- Clean, modern interface
- Subtle shadows for depth
- Round corners (8px)
- Consistent padding (16px/24px)

**Overlays:**
- Semi-transparent background
- White text with shadow for readability
- Position: bottom-left or top-right
- Fade-in animations for video

## Screenshot Workflow

1. **Setup clean environment:**
   - Clear desktop
   - Organized terminal/editor
   - Good color scheme (dark mode preferred)

2. **Capture at 2x resolution:**
   - 3840x2160 for 1920x1080 final
   - Downscale for sharpness

3. **Add overlays/annotations:**
   - Use Figma or Photoshop
   - Keep text minimal and impactful
   - Ensure mobile readability

4. **Export optimized:**
   - PNG for quality (screenshots)
   - JPG for file size (photos)
   - 80-90% quality for JPG

5. **Test on devices:**
   - Check mobile preview
   - Verify text is readable
   - Ensure key info is visible
