## First Comment

Hey Product Hunt! 👋

I'm Tom, and I built Clawdbot because I was frustrated with AI assistants that forget everything between sessions.

**The Problem:**
Every conversation with ChatGPT starts from zero. You explain your setup, your preferences, your projects... again and again. It's like Groundhog Day.

**What Makes Clawdbot Different:**

🧠 **Real Memory System**
- Persistent knowledge across all conversations
- Remembers your preferences, people, projects
- Daily logs + curated long-term memory
- Entity files for important people/projects

💓 **Proactive Heartbeats**
- Checks your email, calendar, social mentions
- Reaches out when something needs attention
- Does background work during idle time
- Like having an assistant who actually pays attention

🛠️ **Skills That Actually Work**
- Controls your browser, takes screenshots
- Reads/sends email, manages calendar
- SSH into servers, run commands
- Camera access, screen recording on paired devices
- Voice/TTS for storytelling
- And more via modular skill system

🤖 **Smart Context Management**
- Spawns subagents for complex tasks
- Keeps main context clean and focused
- Works in parallel, not sequential
- Self-improving through documented learnings

**Built for Power Users:**
This isn't a dumbed-down chat interface. It's a full-featured agent that can:
- Debug code and prove fixes work
- Manage your infrastructure
- Handle your communications
- Remember what matters

**Open Development:**
I'm building this in public. The memory system, skills, and agent coordination are all designed to be extensible and hackable.

Would love your feedback on what skills you'd want your AI to have!
